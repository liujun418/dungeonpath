## 1. Introduction

DungeonPath ("we", "our", or "us") operates dungeonpath.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

## 2. Information We Collect

### Information Automatically Collected

When you visit our site, certain information is collected automatically:

- **Log data** — IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, pages visited, and timestamps
- **Cookies and tracking technologies** — We and our third-party partners use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to collect browsing data

### Information You Voluntarily Provide

- **Contact information** — If you reach out to us via email, we collect your email address and any information you include in your message

## 3. Third-Party Advertising

We use **Google AdSense** to display advertisements on our website. Google and its partners may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other websites on the internet.

- Google uses the **DART cookie** to serve ads based on your browsing history
- You may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads)
- Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the internet

You can manage your ad personalization settings by visiting [Google Ads Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads) or by opting out through the [Network Advertising Initiative](https://www.networkadvertising.org/choices/).

## 4. Analytics

We use **Google Analytics** to understand how visitors interact with our website. Google Analytics collects information such as how often users visit the site, what pages they visit, and what other sites they used prior to visiting. The information is used to improve our site and services.

## 5. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

- Operate and maintain the website
- Improve user experience and content
- Display relevant advertisements
- Analyze site usage and traffic patterns
- Respond to your inquiries

## 6. Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregated data with partners for analytical purposes.

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at jzerov@live.com.
