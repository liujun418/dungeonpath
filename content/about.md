## What Is DungeonPath?

**DungeonPath** is an independent online publication providing free, detailed game guides and walkthroughs. We cover **Roblox games, mobile RPGs, and PC survival and co-op titles** — writing guides that help players progress faster, make better decisions, and enjoy their games more.

Every guide on the Site is freely accessible. There are **no paywalls, no subscriptions, and no account requirements**. We support the Site through advertising.

## Service Limitations

To be transparent about what you should and should not expect:

| We Provide | We Do NOT Provide |
|------------|-------------------|
| Written guides and data tables | Video content or streaming |
| Strategy and optimization advice | Guaranteed outcomes or "win rate" promises |
| Information based on research and gameplay | Real-time in-game support or coaching |
| Updated content reflecting major patches | Minute-by-minute accuracy for rapidly changing games |
| Content covering 12+ games | Coverage of every game in existence |

## Our Approach to Quality

We are committed to producing content that is **accurate, useful, and trustworthy**:

1. **Research-driven** — Guides are compiled from official game documentation, community wikis, player discussions, and hands-on gameplay verification
2. **Human-reviewed** — Content is reviewed for factual accuracy before publication
3. **Transparent dating** — Every article displays the date it was last updated so readers know how current the information is
4. **No AI-only content** — While we may use tools to assist with research and drafting, all content undergoes human review and fact-checking

We do **not** publish content that makes exaggerated claims, guarantees specific results, or uses clickbait tactics. If information in a guide is found to be inaccurate, we correct it promptly.

## Business Model and Advertising

DungeonPath is a **free, ad-supported publication**. We do not charge readers for access. Our operating costs (hosting, domain, content production) are funded through:

- **Google AdSense** advertisements displayed on the Site

Our advertising practices are fully disclosed in our [Ad Disclosure](/ad-disclosure/). The presence of advertisements does not influence the content, opinions, or recommendations expressed in our guides. Editorial content and advertising are kept strictly separate.
