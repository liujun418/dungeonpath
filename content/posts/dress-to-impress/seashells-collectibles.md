## DTI Collectibles Overview

Dress To Impress features hidden collectibles in the lobby that reward exclusive items. The two main collectible types are **Seashells** and **Treasure Chests**.

---

## Seashell Collectibles

### How Seashells Work

Seashells are hidden throughout the main lobby. Collect all 3 and bring them to a **secret room** (which appears after a round ends) to unlock the exclusive **Seashell Crown** — a rare headpiece that signals your dedication to exploration.

### All 3 Seashell Locations

| Seashell | Location | How to Find It |
|----------|----------|---------------|
| **#1** | Near the entrance/spawn area | Look on the ground near where you first appear in the lobby. It's partially hidden by furniture. |
| **#2** | Behind/near the dressing room area | Walk behind the dressing room section. The seashell is tucked against the wall. |
| **#3** | Near the runway entrance | Look near the back of the lobby, close to where players line up for the runway walk. |

### Seashell Collection Tips

- **Collect during the dressing phase** — you have ~90 seconds of free movement
- **Use Freeplay mode** — no time pressure to explore the lobby
- **Memorize the locations** — after a few rounds, you can collect all 3 in under 30 seconds
- **Watch for other players** — some seashells may be hidden by other players standing in the area

## The Secret Room & Seashell Crown

### How to Access the Secret Room

1. Collect all **3 seashells** during a round
2. Wait for the round to **end** (after results are displayed)
3. The **secret room door appears** in the lobby
4. Enter the secret room with all 3 seashells
5. Claim your **Seashell Crown** reward

### The Seashell Crown

| Property | Detail |
|----------|--------|
| **Type** | Headpiece/Accessory |
| **Rarity** | Exclusive |
| **How to Get** | Collect 3 seashells + secret room |
| **Best Themes** | Beach, Summer, Mermaid, Ocean |
| **Style Notes** | Pairs well with light colors, flowy dresses, and nature-themed outfits |

## Treasure Chests (Scarlet's Treasure Hunt)

### What Are Treasure Chests?

During special events like **Scarlet's Treasure Hunt**, treasure chests appear in the lobby. Each chest contains seashells (50-200 per chest).

### Treasure Chest Locations

| Chest | Location Hint | Reward |
|-------|--------------|--------|
| **Chest 1** | Near the lobby entrance area | ~50 seashells |
| **Chest 2** | Behind the dressing room | ~75 seashells |
| **Chest 3** | Near the runway entrance | ~75 seashells |
| **Final Chest** | Requires finding all previous chests first | ~200 seashells |

### Treasure Hunt Tips

- **Complete during events** — treasure chests only appear during specific events
- **Follow the community** — the DTI Discord and TikTok communities quickly share chest locations
- **Collect all chests** — the final chest requires finding all previous ones
- **Save seashells** — don't spend them immediately; wait for new seashell shop items

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Where exactly are the seashells?

The 3 seashells are hidden near the spawn area, behind the dressing room, and near the runway entrance. They're on the ground level and may be partially hidden by lobby furniture.

### Can I collect seashells during a round?

Yes, you can collect seashells during the dressing phase (~90 seconds) and even during the runway/voting phases if you skip dressing.

### Do seashells reset between rounds?

No, seashells stay collected. Once you pick one up, it's yours until you use it for the secret room quest.

### What happens if I don't use the Seashell Crown right away?

The Seashell Crown stays in your inventory permanently. You can use it in any future round.

### Are treasure chests always available?

No, treasure chests are event-specific. They appear during Scarlet's Treasure Hunt and similar events. Check the DTI Discord for event announcements.

### How many seashells do I need total?

To get the Seashell Crown, you need to collect 3 seashells (the quest items, not the currency). For the seashell shop, you'll need additional seashells earned through codes, events, and daily rewards.
