## VIP Overview

Dress To Impress VIP is a paid game pass that grants access to exclusive items, private dressing areas, and cosmetic perks. It is the game's primary monetization feature alongside the Custom Makeup pass.

### VIP Pricing

| Option | Robux | Approximate USD | Best For |
|--------|-------|-----------------|----------|
| Monthly VIP | 299 | ~$3.99 | Trying it out, short-term players |
| Permanent VIP | 799 | ~$10.00 | Regular players (pays for itself in 3 months) |

If you play DTI even semi-regularly, the permanent option is the clear choice. Three months of monthly VIP already exceeds the permanent price.

## Free Player Item Sources

The free item catalog in DTI has grown enormously through updates. Free players now have access to:

### Seasonal Event Items (Free)

Every major update adds free items available to all players:
- Halloween updates: gothic dresses, monster accessories, dark wings
- Winter updates: coats, scarves, winter gowns, ice-themed accessories
- Valentine's updates: heart accessories, pink/red dresses, romantic poses
- Summer updates: beachwear, sunglasses, summer dresses

### Code Items (Free)

DTI regularly releases codes that unlock exclusive items. These rotate with updates and collaborations. Free players who stay on top of active codes can build an impressive wardrobe.

### In-Game Currency Items (Free)

All players can purchase items with in-game cash earned from rounds:
- Dressing room basics (shirts, pants, shoes)
- Accessory wall items (hats, bags, jewelry)
- Salon hairstyles and colors

## Direct Comparison: Can You Dupe VIP Items?

One of the biggest arguments against VIP is that many of its exclusive items can be approximated with free alternatives:

| VIP Exclusive | Free Alternative | How |
|---------------|-----------------|-----|
| Angel Wings | Seasonal event wings | Halloween or fantasy event drops |
| Fairy Wings | Code wings | Limited-time code rewards |
| Jellyfish Skirt | Layered free skirts + transparent overlay | Creative layering technique |
| Column Dress | Fitted free dress + belt accessory | Accessory layering |
| Victorian Gown | Layered long skirt + corset top | Multi-piece styling |
| VIP Crown | Free tiara / flower crown | Salon hair accessories |
| Sword | Code or event weapon | Limited accessory drops |
| Gold Jewelry | Free jewelry in different metal | Color customization |

**Bottom line**: A skilled free player who knows how to layer items creatively can approximate roughly 70-80% of VIP-exclusive looks.

## VIP vs Custom Makeup: Which to Buy?

This is the most common purchase decision DTI players face:

| Aspect | VIP (799 Robux) | Custom Makeup (349 Robux) |
|--------|-----------------|--------------------------|
| Impact on scores | Moderate — one unique item helps but doesn't guarantee votes | High — custom faces are highly visible and can transform any outfit |
| Intrinsic value | Access to ~15 exclusive physical items | Unlimited custom face designs |
| Longevity | Items may be power-crept by free updates | Feature stays relevant indefinitely |
| Price | Higher | Lower |
| Permanence | Yes (with permanent option) | Yes |

**Recommendation**: If you can only buy one, get **Custom Makeup**. It affects every single round you play and is more visible to voters than a single VIP-exclusive dress.

If you can afford both, the combination is powerful — Custom Makeup for faces and VIP items for outfit pieces.

## Who Should and Shouldn't Buy VIP

| Buy VIP If | Skip VIP If |
|------------|-------------|
| You play daily or near-daily | You play casually (1-2 times per week) |
| You specifically want wings or the jellyfish skirt | You're happy with free items and codes |
| You want private dressing rooms | You don't mind others seeing your work-in-progress |
| You enjoy collecting exclusive items | You're on a budget |
| You've already bought Custom Makeup | You haven't bought Custom Makeup yet (get that first) |

## Pro Tips for Both Free and VIP Players

### For Free Players
- Redeem every code on release day — they expire
- Farm cash during 2x weekends to buy all available free items
- Master item layering to create looks that rival VIP-exclusive items
- Study VIP-only items on the runway to identify what you can approximate

### For VIP Players
- Focus your VIP items on themes where wings or the jellyfish skirt dominate (fantasy, mermaid, angel)
- Don't over-rely on VIP items — voters notice when you use the same VIP dress every round
- Use the private dressing room to experiment with risky color combinations before debuting them
