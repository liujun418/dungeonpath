## How We Rank Monsters

Each monster is evaluated across four categories and given an overall tier:

- **PvP Multiplayer** — Performance in the ranked ladder against human opponents
- **Team Wars** — Value in coordinated team battles and defense setups
- **Adventure Map** — Efficiency at clearing PvE content
- **Dungeons** — Performance in time-limited dungeon events

Tiers are defined as:

| Tier | Rating | Description |
|------|--------|-------------|
| **S** | Meta-Defining | Dominates their role; no real substitute. Must-have if obtainable. |
| **A** | Excellent | Top-tier performer; consistently strong in their niche. |
| **B** | Good | Solid choice; effective but outclassed by higher tiers. |
| **C** | Niche | Usable in specific situations; not a generalist pick. |
| **D** | Outclassed | Power-crept by newer monsters; invest only if you have nothing better. |

## Best Team Compositions by Game Mode

### PvP Offense Team (Ranked Ladder)

| Slot | Recommended Monster | Alternative | Role |
|------|-------------------|-------------|------|
| Lead | **Griffania** (S) | Cryocrawler (A) | Speed controller — goes first, applies stun/freeze |
| 2 | **Barbatos** (S) | Ra'Zhul (A) | Primary damage dealer |
| 3 | **Zombic** (S) | Miserus (A) | Denier — locks down enemy threats |
| 4 | **Kaguya** (S) | Druid (A) | Healer/support — keeps team alive |

**Strategy:** Lead with Griffania's AoE stun, follow with Zombic's possession on the enemy healer, burst the enemy attacker with Barbatos, then clean up. Kaguya revives if anything goes wrong.

### Team War Defense

| Slot | Monster | Role | Priority |
|------|---------|------|----------|

| 2 | **Nishant's Pet** (A) | AoE possession controller | Second slot — possession procs after tank absorbs |
| 3 | **Warmaster Thalassa** (S) | Burst damage | Third slot — cleans up after control effects land |
| Back | **Kaguya** (S) | Revive support | Last slot — protected by frontline, revives fallen allies |

### Adventure Map Speed Clear

| Slot | Monster | Role | Notes |
|------|---------|------|-------|
| Lead | **Yedra** (A) | AoE attacker | Low cooldowns, clears waves quickly |
| 2 | **Warmaster Thalassa** (S) | Boss killer | Deletes boss nodes in 1-2 turns |
| 3 | **Griffania** (S) | Speed + cleanup | Fast animation, good for farming |

## Rune Recommendations by Role

| Role | Best Rune Set | Stat Priority | Notes |
|------|-------------|---------------|-------|
| **Attacker** | 3x Strength | ATK% > Speed > HP% | Maximize damage output |
| **Speed Controller** | 3x Speed | Speed > HP% > ATK% | Must outspeed enemy team |
| **Denier** | 3x Speed + Accuracy substats | Speed > Accuracy > HP% | Need both speed AND accuracy |
| **Tank** | 3x Life | HP% > DEF% > Speed | Pure survivability |
| **Healer/Support** | 3x Speed | Speed > HP% > DEF% | Go first to heal/cleanse before enemy acts |
