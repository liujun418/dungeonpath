## Understanding Islands and Habitats

Your islands are the foundation of your Monster Legends economy. Every habitat you place generates passive gold, and every monster you house contributes to your collection, breeding pool, and combat roster. A well-organized base produces 2-3x more gold than a haphazard one.

## Optimal Base Layout Principles

### The Central Hub Design

Place these buildings in the center of your Main Island for efficient access:

| Building | Placement | Reason |
|----------|-----------|--------|
| **Breeding Mountain** | Center | Most frequently used building. See [Breeding Guide](/posts/monster-legends/breeding-guide/) for combination recipes. |
| **Hatchery** | Adjacent to Breeding Mountain | Minimize travel between breeding and hatching |
| **Temples** | Edge of main island | Accessed less frequently; don't waste center space |
| **Farms** | Grouped together on one island | Keep food production in one zone |

### Island Specialization

As you unlock more islands, dedicate each to a purpose:

| Island | Specialization | What to Place |
|--------|---------------|---------------|
| **Main Island** | Operations hub | Breeding Mountain, Hatchery, Farms, Temples, 2-3 key habitats |
| **Nature Island** | Nature habitats only | 8-10 Nature habitats, Nature Temple |
| **Fire Island** | Fire habitats only | 8-10 Fire habitats, Fire Temple |
| **Water Island** | Water habitats | Water habitats + Temple |
| **Magic Island** | Magic + overflow | Magic habitats + overflow from full islands |
| **Dark Island** | Dark habitats | Dark habitats + Temple |
| **Later Islands** | Element habitats + decoration | One element per island |

### Space Efficiency Tips

- **Habitats are 3x3 or 4x4.** Plan your grid to fit them without gaps.
- **Don't place temples on element islands.** Temples on the main island free up space on dedicated islands for more habitats.
- **Group buildings by use frequency.** The buildings you tap most (Breeding, Hatchery, Farms) should be closest to where your screen opens.
- **Leave paths between habitat blocks.** Makes collecting gold easier without mis-tapping.

## Gold Collection Optimization

### Collection Frequency

The ideal collection frequency depends on how upgraded your habitats are:

| Habitat Level | Gold Cap Reach Time | Recommended Collection |
|--------------|-------------------|----------------------|
| 1-3 | ~2 hours | Every 2-4 hours |
| 4-6 | ~4 hours | Every 4-6 hours |
| 7-8 | ~6-8 hours | 3x per day (morning, afternoon, evening) |
| 9-10 | ~10-12 hours | 2x per day (morning, night) |

### Collection Strategy

| Strategy | When to Use |
|----------|------------|
| **Tap each habitat individually** | When you have time; collects full amount |
| **Collect All button** | When in a hurry; slight gold loss but time-efficient |
| **Gold production monsters** | Place in highest-level habitats for maximum passive income |

### The Gold Collection Math

```
Daily Gold = (Sum of all habitat gold rates × Gold trait multipliers × Temple bonuses) × Collection frequency efficiency

Collection frequency efficiency:
- Collect every 2 hours: ~90% efficiency (near-perfect)
- Collect 3x per day: ~75% efficiency
- Collect 1x per day: ~50% efficiency (wasting half your potential gold)
```

## Late-Game Base Organization

When you have 8+ islands and 50+ habitats, organization becomes critical:

1. **Elemental islands** (one per element): Fill each entirely with habitats of that element + its temple
2. **Overflow islands**: For elements where you have more monsters than habitat slots
3. **Vanity island**: Use one island for decoration and event buildings (they don't need to be efficient)
4. **Operating island (Main)**: Breeding Mountain, Hatchery, Farms only — move all habitats to dedicated islands
