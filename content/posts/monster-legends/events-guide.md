## Why Events Are Your Best Source of Progression

Events are where Monster Legends gives away its best rewards. A single well-played event can net you a Legendary or even Mythic monster, millions in gold and food, and premium runes—all without spending gems. Players who skip or half-play events progress at a fraction of the speed of those who participate.

Monster Legends rotates through several event types, each with different mechanics and strategies. This guide covers all of them.

---

## Event Type 1: Maze Events

Maze events are the most common and arguably the most important event type. You navigate a branching path grid, collecting rewards at each node.

### How Maze Events Work

| Mechanic | Detail |
|----------|--------|
| **Entry cost** | Maze coins (earned from Adventure Map, dungeons, daily missions) |
| **Movement** | Spend coins to move to adjacent nodes |
| **Rewards** | Monsters (including exclusives), food, gold, runes, gems, cells |
| **Duration** | 7-10 days |
| **Main prize** | Usually a Legendary or Mythic monster at the end of a path |

### Maze Strategy: Path Selection

Not every node is worth collecting. Here's the priority system:

| Node Type | Collect? | Reason |
|-----------|----------|--------|
| **Monster egg/cells** | Always | Primary reason to run the maze |
| **Gems** | Always | Premium currency is always worth 5-15 coins |
| **High-tier runes (Level 5+)** | Usually | Good value; saves rune farming time |
| **Food (10,000+)** | Usually | Worth the coin cost |
| **Gold (100,000+)** | Sometimes | Depends on how many coins you have |
| **Low food (< 5000)** | Skip | Not worth the coin cost |
| **Low gold (< 50,000)** | Skip | Not worth the coin cost |
| **Low-tier runes (Level 1-3)** | Skip | You'll get better runes elsewhere |

### Maze Strategy: Coin Management

- **Never let maze coins cap.** The maximum is 600. Collect them from your inbox before they expire (24-hour expiry).
- **Plan your route before spending.** Look at the full maze map (community maps are posted on Reddit and the Monster Legends Wiki within hours of event launch).
- **Calculate coin requirements.** If the main prize is 3,000 coins away and you earn ~400/day, you need 7.5 days—check the event timer.
- **Save coins between Maze events.** Leftover coins DO carry over to the next Maze, but only up to 600.

### Expected Maze Rewards (F2P, Full Participation)

| Reward Category | Typical Haul (7-day Maze) |
|----------------|--------------------------|
| Monster | 1 Legendary OR 1 Mythic (main prize) |
| Monster cells | Enough to Rank 3 one monster |
| Food | 200,000 – 500,000 |
| Gold | 1,000,000 – 3,000,000 |
| Gems | 50 – 150 |
| Runes | 2-5 high-tier runes |

## Event Type 2: Race Events

Race events are PvP-like competitions where you compete against other players on a progress track. The farther you progress, the better your rewards.

### How Race Events Work

| Mechanic | Detail |
|----------|--------|
| **Progression** | Earn points by completing tasks (breed monsters, collect gold, feed monsters, etc.) |
| **Milestones** | Reach point thresholds to claim rewards |
| **Leaderboard** | Compete against ~100 other players on your race track |
| **Duration** | 3-4 days |
| **Main prize** | Top 3 on leaderboard get exclusive monster |

### Race Event Tasks and Point Values

| Task | Points (Typical) | Repeatable? | Best Strategy |
|------|-----------------|-------------|---------------|
| Breed a monster | 5-15 per breed | Yes | Run 15s-5min breeds back-to-back |
| Collect gold | 1 per 100 gold | Yes | Save habitat collection for race |
| Hatch an egg | 10-30 per hatch | Yes | Stockpile eggs before race starts |
| Feed monsters | 1 per 100 food | Yes | Save food for race tasks |
| Win PvP battles | 20-50 per win | Yes | Drop rank before race, then climb |
| Complete dungeon node | 15-40 per node | Limited by keys | Save keys for race |

### Race Strategy: Preparation Is Everything

The key to winning Races is preparing **before** the event starts:

1. **Stockpile eggs.** Keep 3-4 eggs ready in your hatchery. Don't hatch them until the Race starts.
2. **Save food.** Stop feeding monsters 2-3 days before an expected Race.
3. **Save gold.** Uncollected habitat gold can be claimed during the Race for instant point bursts.
4. **Save dungeon keys.** Cap at 3/3 before the Race starts, then spend them all when the Race is live.
5. **Drop PvP rank.** Lose some matches on purpose before the Race to get easier opponents for quick wins.

### Race Strategy: During the Event

| Phase | What to Do |
|-------|------------|
| **First hour** | Claim all saved eggs, food, gold, and dungeon keys for instant points; surge to top 10 |
| **Middle** | Consistently complete tasks; run short breeding timers; win PvP matches |
| **Last hour** | Spend any saved resources; snipe leaderboard position |
| **Gem usage** | Only spend gems if you're 1-3 spots away from the exclusive monster in the last 30 minutes |

## Event Type 3: Puzzle Events

Puzzle events require you to solve a grid-based puzzle by moving tiles. It's the most F2P-friendly event type.

### How Puzzle Events Work

| Mechanic | Detail |
|----------|--------|
| **Tokens** | Earned from Adventure Map, dungeons, and missions |
| **Puzzle board** | 3x3 to 5x5 grid with face-down tiles |
| **Flips** | Spend tokens to flip tiles; matching pairs give rewards |
| **Duration** | 5-7 days |

### Puzzle Strategy

1. **Always flip the guaranteed monster tile path.** Community guides show which tiles contain the monster—prioritize those.
2. **Don't try to flip every tile.** Focus on the tiles with monster cells, gems, and high-value runes.
3. **Tokens are limited.** You won't have enough to clear the board; budget accordingly.
4. **Use a screenshot or notes.** Track which tiles you've flipped to avoid wasting tokens on known junk tiles.

## Event Type 4: Team Dungeons

Team Dungeons require coordination with your Team members to clear nodes and defeat bosses.

### How Team Dungeons Work

| Mechanic | Detail |
|----------|--------|
| **Node types** | Battle nodes, puzzle nodes, donation nodes |
| **Progress** | Each node cleared by a team member advances the dungeon |
| **Boss** | At the end of each floor, a boss must be defeated by multiple members |
| **Rewards** | Distributed to all participants based on contribution |
| **Duration** | 5-7 days |

### Team Dungeon Strategy

| Role | What to Do |
|------|------------|
| **Heavy hitters** | Focus on the boss; use your best team |
| **Mid-level players** | Clear puzzle and donation nodes |
| **Newer players** | Contribute to donation nodes; even small contributions add up |

**Key tip:** Donation nodes are the easiest way for lower-level players to contribute significantly. Donate gold and food rather than battling if your team is underleveled.

## Event Type 5: Breeding Events

Covered in detail in our [Breeding Guide](/posts/monster-legends/breeding-guide/). Key reminders:

- Breeding events have **limited-time parents**—you must use specific monsters during the event window
- The event island is **separate** from your regular Breeding Mountain
- Breed timers on the event island are typically shorter than normal (50-70% of normal time)
- **Start your last breed before the timer expires**—the breed can finish after the event ends

## Monthly Event Rotation (Typical Pattern)

Monster Legends follows a roughly predictable event schedule:

| Week of Month | Event Type | Typical Reward Tier |
|--------------|------------|-------------------|
| Week 1 | Maze | Legendary monster |
| Week 2 | Team Dungeon | Monster cells + resources |
| Week 3 | Race | Exclusive Legendary/Mythic (top 3) |
| Week 4 | Breeding Event | Exclusive breedable monster |

Events often overlap by 1-2 days. During overlaps, prioritize: **Race > Maze > Breeding > Team Dungeon**.

## Event Gem Spending: When It's Worth It

In general, play events F2P. The rare exceptions where gems are worth spending:

| Situation | Recommended Spend | Why |
|-----------|-----------------|-----|
| Race: 3rd place, 30 min left, 500 pts behind | 50-100 gems on energy refills | Exclusive monsters are worth 500+ gems |
| Maze: 50 coins short of main prize on last day | 25 gems for coin pack | Legendary/Mythic for 25 gems is incredible value |
| Breeding: Guaranteed Legendary from event island | 50 gems total on speed-ups | Legendaries cost 500+ gems in shop |
| Regular Maze day 2 | 0 gems | Never worth it this early |
