## SECRET ROOMS AND HOW TO ACCESS THEM

### The Library Secret (Door 50)

The Library at Door 50 contains more than just the Figure encounter and code puzzle.

**How to Find the Secret:**

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Enter The Library at Door 50 |
| 2 | Before starting the book hunt, **explore the perimeter** of the room |
| 3 | Look for a **hidden alcove or bookshelf** that appears slightly different |
| 4 | Interact with the suspicious bookshelf to reveal a hidden area |
| 5 | Inside, you may find **extra loot, lore items, or a rare entity encounter** |

**What You Find:**
- Hidden containers with rare items
- Possible lore fragments about the Hotel's history
- A chance encounter with rare entities

---

### The Electrical Room Secrets (Door 100)

The Electrical Room at Door 100 has hidden elements beyond the circuit breaker puzzle.

**Secrets to Find:**

| Secret | How to Access | Reward |
|--------|--------------|--------|
| **Hidden Panel** | After solving the circuit breaker, search the walls for a hidden panel | Lore item or rare container |
| **Developer Note** | Examine the electrical panels closely; some contain developer messages | Easter egg text |
| **Extra Loot** | Search behind the circuit breaker box | Additional gold or items |

**Pro Tip:** Take your time in the Electrical Room after solving the puzzle. Figure is gone once the door opens, so you can safely search for hidden items.

### The Green House

A secret room with a garden theme, hidden somewhere in The Hotel.

**How to Find It:**

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Play through The Hotel normally |
| 2 | Look for rooms that have **unusual plant decorations** or a **green-tinted door** |
| 3 | The Green House may appear randomly between certain door ranges |
| 4 | Enter when you spot it — it does not appear in every run |

**What You Find:**
- Themed room with plant decorations
- Possible Green Herb (used for Skull Lock in the Infirmary)
- Easter egg references to other LSPLASH games
- Rare item containers

## HIDDEN LORE AND STORY ELEMENTS

DOORS has a surprisingly deep lore hidden throughout the game. Here is what the community has pieced together:

### The Hotel's Story

| Lore Element | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **The Hotel** | An endless, procedurally generated hotel that seems to exist outside of normal reality |
| **The Entities** | Each entity appears to be a manifestation of different fears or concepts (Rush = panic, Figure = being hunted, Seek = being pursued) |
| **Guiding Light** | A benevolent force that helps players navigate the Hotel — possibly the Hotel's "conscience" |
| **Jeff** | A shopkeeper who exists within the Hotel, seemingly unaffected by the entities |
| **The Doors** | Each door leads to a new, randomly generated room — the Hotel reconfigures itself for each visitor |

### The Mines' Story

| Lore Element | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **The Mines** | A deeper, more dangerous level beneath The Hotel |
| **The Nest** | Figure's home territory — the entity is most powerful here |
| **The Figure** | A blind hunter that has existed in both The Hotel and The Mines, suggesting it is a permanent resident |
| **Generator Rooms** | The Mines require power to progress, suggesting they were once an operational facility |

### The Outdoors' Story

| Lore Element | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **The Outdoors** | A garden-themed area that contrasts with the dark, enclosed Hotel and Mines |
| **The Groundskeeper** | An entity that enforces rules about staying on paths — possibly a guardian of the garden |
| **The Lotus** | A recurring theme; collecting Lotus Petals and crafting a Lotus Flower suggests a cycle of life and rebirth |
| **The Hedge Maze** | The final challenge of The Outdoors, with the World Lotus at its center |

### Community Theories

| Theory | Evidence |
|--------|----------|
| **The Hotel is alive** | The procedural generation and Guiding Light suggest the Hotel is a sentient entity |
| **Entities are former guests** | Some entities may be previous visitors who were trapped and transformed |
| **The Backdoor leads to the truth** | The secret Backdoor room may reveal the Hotel's origin |
| **Jeff is not what he seems** | Jeff's immunity to entities and constant shop presence raise questions |

## SPEEDRUN TIPS

### Hotel Speedrun Strategy

| Phase | Strategy | Time Target |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| **Doors 1-10** | Sprint through, check for Rush only | ~30 seconds |
| **Doors 11-49** | Maintain fast pace; hide quickly for Rush/Ambush | ~2-3 minutes |
| **Door 50 (Library)** | Rush the book hunt; use Guiding Light if available | ~1-2 minutes |
| **Doors 51-99** | Skip unnecessary container searches; focus on progression | ~3-4 minutes |
| **Door 100 (Electrical)** | Memorize circuit breaker pattern quickly | ~1-2 minutes |
| **Total Time** | **Complete Hotel run** | **~7-12 minutes** |

### Speedrun Optimization Tips

| Tip | Details |
|-----|---------|
| **Memorize entity sounds** | Faster recognition = less time wasted |
| **Know locker locations** | Pre-learned hiding spots save seconds |
| **Skip container searching** | In speedruns, items are secondary to speed |
| **Practice the Library code** | The shape-to-number translation becomes faster with practice |
| **Memorize circuit breaker patterns** | The Door 100 puzzle follows recognizable patterns |
| **Use Vitamins strategically** | Speed boost during Seek chase saves time |

### Rush Mode Speedrun

| Feature | Impact on Speed |
|---------|----------------|
| All entities look like Rush | Easier recognition (uniform appearance) |
| Faster Figure and Seek | Harder to survive, but faster overall run |
| Shooting mechanic | Can eliminate some Rush entities |
| **Time Target** | **Under 5-8 minutes for experienced players** |

### No Item Run

Complete The Hotel without picking up or buying any items.

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|

Complete The Hotel without using any lockers or hiding spots.

| Requirement | Details |

## FAQ

**Q: How many achievements are there in DOORS?**
A: There are **87+ achievements/badges** in DOORS as of May 2026. New achievements are added with each major update.

**Q: What is the hardest achievement to get?**
A: The **Perfectionist** achievement (complete The Hotel without taking any damage) is widely considered the hardest. It requires flawless execution of every entity encounter.

**Q: How do I find the secret Backdoor?**
A: The Backdoor is a secret room that appears under specific conditions. The exact trigger is still being explored by the community, but it is believed to involve specific door sequences and camping behaviors.

**Q: Are there any achievements that require multiplayer?**
A: Some achievements are easier with multiplayer (like reviving teammates), but most can be completed solo. The **Solo Survivor** achievement specifically requires playing alone.

**Q: What is the Library secret?**
A: The Library at Door 50 contains a hidden alcove or room behind certain bookshelves. It may contain rare items, lore fragments, or hidden entity encounters.

**Q: Can I get achievements in Rush Mode?**
A: Rush Mode has its own set of achievements, including **Rush Mode Clear**. Some general achievements also apply to Rush Mode runs.

**Q: What achievements were added with The Outdoors?**
A: The Outdoors added achievements including **Touched Grass**, **Down The Water Spout**, **Do'h!**, **Feed The Birds**, **Picking Flowers**, **Detour**, and **Rebirth**.

**Q: How do I get the "Pls Donate" achievement?**
A: Tip Jeff a total of 100g across multiple donations. This means donating 10g ten times (or equivalent) at Jeff's Shop.

**Q: Is there a 100% completion guide?**
A: This guide covers all known achievements and secrets. For the most up-to-date completion checklist, check the [DOORS Wiki Achievements page](https://doors-game.fandom.com/wiki/Achievements).

**Q: Will The Archives update add new achievements?**
A: Yes, The Archives update (expected May/June 2026) will add a significant number of new achievements tied to the revamped Rooms subfloor content.
