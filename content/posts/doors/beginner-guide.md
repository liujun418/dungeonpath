## What Is DOORS?

**DOORS** is a multiplayer roguelike horror game on Roblox developed by **LSPLASH** in collaboration with **Redibles**. Since launching in 2022, it has become one of the most iconic horror games on the platform — inspiring countless YouTube videos, TikTok clips, and a passionate community.

Inspired by games like *Spooky's Jump Scare Mansion* and *Spelunky*, DOORS puts you in a procedurally generated hotel (and later, mines) where you must navigate through 100+ numbered doors, survive hostile entities, solve puzzles, and reach the end alive.

**Why play DOORS?**
- Tense, atmospheric horror with clever entity design
- Procedurally generated runs — every playthrough is unique
- Deep entity roster with unique warning signs and survival mechanics
- Puzzle-solving (code-breaking, memory games, item hunts)
- Multiplayer co-op — revive teammates and share resources
- Multiple floors and game modes for endless replayability
- 87+ achievements to collect

## The Hotel (Floor 1: Doors 1-100)

### Room Progression

| Door Range | What to Expect | Key Entities |
|------------|---------------|-------------|
| **1-8** | Tutorial area — basic mechanics | Timothy, Eyes |
| **9-49** | Increasing difficulty, puzzles appear | Rush, Ambush, Screech, Dupe, Halt |
| **50** | **The Library** — Figure encounter + code puzzle | **Figure**, Guiding Light |
| **51-59** | Jeff Shop appears (Door 52-59) | Rush, Ambush |
| **60-99** | Peak difficulty, complex puzzles | Seek (again), Ambush |
| **100** | **The Electrical Room** — final Figure + circuit breaker | **Figure** (final boss) |

### All Hotel Entities

For a detailed bestiary covering every entity's behavior, spawn conditions, and survival strategies, see our [DOORS Entities Bestiary](/posts/doors/entities-bestiary/).

#### Rush

| Warning | Lights flicker/dim |
|---------|-------------------|
| Survival | Hide in nearest **locker** or behind a **bed** immediately |
| Danger | ★★ |

Rush is the first entity you'll encounter and the most common. When lights flicker, **don't panic** — find the closest hiding spot and get in. The key is speed, not perfection.

#### Ambush

| Warning | Lights flicker for LONGER than Rush |
|---------|-----------------------------------|
| Survival | Hide in locker; it **bounces back MULTIPLE times** — stay hidden until all passes end |
| Danger | ★★★ |

**The entity that kills most beginners.** Unlike Rush, Ambush doesn't just pass once — it returns several times. Players who peek between passes get caught. **Stay hidden until you're absolutely sure it's gone.**

#### Screech

| Warning | Whispering sound in dark rooms |
|---------|-------------------------------|
| Survival | **Quickly look around** (move your mouse/camera) to scare it away |
| Danger | ★ |

Simple encounter — if you hear whispering in a dark room, spin your camera rapidly. Don't overthink it.

#### Seek

| Warning | Cutscene triggers at Door 50 |
|---------|-----------------------------|
| Survival | Run the chase; follow **glowing arrows and hands** for direction; **jump over fallen furniture** |
| Danger | ★★★ |

Seek triggers a long hallway chase sequence. The key is to follow the glowing guidance (arrows, floating hands) and jump over obstacles. Don't try to fight back — just run.

#### Figure (Door 50 & Door 100)

| Warning | Heavy breathing sound; you hear it approaching |
|---------|----------------------------------------------|
| Survival | **Crouch-walk** to stay quiet; **hide in closets** when it's close; **hold breath** (hold Space) |
| Danger | ★★★★ |

Figure is the most complex entity in The Hotel. There are two encounters:

**Door 50 — The Library:** You need to find books with shape symbols, translate them to numbers using a chart, and enter the code to unlock the door. Figure hunts you during this process.

**Door 100 — The Electrical Room:** Final boss. Circuit breaker memory puzzle — a pattern of switches is briefly shown, and you need to replicate it. Memorize and match the pattern.

**Key tips for Figure:**
- Always crouch-walk when Figure is active — running makes noise
- Hide in closets when you hear it approaching
- Hold Space to hold your breath when Figure is very close

#### Dupe

| Warning | Multiple doors in one room with different numbers |
|---------|------------------------------------------------|
| Survival | Only enter the door with the **correct number** matching the room count |
| Danger | ★★ |

Simple but deadly. Count which door you should be on, then only enter that door. Wrong doors = damage.

#### Halt

| Warning | Glitching visuals in long hallway |
|---------|----------------------------------|
| Survival | Run **BACKWARD** the way you came, then forward again |
| Danger | ★★ |

Counterintuitive mechanic — you need to go backward first. Many players die because they try to push forward.

#### Eyes

| Warning | Floating eyeballs visible in rooms |
|---------|-----------------------------------|
| Survival | Walk around them; touching causes damage |
| Danger | ★ |

#### Snare

| Warning | Distorted door texture (subtle visual cue) |
|---------|-------------------------------------------|
| Survival | Don't walk through doors with suspicious textures |
| Danger | ★★ |

#### Guiding Light (Friendly)

| Appearance | Blue light beams |
|------------|-----------------|
| Effect | Heals, gives hints, revives players |
| Notes | Helpful entity — follow it! |

#### Timothy (Minor)

| Warning | Opening a drawer |
|---------|-----------------|
| Effect | Small spider jumpscare; minor HP damage |
| Danger | ☆ |

## Puzzle Solutions

### Library Safe Code (Door 50)

1. Enter The Library and collect scattered **books**
2. Each book contains **shape symbols** that correspond to **numbers**
3. Find the door with a **code lock** and reference **chart**
4. Translate shapes → numbers using the chart
5. Enter the number combination to unlock

**Pro tip:** Camp before the library for 3-4 minutes so **Guiding Light** spawns and guides you through the puzzle.

### Circuit Breaker (Door 100)

1. A pattern of switches is **briefly displayed** in a box
2. **Empty square** = leave switch OFF
3. **Filled square** = turn switch ON (match the number shown)
4. Memorize and replicate the pattern

**In Super Hard Mode**, the puzzle is more challenging but follows the same rules. Practice the memory technique — chunk the pattern into groups of 2-3.

### Generator Rooms (The Mines)

1. Find **Fuses** scattered in nearby rooms
2. Install them in the **generator** to power it and open the next door
3. If you have a **Crowbar** (3% spawn), you can skip these entirely

### Minecart Track Switching (The Mines)

1. Use minecarts to break through wooden barricades
2. Adjust the **rail lever/dial** to switch tracks toward the correct path
3. Speedrun technique: send the cart the right way without changing the dial on Door 101

### The Nest Anchors (Door 200)

1. Activate **5-6 Anchors** around the area
2. Figure hunts by sound — **crouch-walk** everywhere
3. Place **Alarm Clocks** away from your position to distract Figure
4. Be strategic about movement order — plan your route before starting

## Tips for Beginners

### Essential Survival Tips

| Tip | Why It Matters |
|-----|---------------|
| **Listen carefully** | Audio cues are the #1 survival tool — learn the sounds |
| **Learn locker locations** early | Hide spots save your life every run |
| **Crouch-walk** around Figure | Running makes noise; Figure hunts by sound |
| **Keep moving** in The Mines | Staying still triggers Dread |
| **Check door numbers** | Dupe will kill you if you enter the wrong door |
| **Don't stay in lockers longer than needed** | You waste time and resources |
| **Save Crucifix for dangerous encounters** | It's expensive but can save your run |
| **Collect light items early** | Essential for Mines and dark rooms |
| **Cooperate in multiplayer** | Revive teammates; share items |
| **Expect to die many times** | Trial and error is how you learn |

### Entity Sound Reference

| Entity | Sound Cue | What to Do |
|--------|-----------|------------|
| **Rush** | Lights flicker, whooshing | Hide immediately |
| **Ambush** | Lights flicker longer | Hide; expect multiple passes |
| **Figure** | Heavy breathing | Crouch-walk, hide in closets |
| **Screech** | Whispering in dark rooms | Spin camera rapidly |
| **Gloombats** | Buzzing in dark areas | Use light source |
| **Giggle** | Low growl/ping | Look up for ceiling rocks |
| **Grumble** | Wandering sounds | Break line of sight, hide |

### Common Beginner Mistakes

| Mistake | Consequence | Fix |
|---------|------------|-----|
| Running during Figure encounters | Figure hears you and attacks | Crouch-walk, hold breath when close |
| Not looking up in The Mines | Giggle drops rocks on you | Always check ceiling in dark areas |
| Stepping on Snare doors | Damage from disguised doors | Look for distorted door textures |
| Ignoring door numbers | Dupe kills you | Count and verify before entering |
| Not searching containers | Missing essential items | Check every drawer, cabinet, and shelf |
| Staying still too long in Mines | Dread spawns | Keep moving between rooms |
| Stepping on grass in Outdoors | Groundskeeper kills you | Stay on paths |
| Peeking between Ambush passes | Caught by return pass | Stay hidden until ALL passes complete |

## FAQ

**Q: Is DOORS free to play?**
A: Yes, DOORS is completely free on Roblox.

**Q: How many floors are there?**
A: There are multiple floors: **The Hotel** (Doors 1-100), **The Mines** (Doors 101-200), **The Outdoors** (subfloor), **The Backdoor** (secret ~25 rooms), **The Rooms** (subfloor, being revamped as The Archives), **Rush Mode**, and **Endless Mode**.

**Q: What's the hardest entity in DOORS?**
A: **Figure at Door 200 (The Nest)** is widely considered the hardest. It hunts by sound in a large area while you need to activate 5-6 Anchors under pressure. The Door 100 circuit breaker puzzle is also notoriously difficult for beginners.

**Q: How do I unlock The Mines?**
A: You can access The Mines from the Lobby with the **Rock Bottom badge**, or after completing The Hotel.

**Q: What are Knobs and how do I get them?**
A: Knobs are the permanent out-of-run currency. Earn them through codes (redeem in Lobby Shop), achievements, and gameplay. Use them for permanent upgrades and Lobby Shop items.

**Q: Can I play DOORS with friends?**
A: Yes! DOORS supports multiplayer. Playing with friends makes it easier to survive, share items, and revive teammates.

**Q: What's Rush Mode?**
A: Rush Mode is a permanent game mode where all entities look and sound like Rush. Figure and Seek are significantly faster, and there's a new shooting mechanic. It's chaotic and fast-paced.

**Q: What's Endless Mode?**
A: Endless Mode features a single looping room that continues indefinitely with no set endpoint. It tests endurance and consistency.

**Q: How do I get the best codes rewards?**
A: The code **777** gives 777 Knobs + Stardust + 1 Revival — it's the most valuable. Redeem it ASAP as codes may expire at any time.
