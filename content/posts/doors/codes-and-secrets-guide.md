## Overview

**DOORS** by LSPLASH and Redibles is one of the most content-rich horror games on Roblox, packed with codes, secret rooms, easter eggs, hidden achievements, and developer references that many players never discover. Whether you're trying to maximize your free Knobs through codes, find every secret room, or uncover the hidden lore scattered throughout The Hotel and The Mines, this guide has everything you need.

Unlike many Roblox games, DOORS rewards thorough exploration and attention to detail. The developers have hidden dozens of secrets across multiple floors, and the community continues to discover new ones years after the game's launch. ## Active Codes in DOORS (May 2026)

DOORS codes are promotional strings that players redeem for free **Knobs** (the permanent out-of-run currency), **Stardust**, and occasionally special items like Revivals. Knobs are used in the Lobby Shop to buy permanent upgrades and cosmetics.

### How to Redeem Codes

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Launch DOORS on Roblox and enter the **Lobby** |
| 2 | Walk to or look for the **Shop** area in the Lobby |
| 3 | Find the **text input box** (code redemption field) in the Shop menu |
| 4 | Type the code **exactly as shown** — codes are case-sensitive |
| 5 | Click **Redeem** or press Enter |
| 6 | A confirmation message will appear showing your reward (Knobs, Stardust, etc.) |

## Secret Rooms in DOORS — Complete Guide

DOORS contains numerous secret rooms that are not part of the main progression path. These rooms offer unique experiences, exclusive items, achievement progress, and easter eggs. Many players complete dozens of runs without ever finding them.

### All Confirmed Secret Rooms

| Secret Room | Location | Floor | Difficulty to Find | Reward |

| **The Library** (Door 50) | Mandatory story room | The Hotel | N/A (forced encounter) | Figure encounter, book code puzzle |
| **The Electrical Room** (Door 100) | Mandatory story room | The Hotel | N/A (forced encounter) | Final Figure boss, circuit breaker puzzle |
| **Green House** | Hidden side room | The Hotel | Medium | Rare items, Guiding Light interaction |

### Step-by-Step: Finding the Green House

The Green House is one of the more accessible secret rooms in The Hotel.

| Step | Action | Details |
|------|--------|---------|
| 1 | Progress through The Hotel normally | The Green House can appear between Doors 30-60 |
| 2 | Look for a door with a **plant/vine motif** | It will look different from standard doors |
| 3 | Enter the room | You'll find a greenhouse-like area |
| 4 | Search for **Guiding Light** interactions | Blue light beams may appear and guide you |
| 5 | Collect any items on display | Some rooms contain Bandages or Vitamins |

### Step-by-Step: Finding the Painting Room

| Step | Action | Details |
|------|--------|---------|
| 1 | Pay attention to **door paintings** as you progress | Each room has a painting near the door |
| 2 | Look for paintings that are **unusual or distorted** | These indicate a Painting Room entrance |
| 3 | Interact with the unusual painting | Some paintings are interactive secret doors |
| 4 | Enter the hidden room | You'll find a gallery-like space |
| 5 | Observe the artwork | Many paintings are references to other games, developers, or community members |

### Step-by-Step: Accessing the Developer Room

The Developer Room is one of the hardest secrets to find in all of DOORS.

| Step | Action | Details |
|------|--------|---------|
| 1 | Progress to **The Mines** (Door 101+) | The Developer Room is in the Mines floor |
| 2 | Search **office and industrial areas** carefully | Look for walls with unusual textures or interactable objects |
| 3 | Look for **hidden switches or interactive panels** | These may be disguised as normal wall decorations |
| 4 | Interact with the hidden mechanism | A secret door or passage will open |
| 5 | Enter and explore | You'll find developer messages, inside jokes, and possibly an exclusive badge or achievement |

### Step-by-Step: Finding the Infirmary and Skull Lock

| Step | Action | Details |
|------|--------|---------|
| 1 | Explore side rooms in The Hotel thoroughly | The Infirmary is a hidden side room |
| 2 | Look for a door with a **medical cross symbol** | This indicates the Infirmary |
| 3 | Inside, find the **Skull Lock** | It's a special lock that requires a Skeleton Key |
| 4 | Use a **Skeleton Key** (purchased from Jeff's Shop for 300g) | The Skeleton Key opens the Skull Lock |
| 5 | Collect the reward | Usually a **Green Herb** and other rare items |

### Tips for Finding Secret Rooms

| Tip | Details |
|-----|---------|
| **Explore every room thoroughly** | Secret rooms are often accessed through doors or walls that look slightly different |
| **Check behind paintings and decorations** | Many secret entrances are disguised as normal room decorations |
| **Look for Guiding Light** | Blue light beams often lead to hidden rooms or important items |
| **Don't rush through rooms** | Speed-running through DOORS means you'll miss most secret rooms |
| **Listen for audio cues** | Some secret areas have distinct ambient sounds |
| **Play multiple runs** | Secret room spawns are partially randomized — you won't find them all in one run |
| **Use a Skeleton Key** | It guarantees access to locked rooms and increases shop spawn chances |

## FAQ

**Q: How do I redeem codes in DOORS?**
A: Open the Shop in the Lobby, find the text input box, type the code exactly as shown (case-sensitive), and click Redeem. The most valuable code is **777**, which gives 777 Knobs + Stardust + 1 Revival.

**Q: Do DOORS codes expire?**
A: Yes, most codes expire after a period of time. Some codes like **777** and **LAZYDEVS** have remained active for extended periods, but seasonal and event codes typically expire within weeks or months.

**Q: How many secret rooms are there in DOORS?**
A: At least 10 secret rooms have been confirmed across all floors: The Library, The Electrical Room, Green House, Painting Room, The Adit, Developer Room, Jeff's Secret Back Room, The Infirmary, Hidden Courtyard, and Vent System. More may exist.

**Q: How do I find the Developer Room?**
A: The Developer Room is in The Mines floor. Search office and industrial areas for hidden switches or interactive panels disguised as normal wall decorations. It's one of the hardest secrets to find.

**Q: What's the Skull Lock and how do I open it?**
A: The Skull Lock is found in The Infirmary (a hidden room in The Hotel). It requires a **Skeleton Key** (300g from Jeff's Shop) to open. Inside, you'll find a Green Herb and potentially other rare items.

**Q: How many achievements does DOORS have?**
A: DOORS has **87+ badges/achievements** as of the latest update. This number increases with each major update.

**Q: What's the best code to redeem first?**
A: **777** — it gives 777 Knobs, Stardust, and a Revival, making it by far the most valuable single code. Redeem it before any other code.

**Q: Can I access secret rooms in multiplayer?**
A: Yes. Secret rooms are accessible in both solo and multiplayer runs. In fact, having more players increases the chances of someone spotting a hidden entrance.

**Q: Are there hidden achievements in DOORS?**
A: Yes. Many achievements are hidden — their names and requirements are not shown until unlocked. Examples include "Secret Seeker" (find all secret rooms), "A Bone To Pick" (open Skull Lock), and "Picking Flowers" (have Grampy create a Lotus Flower).

**Q: What is The Archives Update?**
A: The Archives Update is a major upcoming update (expected May/June 2026) that will revamp The Rooms subfloor, add new entities, re-add vent rooms, improve replayability, and likely include new codes, achievements, and secrets.

**Q: How do I unlock The Outdoors?**
A: You need 10 Stardust, which you can get from the **777** code or gameplay. Once you have Stardust, select The Outdoors from the Lobby to access the garden-themed subfloor.

**Q: Is there a speedrun community for DOORS?**
A: Yes. The DOORS community has active speedrunners who compete in categories like Any% Hotel, Any% Mines, and 100% runs. Check the DOORS Discord and Reddit for speedrun leaderboards and strategies.
