## How Enchantments Work

Enchantments are equipable passive effects that define your build in Pet Simulator 99. With up to 8 enchant slots (5 free from Rank progression + 3 premium Robux slots), your enchant loadout determines how fast you farm, how hard you hit, and how lucky your hatches are.

There are two categories: **Standard Enchants** (9 types, upgradable through tiers) and **Special Enchants** (21+ types, fixed rarity, obtained from achievements, chests, and the Exclusive Shop).

### Enchant Slots

| Slot Type | How to Unlock | Total |
|-----------|---------------|-------|
| Free Slots | Increase your Rank by completing quests | 5 |
| Premium Slots | Purchase with Robux | 3 |
| **Maximum** | -- | **8** |

## Upgrade System — From Basic to Superior

All standard enchant upgrades are performed at the **Upgrade Enchants Machine** in the **Crimson Forest** area. You fuse multiple books of one tier to create a single book of the next tier.

### Full Upgrade Cost Table

| Tier | Rarity | Books Required | Cumulative Basic Books | Diamond Cost |
|------|--------|---------------|----------------------|--------------|
| 1 | Basic | -- | 1 | -- |
| 2 | Rare | 5 × Tier 1 | 5 | Low |
| 3 | Epic | 5 × Tier 2 | 25 | Moderate |
| 4 | Legendary | 5 × Tier 3 | 125 | High |
| 5 | Mythical | 7 × Tier 4 | 875 | Very High |
| 6 | Exotic | 7 × Tier 5 | 6,125 | Extreme |
| 7 | Superior | 7 × Tier 6 | 42,875 | Maximum |

Reaching a Superior enchant from scratch requires 42,875 Basic books and millions of diamonds. Prioritize upgrading Diamonds and Critical first — these two have the widest utility across all builds.

## Diminishing Returns — Critical Mechanic

Using multiple enchants of the same type stacks with progressively lower effectiveness:

| Copy | Effectiveness |
|------|--------------|
| 1st (Base) | 100% |
| 2nd | 60% |
| 3rd | ~38% |
| 4th | ~28% |
| 5th+ | Diminishes further |

**Exceptions** (no diminishing returns): Magic Orb, Fireworks, Starfall, all Magnet enchants.

Optimal rule: **never equip more than 2 copies of the same enchant.** The second copy at 60% effectiveness is usually worth it for Diamonds and Critical. A third copy at 38% almost never is.
