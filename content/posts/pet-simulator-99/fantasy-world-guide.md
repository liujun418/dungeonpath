## What Is the Fantasy World Event?

The Fantasy World is Pet Simulator 99's major May 2026 limited-time event, built around two core mechanics: **Time Trials** (speed-running breakable waves for leaderboard rewards) and the **Combine-O-Matic** (a present-upgrading machine that lets you convert low-tier presents into Titanic-tier rewards).

The event is accessible to players who have completed all regular worlds. If you're still progressing through earlier worlds, see our [Pet Simulator 99 Beginner Guide](/posts/pet-simulator-99/beginner-guide/) to speed through the main content first.

## How to Access the Fantasy World

1. Complete all regular worlds (World 1 through the most recent world).
2. Once all worlds are cleared, the Fantasy World portal appears in the main hub.
3. Enter the Fantasy World to access Time Trials and the Combine-O-Matic.

The Fantasy World originally launched in May 2025 with 10 zones (Fantasy Spawn, Flora Falls, Lantern Forest, Honeycomb Hollow, Fairy Glade, Windmill Point, Radiant Runestones, Fogbound Forest, Rune Graveyard, Halo Spires), later expanded with 5 additional zones starting at Twilight Grove. The May 2026 event builds on this foundation with new mechanics.

## Combine-O-Matic: How It Works

The Combine-O-Matic is the centerpiece of the Fantasy World event. It converts lower-tier Fantasy Presents into higher-tier ones at a 10:1 ratio.

### Present Tiers

| Tier | Rarity | Combine Cost |
|------|--------|-------------|
| **Small Present** | Common | — (base drop) |
| **Medium Present** | Uncommon | 10 Small |
| **Large Present** | Rare | 10 Medium |
| **Huge Present** | Epic | 10 Large |
| **Titanic Present** | Legendary | 10 Huge |

### Combine-O-Matic Strategy

- **Feed 10 presents of the same tier** into the machine to receive 1 present of the next tier.
- To reach a single Titanic Present, you need **10,000 Small Presents** (10⁴) if starting from scratch — but in practice, Time Trials drop higher-tier presents directly, drastically reducing the grind.
- **Don't hoard low-tier presents** — combine them immediately. The machine shuts down at the event deadline, and unused presents are wasted.
- **Prioritize Time Trials over breakable farming** — Time Trials drop Large and Huge presents directly, skipping multiple combine steps.

### How to Get Fantasy Presents

| Method | Efficiency | Best For |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| **Time Trials** | Highest | Large/Huge presents, leaderboard eligibility |
| **Breakable destruction** | Moderate | Steady Small/Medium supply |
| **Trading** | Variable | Filling gaps in specific tiers |

## Time Trials: Complete Guide

Time Trials are competitive speed runs where you crush through waves of breakables as fast as possible, ending with a boss chest. Your completion time determines your leaderboard ranking.

### Two Leaderboard Types

| Leaderboard | Format | Rewards Based On |
|-------------|--------|-----------------|
| **League** (Team) | Team-based cumulative | Ended May 4 |
| **Solo** | Individual, resets daily | Ongoing through May 16 |

### Time Trials Strategy

- **Use your absolute best pets** — equip your highest-damage pets with top-tier enchantments (Strength, Critical, Royalty).
- **Max out pet slots** — every additional pet slot is more damage output. Prioritize slot expansions before attempting competitive runs.
- **Stack items and power-ups** — activate all damage-boosting items before starting a run. Fruits, potions, and event consumables stack multiplicatively.
- **Use a private server** — powering up without interruptions from other players prevents buff loss and distraction. Public servers can lag during peak hours.
- **Learn the wave layout** — each Time Trial map has a fixed breakable arrangement. Memorizing spawn positions saves seconds per wave.
- **Boss chest optimization** — position yourself at the boss spawn point before the final wave ends to start damaging instantly.

### Time Trials Rewards

| #1 on #1 Team (League) | Rainbow Shiny Gargantuan Wicked Kirin |
| Top 15 Teams / Top 3 Daily Solo | Gargantuan Wicked Kirin |
| Top 765 / Top 753 | Titanic Veil Horse |
| Top 10,765 / Top 5,753 | Huge Knight Golem |

## FAQ

### Can I still use the Combine-O-Matic after May 16?
No. The Combine-O-Matic is a limited-time feature and will be disabled when the Fantasy World event ends on May 16, 2026. Combine all presents before the deadline.

### Do I need to complete all regular worlds to access Fantasy World?
Yes. The Fantasy World is endgame content and requires completing every regular world first. New players should focus on world progression before attempting to join the event.

### Are Time Trials rewards distributed immediately?
League rewards are distributed after the competition period ends. Solo daily rewards reset and are distributed each day. Check the leaderboard UI for your current standing.
