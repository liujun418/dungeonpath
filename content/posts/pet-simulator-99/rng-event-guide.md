The **Pet Simulator 99 Void RNG Event** runs from **May 9 to May 23, 2026**, and it's one of the most complex limited-time events the game has ever released. Instead of the usual egg-hatching loop, the RNG Event introduces a **dice-rolling luck system** set in a dark void world where every roll is a gamble — and the top prize, the **Titanic Arcane Void Cat**, sits behind odds of roughly **1 in 3 billion**.

This guide covers every mechanic, upgrade priority, weather stacking strategy, and the most efficient path to maximizing your odds.

---

## How the RNG Event Works

The RNG Event takes place in the **Void RNG World**, a dedicated zone accessed through a portal in the main hub. The core gameplay loop replaces normal hatching with a **giant blue RNG egg** that you roll using dice mechanics:

1. **Roll the RNG Egg** — Each roll consumes time and generates a random pet from the event pool
2. **Build Combo Streaks** — Continuous rolling without pausing builds a luck multiplier (cap: **1.75× at 5,000 rolls**)
3. **Apply Dice Boosts** — Craft and use dice for massive temporary luck injections
4. **Exploit Weather** — Random weather effects provide short windows of massively boosted odds
5. **Fight the RNG Ultra Chest** — A server-wide boss with **20 billion HP** that drops premium rewards

### Event Duration

| Event | Start | End | Status |
|-------|-------|-----|--------|
| Void RNG Event | May 9, 2026 | May 23, 2026 | **Active** |
| Fantasy World (Combine-O-Matic) | May 2, 2026 | May 16, 2026 | Ended |

## RNG Coins and Permanent Upgrades

**RNG Coins** are the event's dedicated currency, earned primarily by selling weak pets to the **RNG Merchant**. You'll want to invest these coins into permanent upgrades that persist throughout the event.

### Upgrade Priority Order

| Priority | Upgrade | Effect | Why First |
|----------|---------|--------|-----------|
| **1** | RNG Egg Luck | Increases quality of pets rolled | Directly affects Titanic/Huge odds |
| **2** | RNG Hatch Speed | Reduces time per roll | More rolls = more chances + faster combos |
| **3** | Combo Duration | Slows combo streak decay | Keeps your 1.75× multiplier alive longer |
| **4** | Coin Multiplier | Boosts RNG Coin earnings | Helps max other upgrades faster |
| **5** | Dice Effectiveness | Amplifies dice luck bonuses | Strong late-game optimization |

**Strategy tip**: Max RNG Egg Luck before anything else. A 5% better pet quality roll is worth more than rolling 10% faster with worse odds.

## Dice System: Mega Lucky Dice Explained

The dice system is the core multiplier engine of the RNG Event. You craft progressively stronger dice that provide **temporary luck boosts** measured in the millions of percent.

### Dice Crafting Chain

| Dice | Recipe | Luck Boost | Duration |
|------|--------|------------|----------|
| Lucky Dice I | 5 RNG Fragments | +500,000% | 3 min |
| Lucky Dice II | Lucky Dice I ×3 | +2,000,000% | 5 min |
| Mega Lucky Dice I | Lucky Dice II ×3 | +5,000,000% | 8 min |
| **Mega Lucky Dice II** | Mega Lucky Dice I ×3 | **+10,000,000%** | 12 min |

Each tier requires **three** of the previous tier, meaning one Mega Lucky Dice II costs **27 Lucky Dice I** (135 RNG Fragments total). Save your fragments and craft upward rather than burning low-tier dice.

### When to Use Mega Lucky Dice II

Don't pop your Mega Lucky Dice II randomly. Wait for:

1. **Lightning Storm weather** is active (see below)
2. Your **combo streak is at 5,000+** (1.75× multiplier)
3. You have at least **30+ minutes** of uninterrupted play time ahead

Stacking all three conditions turns that 1-in-3-billion chance into something meaningfully better.

## Weather Effects: Lightning Storm vs. Blizzard

The RNG Event features two random weather effects. Learning to play around them is the difference between average results and top-tier pulls.

### Lightning Storm

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Luck multiplier | **3× (200% boost)** |
| Duration | 2 minutes |
| Best action | Immediately pop your best dice |

Lightning Storm is the money weather. When it hits, drop everything and:
- Pop Mega Lucky Dice II if available
- Make sure auto-roll is ON (combo must not break)
- Consider using multiple dice simultaneously if you have extras

### Blizzard

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Combo decay | **Slowed significantly** |
| Dice boost duration | **Doubled** |
| Best action | Pop long-duration dice for efficiency |

Blizzard is less flashy but more strategic. It doubles the duration of any dice you pop, meaning a 12-minute Mega Lucky Dice II becomes **24 minutes**. If you're low on dice, save them for Blizzard windows to maximize uptime.

## Combo Streak System

The combo streak is a luck multiplier that builds as you continuously roll the RNG Egg. The key numbers:

| Rolls | Multiplier |
|-------|------------|
| 100 | 1.05× |
| 500 | 1.15× |
| 1,000 | 1.25× |
| 2,500 | 1.50× |
| **5,000 (cap)** | **1.75×** |

### Preventing Combo Decay

- **Auto-Roll must stay ON** — any gap in rolling starts decay immediately
- The decay rate speeds up over time, so brief interruptions hurt more the longer they last
- Blizzard weather significantly slows decay — use this to take short breaks

## Optimal Strategy Summary

### For Free-to-Play Players

1. **Farm RNG Coins aggressively** — sell every low-tier pet, don't hoard
2. Max upgrades in priority order (Luck → Speed → Combo)
3. Save 135 RNG Fragments for one Mega Lucky Dice II
4. Only pop dice during Lightning Storm
5. Keep auto-roll running at all times
6. Join every RNG Ultra Chest fight for bonus coins and dice

### For Spending Players

1. Buy the **RNG Event Pass** for boosted fragment drops
2. Consider purchasing RNG Coin packs to max upgrades on Day 1
3. Run Mega Lucky Dice II during every Lightning Storm
4. Join an active clan for Clan Battle rewards

### Daily Checklist

- [ ] Claim RNG Coin daily bonus
- [ ] Run auto-roll for at least 1 hour to maintain combo
- [ ] Participate in 3+ RNG Ultra Chest fights
- [ ] Check weather timer — plan dice usage around Lightning Storm windows
- [ ] Sell all inventory pets below Huge tier to the RNG Merchant
