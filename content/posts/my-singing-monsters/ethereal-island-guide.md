## What Is Ethereal Island?

Ethereal Island is a special island unlocked at Level 12 that serves as the permanent home for Ethereal monsters. Unlike Natural Islands where Ethereals are initially bred, Ethereal Island is the only place where they can produce shards -- a special currency used for Ethereal purchases, Tribal Island feeding, and various endgame activities.

### Ethereal Island Overview

| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| Unlock level | 12 |
| Cost to unlock | 500,000 coins |
| Island theme | Dark, cosmic, ghostly |
| Monster types | Ethereal monsters only (5 main + Rare variants) |
| Currency produced | Shards (instead of coins) |
| Bed capacity | Starts at 10, upgradeable |
| Song genre | Ambient, ethereal, choral |
| Teleport mechanic | Must teleport Ethereals from Natural Islands |

## The Five Core Ethereals

There are five main Ethereal monsters, one associated with each natural element. Each is bred on a specific Natural Island and then teleported to Ethereal Island.

### Ethereal Monster Overview

| Ethereal | Element | Bred On | Teleport Cost (Shards) | Breed Time | Base Shard Rate (Lv10) |
|----------|---------|---------|----------------------|-----------|----------------------|
| Ghazt | Plant (Plasma) | Plant Island | 500 | 36 hours | 15/h |
| Grumpyre | Cold (Shadow) | Cold Island | 750 | 36 hours | 18/h |
| Reebro | Air (Nexus) | Air Island | 1,000 | 36 hours | 20/h |
| Jeeode | Water (Crystal) | Water Island | 1,500 | 36 hours | 22/h |
| Humbug | Earth (Mech) | Earth Island | 2,000 | 36 hours | 25/h |

## Breeding Ethereal Monsters

### The Ethereal Breeding Formula

All Ethereals follow the same breeding formula:
- **Required:** The island's 4-element Natural monster + any 3-element monster

| Ethereal | Required 4-Element Monster | 3-Element Partner | Island |
|----------|---------------------------|-------------------|--------|
| Ghazt | Entbrat | Bowgart, Clamble, T-Rox, PomPom, Pango, Reedling, or Thumpies | Plant |
| Grumpyre | Deedge | Bowgart, Clamble, Thumpies, Pango, Stogg, or Sporerow | Cold |
| Reebro | Riff | PomPom, Reedling, Pango, Spunge, T-Rox, or Thumpies | Air |
| Jeeode | Shellbeat | Pango, Bowgart, Oaktopus, Spunge, or Reedling | Water |
| Humbug | Quarrister | Clamble, T-Rox, Dandidoo, Thumpies, or Stogg | Earth |

### Detailed Breeding Guide: Ghazt (Plant Island)

Ghazt is the first Ethereal most players attempt because Plant Island is the earliest island you develop.

| Detail | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Breeding combo | Entbrat + any 3-element monster |
| Best 3-element partner | Bowgart (12h breed time -- fastest failure recovery) |
| Breed time (success) | 36 hours |
| Enhanced breed time | 18 hours |
| Failure outcome | Entbrat (24h) or the 3-element partner (12h) |
| Success rate (no torches) | ~1% |
| Success rate (all torches lit) | ~5-8% |
| Success rate (all torches + enhanced) | ~10-15% |

**Ghazt Breeding Strategy:**

| Step | Action | Detail |
|------|--------|--------|
| 1 | Get an Entbrat | Breed T-Rox + Oaktopus on Plant Island |
| 2 | Get a Bowgart | Breed Oaktopus + Mammott on Plant Island |
| 3 | Level both to 4 | No benefit to going higher |
| 4 | Light all Plant Island Wishing Torches | Critical for success rate |
| 5 | Breed Entbrat + Bowgart | 24h or 36h outcome |
| 6 | If 24h = Entbrat (failure), retry | Re-breed with new Entbrat |
| 7 | If 36h = Ghazt (success!) | Hatch and prepare for teleport |

**Expected attempts:** 10-25 for most players

## Teleportation System

Once you successfully breed an Ethereal and hatch it, you must teleport it to Ethereal Island. Teleportation costs shards and removes the monster from the Natural Island permanently.

### Teleportation Costs and ROI

| Ethereal | Teleport Cost (Shards) | Shards/Hour at Lv10 | Shards/Hour at Lv15 | ROI Time (Lv10) |
|----------|----------------------|---------------------|---------------------|-----------------|
| Ghazt | 500 | 15 | 38 | 33 hours |
| Grumpyre | 750 | 18 | 45 | 42 hours |
| Reebro | 1,000 | 20 | 50 | 50 hours |
| Jeeode | 1,500 | 22 | 55 | 68 hours |
| Humbug | 2,000 | 25 | 63 | 80 hours |

### Teleportation Rules

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| One-way trip | Teleported Ethereals cannot return to Natural Islands |
| Shard cost | Must have enough shards to teleport |
| Level requirement | Monster must be at least Level 10 to teleport (some exceptions) |
| Multiple copies | You can breed and teleport multiple copies of the same Ethereal |
| Rare Ethereals | Rare Ethereals can also be teleported |
| Wublin usage | Ethereal eggs CANNOT be zapped to Wublins |
| Ethereal Island placement | Teleported Ethereals appear on Ethereal Island automatically |

### Teleportation Preparation

Before teleporting an Ethereal, consider:

| Factor | Recommendation |
|--------|---------------|
| Level the Ethereal | Level to at least 10 before teleporting for immediate shard production |
| Save shards | Have 2x the teleport cost in shards before attempting (safety buffer) |
| Ethereal Island beds | Ensure you have enough open beds on Ethereal Island |
| Breeding completion | Make sure you do not need the Natural Island copy for breeding |

## Common Ethereal Island Mistakes

### Mistake 1: Trying Ghazt Too Early

**Issue:** Attempting Ghazt breeding before having a stable Entbrat supply.

**Fix:** Breed 3-4 Entbrats first and level them for coin production. A stable coin income funds the repeated breeding attempts.

### Mistake 2: Not Saving Enough Shards

**Issue:** Breeding Ghazt successfully but not having 500 shards to teleport it.

**Fix:** Save shards before starting Ethereal breeding. Target having 2x the teleport cost saved before your first attempt.

### Mistake 3: Teleporting Low-Level Ethereals

**Issue:** Teleporting a Level 1 Ghazt to Ethereal Island.

**Fix:** Level the Ethereal to at least Level 10 on the Natural Island before teleporting. Higher level = more immediate shard production. You cannot level it on Ethereal Island without treats from Bakeries.

### Mistake 4: Ignoring Natural Island Breeding

**Issue:** Focusing only on Ethereal Island and neglecting Natural Island development.

**Fix:** Natural Islands fund your Ethereal breeding attempts. Keep breeding coin-producing monsters alongside Ethereal attempts.

### Mistake 5: Giving Up After a Few Attempts

**Issue:** Attempting Ghazt 5 times, failing, and giving up.

**Fix:** Ethereal breeding is a marathon. Plan for 10-30 attempts per Ethereal. Each failure produces a useful 4-element or 3-element monster for coins or Wublin zapping.

## Conclusion

Ethereal Island is a critical component of the mid-to-late game MSM experience. The five core Ethereals -- Ghazt, Grumpyre, Reebro, Jeeode, and Humbug -- offer the highest shard production in the game, making them essential for players who want to participate in Tribal Island, purchase Ethereal market items, and progress through the shard economy.

The path to a fully operational Ethereal Island is long, starting with countless Ghazt breeding attempts on Plant Island. Be patient, light those Wishing Torches, and celebrate each 36-hour breed time as a potential Ethereal success. Once established, Ethereal Island becomes a passive shard engine that fuels your endgame progression.
