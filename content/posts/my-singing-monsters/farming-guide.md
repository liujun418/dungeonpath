## Resource Overview

MSM has four primary resources that players must manage. Each requires different farming strategies.

| Resource | Primary Uses | Early Game Source | Endgame Source |
|----------|-------------|-------------------|----------------|
| Coins | Buying monsters, upgrades, decorations | Monster production | High-level monsters, Wublins |
| Diamonds | Speed-ups, enhanced breeding, rare items | Leveling up, daily rewards | Wublins, Colossingum, Wishing Torches |
| Shards | Ethereal monsters, Tribal Island feeding | Ethereal monsters | Wublins, Ethereal Island, Tribal Island |
| Treats | Leveling up monsters | Bakeries | Multiple Bakeries, Wublins, events |

## Coin Farming

Coins are the most important early-game resource and remain relevant throughout your MSM journey.

### How Coin Production Works

Every monster produces coins based on its level, happiness, and base coin rate. Coins accumulate in the monster's "bank" and can be collected by tapping the monster.

| Factor | Effect on Coin Production |
|--------|--------------------------|
| Monster Level | Higher level = more coins per minute (see table below) |
| Monster Rarity | Epic > Rare > Common |
| Monster Element Count | 4-element > 3-element > 2-element > 1-element (generally) |
| Happiness | 25% bonus when all monsters on island are 100% happy |
| Wishing Torch boost | No direct coin boost, but more monsters = more coins |
| Island structure | Each island generates coins independently |

### Island Happiness Optimization

A 100% happy island produces 25% more coins. Happiness is affected by:

| Factor | Effect | Cost |
|--------|--------|------|
| Decorations matching monster element | +10-25% per monster | Coins |
| Island skin (if available) | +25% all monsters | Diamonds or event currency |
| Monsters of same element nearby | +5-10% | Free (placement) |
| Wishing Torches lit | +5-10% per torch | Friend help or diamonds |

## Diamond Farming

Diamonds are the premium currency and the hardest resource to farm as a free-to-play player.

### Diamond Income Sources

| Source | Average Daily Income | Effort Required | Notes |
|--------|---------------------|-----------------|-------|
| Leveling up | 2-5 (one-time) | Passive | One-time, not recurring |
| Wublin Island | 5-20 | Medium (setup) | Best recurring source |
| Colossingum | 2-5 | Low (daily battles) | Free diamonds daily |
| Wishing Torches | 1-3 | Low (friend lighting) | Passive with friends |
| Daily login | 1-2 | Minimal | Log in every day |
| Puzzles / mini-games | 1-5 | Low | Play daily |
| Tribal Island rewards | 5-10 weekly | Medium | Join active tribe |
| Spin wheel | 0-5 (variable) | Low | Daily free spin |
| Achievements | 10-50 (one-time) | Medium | One-time completion |
| **Total (free-to-play)** | **15-30 per day** | **Consistent play** | |

| Mixed (5 Astropod + 5 Thwok + 5 Zynth) | 3-4 weeks | 15-25 | Optimal diamond setup |

|------|---------------------|-------------------|

#### Method 3: Wishing Torch Farming

## Treat Farming

Treats are required to level up monsters, and treat demand grows exponentially with each level.

### Bakery Overview

| Bakery Type | Treat Produced | Cost to Build | Production Time | Treat Yield |
|-------------|---------------|--------------|-----------------|-------------|
| Basic Bakery | Cookie | 10,000 coins | 30 minutes | 25 treats |
| Enhanced Bakery | Cookie | 25,000 coins | 15 minutes | 25 treats |
| VIP Bakery | Candy | 100,000 coins | 2 hours | 100 treats |
| Super Bakery | Cake | 500,000 coins | 8 hours | 500 treats |
| Mega Bakery | Cake | 2,000,000 coins | 4 hours | 600 treats |

### Treat Production Efficiency

| Treat Type | Base Yield | Cost to Produce (Coins) | Treats per Hour (per Bakery) | Coins per Treat |
|------------|-----------|----------------------|-----------------------------|-----------------|
| Cookie | 25 | 2,000 | 50 | 80 |
| Candy | 100 | 15,000 | 50 | 150 |
| Cake | 500 | 100,000 | 62.5 | 200 |

### Optimal Bakery Strategy

| Game Stage | Recommended Setup | Notes |
|------------|------------------|-------|
| Early (Levels 1-10) | 1-2 Basic Bakeries | Enough for early leveling |
| Mid (Levels 10-20) | 2 Enhanced + 1 VIP Bakery | Switch to Candy for efficiency |
| Late (Levels 20-30) | 3-4 VIP + 1 Super Bakery | Mass produce Cake |
| Endgame (Levels 30+) | 4-5 Mega Bakeries | Maximum treat throughput |

### Treat Farming Tips

1. **Upgrade Bakeries early** -- Even one upgrade dramatically improves treat output
2. **Run Bakeries continuously** -- Always have treats cooking, even if you don't need them yet
3. **Switch to Candy at mid-game** -- Better treats-per-coin ratio than Cookies
4. **Cake for high-level pushes** -- Only use Cake when pushing monsters past Level 15
5. **Wublin treats supplement** -- Screemu and Bona-Petite provide free treats daily

## Advanced Efficiency Techniques

### The Double-Collect Method

Because monsters store coins for a period before maxing out, you can optimize collection timing:

| Collection Frequency | Efficiency | Note |
|--------------------|-----------|------|
| Every 4-6 hours | ~95% of max | Good for active players |
| Every 8-12 hours | ~85% of max | Standard for casual players |
| Every 24 hours | ~60% of max | Monsters hit cap and stop producing |
| Multiple times daily + Wublins | ~100% | Optimal but time-intensive |

### Bed Efficiency Optimization

Each bed should generate maximum value. Here is the ranking:

| Monster | Beds Used | Coins/Day at Lv10 | Coins per Bed per Day |
|---------|-----------|-------------------|----------------------|
| Entbrat | 4 | 288,000 | 72,000 |
| Deedge | 4 | 259,200 | 64,800 |
| Riff | 4 | 240,000 | 60,000 |
| T-Rox | 3 | 151,200 | 50,400 |
| Bowgart | 3 | 144,000 | 48,000 |
| Furcorn | 2 | 86,400 | 43,200 |
| Noggin | 1 | 14,400 | 14,400 |

**Conclusion:** 4-element monsters are the most bed-efficient coin producers. Replace single-element monsters with 4-element monsters as soon as you have enough beds.

### Treat-to-Coin Conversion

Treats are ultimately an investment that pays back through higher-level monsters producing more coins.

| Level Push | Treat Investment | Coin Production Increase | ROI Time |
|-----------|-----------------|------------------------|----------|

| Lv10 to Lv15 | 12,000 treats | 2.3x Lv10 rate | ~10 days |

| Lv20+ | 200K+ treats | Marginal | Only for endgame |

## Conclusion

Resource farming in My Singing Monsters follows a clear progression: coins first, then diamonds, then shards, with treats as the bottleneck throughout. The early game is about establishing coin income through Entbrats and 3-element monsters. The mid game shifts to Wublin Island for passive diamond income. The endgame optimizes every island for maximum resource production.

Remember these key principles:
1. **4-element monsters are the most efficient** coin producers per bed
2. **Wublin Island is the best diamond source** for free-to-play players
3. **Treats are the real bottleneck** -- upgrade Bakeries early and run them constantly
4. **Level monsters evenly** before pushing individual monsters very high
5. **Collect frequently** -- idle monsters stop producing when their bank is full
