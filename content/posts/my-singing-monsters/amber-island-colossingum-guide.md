## Amber Island: The Vessel System

Amber Island is MSM's endgame resource island. Instead of breeding, you fill Vessels — containers that require specific monster eggs from your other islands. Each vessel becomes a Fire-element monster that sings and produces Relics.

### How Vessels Work

1. Buy a vessel from the Amber Island Market (costs Coins, 100K-2M depending on monster rarity)
2. The vessel shows a list of required monsters spread across your islands
3. Breed or teleport those specific monsters to the correct islands
4. "Zap" eggs from each monster to fill vessel slots
5. When all slots are filled, the vessel hatches into that monster
6. The monster now lives on Amber Island and generates Relics

### Egg Zapping Rules

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| One egg per monster per vessel | Each monster donates only once to each vessel |
| Monster must be placed | The required monster must exist on the specified island |
| Breeding time varies | Higher-level monsters produce zappable eggs faster |
| Eggs are free | Zapping costs nothing — you just need the monster |
| Cross-island tracking | The vessel UI shows which monsters you still need and where they go |

### Recommended Vessel Order

| Priority | Monster | Cost (Coins) | Required Islands | Difficulty |
|----------|---------|-------------|-----------------|------------|
| **1** | Kayna | 500K | Plant, Cold, Air | Easy |
| **2** | Stogg | 750K | Plant, Cold, Air, Water | Easy |
| **3** | Ziggurab | 1M | Plant, Cold, Air, Water | Medium |
| **4** | Tiawa | 1.2M | Plant, Cold, Air, Water, Earth | Medium |
| **5** | Flum Ox | 1.5M | All 5 Natural islands | Hard |
| 6+ | Remaining Fire monsters | 1.5-2M each | Various | Hard |

Start with Kayna. Its 6 required monsters (Potbelly, Toe Jammer, Mammott on 3 islands) should already exist on your islands by the time you unlock Amber Island.

### Relic Production & Spending

| Relic Source | Relics/Day at Level 10 | Relics/Day at Level 20 |
|-------------|----------------------|----------------------|
| Single-element Fire (Kayna) | 3-5 | 6-9 |
| Double-element Fire (Stogg, Ziggurab) | 5-8 | 10-14 |
| Triple-element Fire (Tiawa, Flum Ox) | 8-12 | 15-20 |

**What to spend Relics on:**
- More vessels (cycles back into more Relic production)
- Amber Island decorations (boost monster happiness → more Relics)
- Rare Wubbox on Amber Island (endgame flex, costs 50,000+ Relics)

## Colossingum: Tournament Battling System

The Colossingum is a completely separate combat system where your monsters battle in turn-based tournaments. Progress here doesn't affect your singing islands, and singing island monsters don't automatically fight — you must train them in the Colossingum separately.

### Training Basics

| Training Method | Cost | Effect |
|----------------|------|--------|
| Battle participation | Free (daily limit: 3) | Primary XP source |
| Training dummy | Medals | Limited XP per day |
| Costume equipping | Medals (to buy costumes) | Stat bonuses in battle |
| Level-up | Automatic with enough XP | Unlocks new battle moves |

### Battle Mechanics

Colossingum battles are turn-based with 3-5 monsters per side. Each monster has:
- **HP** — Total health pool (determined by species + level)
- **Power** — Base damage stat
- **Speed** — Determines turn order
- **Element** — Determines type matchups (Fire > Plant > Water > Fire, etc.)
- **Special Move** — Unlocked at level 10, high damage with cooldown

### Type Advantage Chart

| Attacking Element | Strong Against | Weak Against |
|------------------|----------------|-------------|
| Fire | Plant, Ice | Water, Earth |
| Water | Fire, Earth | Plant, Ice |
| Plant | Water, Earth | Fire, Ice |
| Ice | Plant, Water | Fire, Earth |
| Earth | Fire, Ice | Water, Plant |

### Best Colossingum Monsters (Current Meta)

| Monster | Element | Base Power | Speed | Role |
|---------|---------|-----------|-------|------|
| Entbrat | Plant | 85 | 60 | Tank/DPS hybrid |
| Deedge | Ice | 80 | 75 | Fast sweeper |
| Riff | Air | 75 | 90 | Fastest attacker |
| Shellbeat | Water | 90 | 50 | Slow but hits hardest |
| Quarrister | Earth | 82 | 65 | Balanced stats |

A balanced team usually has 2 DPS (high Power), 1 tank (high HP), and 1 speed monster (to get first turn).

## Daily Routine for Both Systems

| Task | Time | Priority |
|------|------|----------|
| Collect Relics from Amber Island | 30 seconds | Daily — Relics accumulate slowly |
| Zap eggs for active vessels | 2-5 minutes | Check every 2-3 days |
| Colossingum daily battles (×3) | 5-10 minutes | Daily for XP |
| Check vessel progress | 1 minute | Once per week is fine |
| Colossingum tournament (if active) | 15-20 minutes | Weekly during season |

Neither system requires daily grinding. Amber Island is a slow-burn system measured in weeks per vessel, and Colossingum tournaments run on seasonal schedules.
