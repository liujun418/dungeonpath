## What Is Legendary Island?

Legendary Island is one of the most challenging islands in My Singing Monsters, featuring exclusive legendary-tier monsters that cannot be found on any other island. These monsters contribute unique instrumental and vocal tracks to the island's song, and collecting them all unlocks the full musical composition.

The island was introduced as part of MSM's ongoing content expansion and represents the pinnacle of breeding complexity. Legendary monsters require rare breeding combinations, significant resource investment, and patience.

### Island Unlock Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Minimum Player Level** | Level 15 |
| **Prerequisite Islands** | At least 3 other islands built and populated |
| **Diamond Cost** | 500–1,000 Diamonds (varies by game version) |
| **Structure Cost** | Additional buildings cost 200–500 Diamonds each |

For players working toward the level requirement, our [Farming Guide](/posts/my-singing-monsters/farming-guide/) covers efficient coin and food production strategies.

## Breeding Strategy for Legendary Island

### The Entbrat Strategy

Entbrat is the cornerstone of legendary monster breeding. It carries all four natural elements (Plant, Cold, Air, Water), making it the universal breeding partner for ethereal monsters.

| Step | Action | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| 1 | **Breed Entbrat** | T-Rox + Potbelly = Entbrat (4-element monster) |
| 2 | **Level Entbrat to 15** | Higher level = slightly better breeding odds |
| 3 | **Pair with target parent** | E.g., Entbrat + T-Rox for Ghazt |
| 4 | **Use breeding structure** | The Enhanced Breeding Structure increases odds by 5–10% |
| 5 | **Repeat** | Expect 50–100+ attempts for ethereal monsters |

### Breeding Structure Options

| Structure | Cost | Bonus | Worth It? |
|-----------|------|-------|-----------|
| **Basic Breeding Structure** | Free (default) | No bonus | Starting point only |
| **Enhanced Breeding Structure** | 150 Diamonds | +5% legendary chance | Yes — essential for serious breeding |
| **Super Breeding Structure** | 500 Diamonds | +10% legendary chance | Yes — best long-term investment |
| **Ultra Breeding Structure** | 1,000 Diamonds | +15% legendary chance | Situational — only for dedicated collectors |

## Step-by-Step Walkthrough

### Phase 1: Unlock Legendary Island (Days 1–3)

1. **Reach Player Level 15** — Focus on completing objectives and breeding on your existing islands
2. **Build 3 prerequisite islands** — Ensure you have at least Plant Island, Cold Island, and Air Island populated
3. **Accumulate 500–1,000 Diamonds** — Save from daily rewards, achievements, and level-up bonuses
4. **Purchase Legendary Island** — Tap the island menu and select Legendary Island

### Phase 2: Build Core Infrastructure (Days 3–7)

1. **Place the Enhanced Breeding Structure** (150 Diamonds) — This is your first purchase
2. **Build 2–3 food structures** — You will need significant food for legendary breeding
3. **Place decorations** — Maximize happiness from day one
4. **Breed your first Entbrat** — If you do not already have one at level 15

### Phase 3: Begin Legendary Breeding (Days 7–30)

1. **Start with Ghazt** — It is the most commonly obtained legendary and serves as proof of concept
2. **Breed 50+ attempts** — Expect failure; the 1–3% rate means you will fail many times
3. **Once Ghazt succeeds, target the next ethereal** — Move through Grumpyre, Reebro, Jeeode, Humbug
4. **Level each legendary to 15** — Maximizes coin production and happiness bonus

### Phase 4: Complete the Collection (Days 30–90)

1. **Target Karbunkle and Whisp** — These require 3-element combinations and have slightly better rates
2. **Work on Epic variants** — These are the rarest and may take months of breeding
3. **Complete the song** — Place all monsters and enjoy the full Legendary Island composition
4. **Claim all milestone rewards** — Ensure you collect all diamond and item rewards at each milestone

## Tips and Tricks

### Breeding Efficiency Tips

| Tip | Effect |
|-----|--------|
| **Breed during double-breeding events** | Success rate doubles (2–6% instead of 1–3%) |
| **Use the Enhanced or Super Breeding Structure** | +5% to +10% base chance |
| **Clear failed breeding results quickly** | Maximizes breeding attempts per day |
| **Breed at night** | Some players report slightly better rates; may be placebo but worth trying |
| **Keep Entbrat at level 15+** | Higher level parents may have marginally better odds |

### Event-Based Opportunities

| Event | Benefit | Frequency |
|-------|---------|-----------|
| **Double Breeding Event** | 2x legendary breeding success rate | Monthly |
| **Breeding Bonanza** | Specific legendary rates increased to 5–10% | Quarterly |
| **Login Rewards** | Free diamonds for breeding structures | Daily (accumulates) |
| **Seasonal Events** | Exclusive legendary variants | Seasonal (4x per year) |

### Common Breeding Pitfalls

| Pitfall | Result | Avoidance |
|---------|--------|-----------|
| **Using low-level parents** | Wasted attempts, lower success rate | Always level parents to 15+ |
| **Wrong breeding combination** | Produces common monsters instead of legendary | Double-check combinations before breeding |
| **Breeding without Enhanced Structure** | Significantly lower success rate | Invest in the Enhanced Breeding Structure first |
| **Giving up too early** | Legendary rates are 1–3%; 50+ attempts is normal | Expect and accept the grind |
| **Not managing food economy** | Running out of food for breeding attempts | Maintain 100,000+ food reserves |
