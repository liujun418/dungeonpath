## What Is Shugabush Island?

Shugabush Island is a special Legendary Monster island in My Singing Monsters. Unlike Natural Islands, Shugabush Island features a unique family of monsters called the "Shugafamily," each playing a rock band-themed instrument. The island's song is an original rock composition that grows more complex as you add more Shugafamily members.

Shugabush Island is considered one of the most rewarding special islands because the Shugafamily monsters have excellent coin production rates and the island serves as a permanent home for Legendary monsters that cannot be placed elsewhere.

### Shugabush Island Overview

| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| Island type | Legendary Monster Island |
| Number of monsters | 8 (Shugabush + 7 Shugafamily members) |
| Monster elements | All 5 natural elements (varies by member) |
| Unlock method | Breed Shugabush on Plant Island |
| Song genre | Rock / Band music |
| Coin production | Very high (comparable to 4-element monsters) |
| Beds | 10 (base, upgradeable via Castle) |

## How to Unlock Shugabush Island

Unlike most islands that unlock by reaching a certain player level, Shugabush Island unlocks by successfully breeding Shugabush himself.

### Step-by-Step Unlock Process

| Step | Action | Detail |
|------|--------|--------|
| 1 | Reach Level 9 | Minimum level requirement |
| 2 | Bowgart | Breed Bowgart on Plant Island (Oaktopus + Mammott, 12 hours) |
| 3 | Clamble | Breed Clamble on Plant Island (Drumpler + Mammott, 12 hours) |
| 4 | Light Plant Island Wishing Torches | Maximize breeding odds |
| 5 | Breed Bowgart + Clamble | The specific combo for Shugabush |
| 6 | Wait for 24 hours | Shugabush breed time |
| 7 | Hatch Shugabush | Place on Plant Island |
| 8 | Shugabush Island unlocks | Automatically available after placing Shugabush |

### Shugabush Breeding Details

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Required monsters | Bowgart + Clamble |
| Island | Plant Island |
| Breed time | 24 hours |
| Enhanced breed time | 12 hours |
| Success rate | ~10-15% with lit torches |
| Failure outcomes | Bowgart (12h) or Clamble (12h) |
| Wishing Torch boost | Significant (highly recommended) |
| Level requirement | Level 9+ |

### Shugabush Breeding Strategy

**Preparation:**
1. Breed Bowgart (Plant + Water + Cold) -- Oaktopus + Mammott
2. Breed Clamble (Plant + Earth + Cold) -- Drumpler + Mammott
3. Ensure both are at least Level 4 (no benefit to going higher)
4. Have all Plant Island Wishing Torches lit by friends
5. Have 10-25 diamonds ready for enhanced breeding if needed

**During breeding:**
1. Place Bowgart and Clamble in Breeding Structure
2. Wait 24 hours (or 12 with enhanced breeding)
3. If you get a 24-hour breed time -- congratulations, it might be Shugabush!
4. If you get 12 hours -- it is Bowgart or Clamble, try again
5. Repeat until successful

**Expected attempts:** 3-10 tries for most players. Some players report getting it on the first try; others may need 20+ attempts.

Shugabush Island features an original rock song composed specifically for MSM. Each Shugafamily member adds a distinct instrumental track.

### Song Structure

| Monster | Instrument | When Added | Musical Role |
|---------|-----------|------------|--------------|

| (Additional) | Percussion | 6th+ monster | Texture / fills |

### Song Completion

The Shugabush Island song reaches its full arrangement when all Shugafamily members are present and awake on the island. Unlike Natural Islands where the song loops, Shugabush Island plays a composed rock track with a defined structure (verse, chorus, bridge, and solo sections).

**Tips for enjoying the song:**
- Place monsters close to the center of the island for balanced audio
- Each monster contributes equally regardless of level
- The song plays continuously when the game is open
- Night mode on the island changes the visual presentation but not the music

## Common Shugabush Island Mistakes

### Mistake 1: Teleporting Shugabush Too Early

**Issue:** Teleporting Shugabush to another island before breeding all Shugafamily members.

**Fix:** Breed all desired Shugafamily members from your first Shugabush before teleporting it anywhere.

### Mistake 2: Not Breeding All Shugafamily Members

**Issue:** Stopping after just Shugabush.

**Fix:** Each Shugafamily member adds to the island song AND increases coin production. Breed them all for the complete experience and maximum income.

### Mistake 3: Forgetting the Breed Time

**Issue:** Getting excited about a 24+ hour breed time and assuming it is a Shugafamily member.

**Fix:** Shugabush breeds are 24-30 hours. Deedge and Quarrister are also 24 hours. Check the exact time to confirm which monster you got.

| Breed Time | Monster (Shugabush breeding context) |
|-----------|--------------------------------------|
| 12 hours | Bowgart, Clamble (failure for Shugabush) |
| 24 hours | Shugabush (success) OR Deedge/Quarrister (failure for Shugafamily) |
| 28 hours | Shugabeats or Shugabass |
| 30 hours | Shugitar or Shugajo |

### Mistake 4: Neglecting Castle Upgrades

**Issue:** Keeping the base 10-bed Castle.

**Fix:** Upgrade the Shugabush Island Castle to at least Level 3 (22 beds) to fit the full Shugafamily plus extra copies of high earners.

## Conclusion

Shugabush Island is one of the most rewarding special islands in My Singing Monsters, offering a unique rock song, high coin production from Shugafamily members, and a fun collection goal. The journey starts with breeding Shugabush on Plant Island (Bowgart + Clamble), then expands to breeding each Shugafamily member on their respective Natural Islands.

The Shugafamily members are excellent coin producers that rival 4-element natural monsters, making them valuable additions to any player's resource generation strategy. The complete island song is a satisfying reward for collectors, and the high bed efficiency of Shugafamily monsters makes them worth the breeding effort.
