## What Is the Venue Takeover?

The Venue Takeover is Fisch's May 2026 limited-time event that transforms the hidden Underground Music Venue into a hub of 8 unique NPCs, each offering a questline that rewards an exclusive **developer rod**. These rods feature custom passives, unique mechanics (dual-bar reeling, kinetic energy meters, day/night mode switching), and stats that rival or exceed the best permanent rods in the game.

If you're new to Fisch, start with our [Fisch Beginner Guide](/posts/fisch/beginner-guide/) to understand rod mechanics, enchantments, and the world map before tackling the Venue.

---

## How to Reach the Underground Music Venue

The journey to the Venue requires several steps and specific prerequisite items:

| Step | Location | Action |
|------|----------|--------|
| 1 | **Vertigo** | Jump into a whirlpool with a wispy light to enter Vertigo |
| 2 | **The Depths** | Inside Vertigo's central pit, fish for a **Depths Key**. Use it on the large door to enter The Depths |
| 3 | **Crystal Cove** | From The Depths, head through the archway in the castle wall (coords: ~1302, -701, 1604) |
| 4 | **Pedestal** | Place a **Chaotic Enchant Relic** on the empty pedestal in Crystal Cove |
| 5 | **Trapdoor** | A hatch opens at coordinates ~1350, -604, 2330. Drop down to enter the **Underground Music Venue** |

**Required items before you go:** Depths Key, Chaotic Enchant Relic.

## All 8 NPCs & Developer Rod Questlines

### Recommended Completion Order

The questlines can be progressed simultaneously, but some rods make others easier. Follow this priority:

```
1. Soul Reaper Rod (strongest rod, makes everything else faster)
2. Steampunk Rod (mid-game utility)
3. Castbound Rod (Shimmer access)
4. Wingkeeper Rod (day/night versatility)
5. Acid Grinder Rod (niche toxic setups)
6. Part Rod (quick bonus, ~10 minutes)
7. Remembrance Rod (save for LAST — butterfly grind is the longest)
8. Lullaby Rod (community still discovering)
```

---

### 1. The Reaper → Soul Reaper Rod (Top Priority)

The Soul Reaper Rod is the strongest rod in the Venue and should be your first target. Its stats and passives make every subsequent questline significantly easier.

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| Lure Speed | 999.99% |
| Luck | 288.88% |
| Control | 0.177 |
| Resilience | 16.66% |
| Max KG | Infinite |

**Passives:**
- **Paranormal Mutation (5x)** — caught fish have a high chance of receiving the Paranormal mutation, multiplying their sell value by 5x
- **+50% Weight Bonus** — all fish weigh 50% more
- **Tombstone Bonus Fish** — occasionally summons an extra fish alongside your catch
- **Spirit Duplicate** — every 3-6 catches, duplicates the fish for double rewards

**Questline Steps:**

| Step | Requirement | How To |
|------|-------------|--------|
| 1 | Shiny + Sparkling + Darkened Rubber Duck | Fish at various locations; Rubber Duck appears as a junk item. Apply mutations via enchantments and totems |
| 2 | Spirit Ancient Depth Serpent | Fish in The Depths using the **Spirit Rod** during Absolute Darkness |
| 3 | Soul-Touched Log | Catch using **Hades Soul Scythe** rod with garbage bait |
| 4 | Distraught Scrap Metal | Catch using the **Dream Breaker Rod** |
| 5 | Phantom String | Catch using the **Eidolon Rod** in Desolate Deep |
| 6 | Deliver soul pieces | Visit 3 Ghost NPCs: **Rosalind** (Volcano), **Keeper** (Altar), **Living Garden** |

### 2. Rivet → Steampunk Rod

A mechanically-themed rod with a unique kinetic energy system. Solid mid-game stats with a powerful activated ability.

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| Lure Speed | 75% |
| Luck | 90% |
| Resilience | 30% |

**Passive — Kinetic Engine:** A meter builds with each successful catch. When activated, triggers:
- 100% Clockwork mutation rate
- +50% progress speed
- +30% resilience
- Duration: 30 seconds

**Questline Steps:**

| Step | Requirement |
|------|-------------|
| 1 | Collect **Rusty Bolt**, **Broken Gear**, **Scrap Metal** (junk items from fishing) |
| 2 | Upgrade each item to **Purified**, **Midas**, or **Crystallized** variants |
| 3 | Travel **75,000 studs** on the bike (use a fast bike + long straight route) |
| 4 | Catch **Electric Kerauno Wyrm** (Zeus area, Atlantis) |

For fishing location details, see our [Fisch Fish Location Map](/posts/fisch/fish-location-map/).

### 3. The Guide → Castbound Rod

A rod designed around precision and accuracy. The only rod capable of fishing in the Shimmer area.

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| Lure Speed | 65% |
| Luck | 110% |
| Control | 0.22 |
| Resilience | 25% |

**Passive — Blade Slash:** Creates slashing arcs during the fishing minigame. Higher accuracy fills the slash meter faster, dealing damage to fish progress.

**Questline Steps:**

| Step | Requirement |
|------|-------------|
| 1 | Catch **10 Shiny Flying Fish** |
| 2 | Perform **150 Perfect Catches** in **Shimmer** (inside Vertigo cave) |
| 3 | Submit rods: Arctic, Carbon, Ethereal Prison, Flimsy, Heaven's, Zeus, and 9 more |

### 4. Seraphel → Wingkeeper Rod

A dual-mode rod that changes behavior based on the day/night cycle — one of the most versatile rods in the event.

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| Lure Speed | 111% |
| Luck | 222% |
| Progress Speed | +66% |
| Resilience | 33% |

**Passive — Day/Night Duality:**
- **Daytime:** Fast progress speed + **Sanctified** mutation (3.5x value)
- **Nighttime:** Slower progress speed + **Veilfallen** mutation (4.5x value)

**Questline Steps:**

| Step | Requirement | Reward |
|------|-------------|--------|
| 1 | Catch **Heavenly Handfish** | 2,500 coins |
| 2 | Catch **Celestial Flying Fish** | Guitar Pick bobber |
| 3 | Catch **Shiny Blessed Harmonic Dove** | Ascendance Title |
| 4 | Catch **Sparkling/Shiny Nova Wyvern** | **Wingkeeper Rod** |

### 5. Axel → Acid Grinder Rod

A specialized rod for toxic/acidic environments. Starts in a restricted state and must be fully unlocked.

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| Lure Speed | 80% |
| Luck | 135% |
| Resilience | 28% |

**Passive — Acid Burn:** Applies Acidic mutation (2x value) to all catches. Stacking effect: consecutive catches in Brine Pool increase mutation multiplier up to 4x.

**Questline Steps:**

| Step | Requirement |
|------|-------------|
| 1 | Catch **Tainted Brine Phantom** (Bloomspire area, toxin rod) |
| 2 | Catch **Brine Sovereign** (only spawns during Brine Storm weather) |
| 3 | Receive restricted rod → catch **Acidic Caustic Starwyrm** to fully unlock |

**Note:** The restricted rod can only use specific bait until fully unlocked.

### 6. BasePart → Part Rod

The quickest questline in the Venue — can be completed in about 10 minutes. The rod itself is a novelty item.

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| All Stats | 0 |

**Passive — Part Mutation (0.01x):** Applies the Part mutation to caught fish, reducing their value to near-zero. Used exclusively for collecting Part-variant fish for the bestiary.

**Questline Steps:**

| Step | Requirement |
|------|-------------|
| 1 | Catch **Exploded Tire** (common junk) |
| 2 | Catch **Part Rock** |

### 7. Luneth → Remembrance Rod (Hardest Questline)

The most complex questline in the Venue. Save this for last — the butterfly mutation grind alone can take hours.

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| Lure Speed | 100% |
| Luck | 300% |
| Control | -0.1 |
| Resilience | 20% |
| Max KG | Infinite |

**Passive — Dual Mode Reeling:**
- The reeling bar has **white fish** (progress) and **dark fish** (penalty)
- **Mode 1 (Fluttering):** 50% Fluttering mutation rate (7x value)
- **Mode 2 (Departed):** Different mutation pool with higher rarity weighting
- **Perfection Mode:** Consecutive perfect catches stack +10% progress speed per second

**Questline Steps:**

| Step | Requirement |
|------|-------------|
| 1 | Catch **Forgotten Orca** (Thalassa's Ruin rod, Shark Head bait, hold 25+ seconds without reeling) |
| 2 | Catch **Glowy Penguin** (crab cages + glowworm bait at Snowburrow) |
| 3 | Catch all 11 butterfly mutations at Living Garden |

**The 11 Butterfly Mutations:**

| 2 | Darkened | Depthseeker Rod |
| 3 | Chaotic | Duskwire Rod |
| 4 | Distraught | Dreambreaker Rod |
| 5 | Sparkling | Blessed + Glittered enchant + Sparkling Totem |
| 6 | Plagued | Plaguereaver Rod |
| 7 | Levitas | Oni Rod |
| 8 | Mourned | Tidemourner Rod |

## Quality of Life Changes

The Venue Takeover update also introduced several system improvements:

- **Equipment Bag** — accessible via Settings → Preferences. Stores spare rods, bobbers, and bait without cluttering your main inventory.
- **Quick Access** — bind frequently used items/actions to hotkeys for faster access during fishing.
- **Multiple Quest Tracking** — the Venue NPCs are designed for overlapping progression. Work on 2-3 questlines simultaneously for maximum efficiency.

## FAQ

### How long is the Venue Takeover event available?
The developers have not announced a specific end date, but have stated the Venue NPCs "won't be sticking around long." Based on previous Fisch limited events, expect 2-4 weeks from the May 2 launch — meaning it could end as early as mid-to-late May 2026. Start with the Soul Reaper Rod questline first.

### Can I keep the developer rods after the event ends?
Yes. Once you earn a developer rod by completing its questline, it's permanently added to your inventory. Only the NPCs and their questlines are limited-time.

### Which rod should I get first?
Soul Reaper Rod. Its 999.99% Lure Speed and infinite Max KG make every other questline dramatically faster. The Rod is widely considered one of the best rods ever released in Fisch.
