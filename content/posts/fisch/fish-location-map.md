## 1. Moosewood — Your Starting Dock

**How to unlock:** Spawn point (automatic)
**Progression stage:** Level 1-5
**Recommended rod:** Flimsy Rod → Carbon Rod

### Overview

Moosewood is the tutorial dock where every player begins. It sits at the center of the map and serves as your home base. The fish here are common and low-value, but it's the perfect place to learn the mechanics and earn your first coins.

### Fish Available

| Fish | Rarity | Base Value | Best Bait | Conditions |
|------|--------|-----------|-----------|------------|
| Common Minnow | Common | 2 C$ | Any | Any |
| Small Bass | Common | 3 C$ | Worms | Any |
| Silver Trout | Uncommon | 8 C$ | Minnows | Daytime |
| Golden Carp | Rare | 25 C$ | Truffle Worms | Rain |

### Tips

- Fish here until you can afford the **Carbon Rod** (~2,000 C$)
- Complete **Pierre's tutorial quests** for bonus XP (see our [Fisch Quests and NPCs Guide](/posts/fisch/quests-and-npcs-guide/) for all quest details)
- Talk to **Phineas** near the docks for your first bait quest
- Use the **Fish Merchant** to sell catches and **Appraiser** to appraise them

## 2. Roslit Bay — Your First Real Destination

**How to unlock:** Travel by boat (directly west of Moosewood)
**Progression stage:** Level 5+
**Recommended rod:** Carbon Rod → Steady Rod

### Overview

Roslit Bay is the most important early-game location. It's your first step away from the tutorial zone and opens up a whole new tier of fish. The **Nurse Shark** farming spot here is the go-to money maker for beginners.

### Fish Available

| Fish | Rarity | Base Value | Best Bait | Conditions |
|------|--------|-----------|-----------|------------|
| Angel Fish | Uncommon | 12 C$ | Minnows | Any |
| **Nurse Shark** | Uncommon | 35 C$ | Minnows | Any — **Best money fish** |
| Coral Grouper | Rare | 50 C$ | Night Shrimp | Nighttime |
| Roslit Bass | Rare | 45 C$ | Worms | Rain |
| Pearl Puffer | Very Rare | 80 C$ | Truffle Worms | Clear weather |

### Tips

- **Nurse Shark + Minnows** is the best early money combo
- Travel to Roslit Bay as soon as you can afford the Carbon Rod
- Look for the **Steady Rod** shop on Roslit Island
- Night fishing yields different fish — try **Night Shrimp** bait after dark

## 3. Forsaken Shores — XP Grinding Spot

**How to unlock:** Travel from Roslit Bay
**Progression stage:** Level 10+
**Recommended rod:** Steady Rod → Fortune Rod

### Overview

Forsaken Shores is a great mid-game location for grinding XP. The fish here are slightly more valuable than Roslit Bay, and the variety is wider. It's also a good spot to start hunting for mutations.

### Fish Available

| Fish | Rarity | Base Value | Best Bait | Conditions |
|------|--------|-----------|-----------|------------|
| Shore Crab | Common | 5 C$ | Flakes | Any |
| Red Snapper | Uncommon | 18 C$ | Worms | Any |
| Forsaken Eel | Rare | 55 C$ | Night Shrimp | Nighttime |
| Tide Pool Octopus | Very Rare | 90 C$ | Truffle Worms | Low tide |

### Tips

- Good spot for leveling up — XP per catch is higher than Moosewood
- Use **Fortune Rod** for better mutation chances
- **Truffle Worms** significantly increase rare fish spawns here

## 4. Grand Reef — Mid-Game Money Hub

**How to unlock:** Mid-game progression (complete quests)
**Progression stage:** Level 20+
**Recommended rod:** Fortune Rod → King's Rod

### Overview

Grand Reef is the premier mid-game location for earning coins. The fish here have higher base values, and the mutation rates are slightly boosted. This is where most players make their big coin reserves for endgame rod purchases.

### Fish Available

| Fish | Rarity | Base Value | Best Bait | Conditions |
|------|--------|-----------|-----------|------------|
| Reef Parrotfish | Uncommon | 20 C$ | Minnows | Any |
| Grand Marlin | Rare | 75 C$ | Night Shrimp | Any |
| Coral Crown Fish | Very Rare | 120 C$ | Truffle Worms | Clear weather |
| Reef Shark | Very Rare | 150 C$ | Luminous Larva | Nighttime + Rain |

### Tips

- This is the best spot to farm for the **Rod of the Depths** (750,000 C$)
- **Rain + Nighttime** yields the highest-value fish
- Mutation rates are slightly boosted — keep an eye out for Shiny and Albino variants

## 5. Snowcap Island — Arctic Zone

**How to unlock:** Boat travel (northern region)
**Progression stage:** Level 25+
**Recommended rod:** Arctic Rod

### Overview

Snowcap Island introduces cold-weather fishing with a unique fish roster. The **Arctic Rod** has a passive bonus here, making it the go-to location for cold-specific fish.

### Fish Available

| Fish | Rarity | Base Value | Best Bait | Conditions |
|------|--------|-----------|-----------|------------|
| Ice Minnow | Common | 8 C$ | Flakes | Any |
| Frost Salmon | Uncommon | 30 C$ | Worms | Snow weather |
| Arctic Cod | Rare | 65 C$ | Night Shrimp | Nighttime |
| Glacier Whale Shark | Legendary | 300 C$ | Truffle Worms | Blizzard |

### Tips

- **Arctic Rod** gives a passive bonus in this zone
- Blizzard weather is rare but spawns the **Glacier Whale Shark** (300 C$)
- Use **Rain Totem** or **Tempest Totem** to force snow weather if you have one

## 6. Desolate Deep — Late-Game Danger Zone

**How to unlock:** Late-game progression (complete prerequisite quests)
**Progression stage:** Level 40+
**Recommended rod:** Rod of the Depths → Trident Rod

### Overview

The Desolate Deep is one of the most challenging fishing zones in Fisch. It's an underwater trench with dark, hazardous conditions and some of the rarest fish in the game. The **Trident Rod** requires 100% completion of the Desolate Deep Bestiary to purchase.

### Fish Available

| Fish | Rarity | Base Value | Best Bait | Conditions |
|------|--------|-----------|-----------|------------|
| Deep Sea Angler | Rare | 80 C$ | Luminous Larva | Absolute Darkness |
| Abyssal Squid | Very Rare | 130 C$ | Luminous Larva | Any |
| Void Ray | Legendary | 250 C$ | Truffle Worms | Absolute Darkness |
| Leviathan | Mythical | 500 C$ | Luminous Larva | Storm + Nighttime |

### Tips

- **Absolute Darkness** is a required condition for many fish here — you need a high-luck rod
- **Luminous Larva** is the best bait for deep-sea fish
- Complete the **Desolate Deep Bestiary** (100%) to unlock the Trident Rod
- The **Rod of the Depths** is nearly essential for the fishing minigame here

## 7. Ancient Isle — Endgame Paradise

**How to unlock:** Endgame progression
**Progression stage:** Level 50+
**Recommended rod:** Trident Rod → No-Life Rod → Endgame rods

### Overview

Ancient Isle is the ultimate fishing destination. It contains the rarest fish in the game, including several that are only available here. The fish values are astronomical, and the mutation rates are the highest in the game.

### Fish Available

| Fish | Rarity | Base Value | Best Bait | Conditions |
|------|--------|-----------|-----------|------------|
| Ancient Goldfish | Rare | 100 C$ | Truffle Worms | Any |
| Dragon Eel | Legendary | 350 C$ | Luminous Larva | Nighttime |
| Phoenix Koi | Mythical | 600 C$ | Truffle Worms | Clear weather |
| Primordial Leviathan | Mythical | 1,000 C$ | Luminous Larva | Storm + Night + Eclipse |

### Tips

- **Trident Rod or better** is strongly recommended
- **Phoenix Koi** with a Shiny + Albino mutation can sell for 3,000+ C$
- The **Primordial Leviathan** requires very specific conditions — use totems to set them up
- This is where endgame money-making happens

## 8. Atlantis & Mariana's Veil — Underwater Realm

**How to unlock:** Endgame quest chain
**Progression stage:** Level 60+
**Recommended rod:** Abyssal Specter Rod → Ethereal Prism Rod

### Overview

Atlantis is a hidden underwater realm accessible only through a specific quest chain. Mariana's Veil is the deepest part of this zone, featuring fish that don't exist anywhere else. The **Abyssal Specter Rod** has a 25% chance to apply the Abyssal mutation here.

### Fish Available

| Fish | Rarity | Base Value | Best Bait | Conditions |
|------|--------|-----------|-----------|------------|
| Atlantean Sardine | Uncommon | 25 C$ | Minnows | Any |
| Neptune's Swordfish | Legendary | 400 C$ | Luminous Larva | Any |
| Mariana's Angler | Mythical | 750 C$ | Truffle Worms | Absolute Darkness |
| Abyssal Titan | Mythical | 1,500 C$ | Luminous Larva | Abyssal mutation active |

### Tips

- **Abyssal Specter Rod** (1,000,000 C$) is available at the Ethereal Abyss shop
- The Abyssal mutation passive (25% chance) stacks with the rod's luck
- **Mariana's Angler** in Absolute Darkness is the single highest-value regular fish

## Weather and Time Conditions by Location

Some fish only appear under specific conditions. Here's a quick reference:

| Condition | How to Check | Affected Locations |
|-----------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Daytime** | Look at the sky / in-game clock | All locations |
| **Nighttime** | Dark sky, stars visible | All locations — different fish pool |
| **Rain** | Rain particles, sound | Roslit Bay, Grand Reef, Desolate Deep |
| **Storm** | Lightning, heavy rain | Desolate Deep, Ancient Isle |
| **Clear** | No precipitation, bright sky | Snowcap Island (for Glacier Whale Shark) |
| **Absolute Darkness** | Complete blackness (Desolate Deep) | Desolate Deep, Mariana's Veil |
| **Blizzard** | Heavy snow, whiteout | Snowcap Island only |

> **Pro Tip:** You can force specific weather conditions using **Weather Totems** (see our [Fisch Advanced Fishing Techniques](/posts/fisch/advanced-fishing-techniques/) guide for totem strategies). The **Rain Totem** forces rain, the **Sundial Totem** forces clear weather, and the **Tempest Totem** triggers a storm. Use these to target specific fish.

## FAQ

**Q: How many fishing locations are there in Fisch?**
A: There are **45 permanent locations** and **9 limited-time event locations** as of May 2026.

**Q: What's the best location for beginners?**
A: **Roslit Bay** is the best early-game location. The Nurse Shark farming spot there is the most reliable way to earn coins as a new player.

**Q: How do I unlock new locations?**
A: Use the **boat at Moosewood docks** to travel to nearby islands. Some locations require completing specific quests or reaching certain levels.

**Q: What's the most valuable fish in the game?**
A: The **Primordial Leviathan** (Ancient Isle, 1,000 C$ base) and **Abyssal Titan** (Mariana's Veil, 1,500 C$ base) are the highest-value regular fish. With mutations, a single fish can sell for 10,000+ C$.

**Q: What is "Absolute Darkness"?**
A: Absolute Darkness is a special condition found in the **Desolate Deep** and **Mariana's Veil**. It's a state of complete darkness that certain rare fish require to spawn. You need a high-luck rod and specific bait to fish effectively in these conditions.
