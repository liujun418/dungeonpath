## Understanding How Money Works in Fisch

Before diving into farming strategies, it's essential to understand the economy.

### What Determines Fish Value?

Every fish in Fisch has a **base value** that depends on:

| Factor | Impact on Value | Example |
|--------|----------------|---------|
| **Species rarity** | Common fish: 5–50 C$, Legendary fish: 5,000–50,000 C$ | A common Minnow sells for 5 C$, a legendary Kraken for 50,000 C$ |
| **Weight** | Heavier fish within the same species sell for more | A 50 kg Bass sells for more than a 5 kg Bass |
| **Mutations** | Multipliers stack multiplicatively (see below) | Shiny + Albino = 1.85 × 1.1 = 2.035x |
| **Appraisal bonus** | Appraisal can increase value by up to 25% | A 100 C$ fish can become 125 C$ after appraisal |
| **Time of day** | Some fish have different values at night | Lunar mutations only appear at night |

### The Golden Rule: Always Appraise

**Never sell a fish without appraising it first.** The Appraiser NPC (found near most docks) gives you two benefits:

1. **XP toward your fishing level** — higher levels unlock new areas and rods
2. **Potential value increase** — up to 25% bonus on the fish's sell price

| Scenario | Without Appraisal | With Appraisal | Difference |
|----------|------------------|----------------|------------|
| Common fish (50 C$) | 50 C$ | 50–62.5 C$ | +0–12.5 C$ |
| Rare fish (500 C$) | 500 C$ | 500–625 C$ | +0–125 C$ |
| Legendary fish (5,000 C$) | 5,000 C$ | 5,000–6,250 C$ | +0–1,250 C$ |
| Mutated legendary (50,000 C$) | 50,000 C$ | 50,000–62,500 C$ | +0–12,500 C$ |

Over a farming session of 100 fish, appraisal alone can earn you an extra 5,000–15,000 C$.

## Mutation Value Guide

Mutations are the single biggest factor in maximizing your earnings. Understanding them is the difference between earning 10,000 C$ per hour and 100,000+ C$ per hour.

### Complete Mutation Multiplier Table

| Mutation | Value Multiplier | How to Obtain | Targeted Strategy |
|----------|-----------------|---------------|-------------------|
| **Albino** | 1.1x | Random chance | Use Lucky Rod, fish at any location |
| **Glossy / Silver** | 1.6x | Random chance | Higher luck = better odds |
| **Shiny** | 1.85x | Random chance | Best overall mutation for consistent income |
| **Sparkling** | 1.85x | Random chance / Events | Similar to Shiny — stack both for 3.42x |
| **Lunar** | 2.5x | Night fishing only | Fish at night with high luck gear |
| **New Year's** | 2.6x | Seasonal event | Only during New Year events |
| **Sunken** | 4x | Deep water fishing | Use deep water bait, fish at Desolate Deep |
| **Harmonized** | 4.2x | Specific locations / events | Location-dependent |
| **Subspace** | 5x | Special methods, late-game | Requires specific rod setups |
| **Prismize** | 5.5x | Enchantments / Ethereal Prism Rod | Best consistent high-value mutation |
| **Fallen** | 6x | Specific locations | Late-game content |
| **Mourned** | 7.5x | Rare event drops | Extremely rare |
| **Mastered** | 8x | Mastery-based | Requires max mastery on fish |
| **Gravitas** | 9.81x | Special conditions | One of the rarest |
| **Tryhard** | 10x | Tryhard Rod (guaranteed) | Guaranteed with the right rod |
| **Plagued** | 10x | Specific methods | Late-game content |
| **Ocean's Ruin** | 10.2x | Endgame content | Highest confirmed multiplier |
| **Aether** | 12x | Rarest mutation | Mythic — nearly impossible to target |

### Mutation Stacking

When a fish has multiple mutations, the multipliers **stack multiplicatively**:

| Mutation Combination | Total Multiplier | Example: 100 C$ Fish Becomes |
|---------------------|-----------------|------------------------------|
| Shiny only | 1.85x | 185 C$ |
| Shiny + Albino | 2.035x | 203.5 C$ |
| Shiny + Albino + Sparkling | 3.765x | 376.5 C$ |
| Lunar + Shiny | 4.625x | 462.5 C$ |
| Tryhard + Shiny + Albino | 20.35x | 2,035 C$ |
| Aether + Tryhard + Shiny | 222x | 22,200 C$ |

> **Pro Tip:** The **Shiny + Albino + Sparkling** triple combo is your best realistic target. While Aether and Gravitas are incredibly rare, the triple common mutation combo is achievable with a decent luck rod and some patience.

### Best Mutations to Target by Game Stage

| Game Stage | Target Mutations | Why |
|------------|-----------------|-----|
| **Early Game** | Albino, Glossy | Easiest to get — even low luck rods can trigger them |
| **Mid Game** | Shiny, Sparkling, Lunar | Requires decent luck stat — Steady Rod or better |
| **Late Game** | Prismize, Sunken, Fallen | Needs high luck + specific locations/enchantments |
| **Endgame** | Tryhard, Gravitas, Aether | Requires top-tier rods, enchantments, and luck |

**Expected Value Calculation:**
- Without Prismize: catching 100 fish worth 50 C$ each = 5,000 C$
- With Prism Rod: 50 fish at 50 C$ + 50 fish at 275 C$ (Prismize) = 16,250 C$

The Tryhard Rod guarantees the Tryhard mutation (10x value) on every catch. While the -500% resilience makes fish harder to reel in, the 10x multiplier more than compensates.

**Strategy:** Use Tryhard Rod at Roslit Bay for Nurse Shark farming. Even with lower catch rates, each successful catch is worth 10x normal value.

## Trading Strategies

The player-to-player trading system in Fisch can be one of the fastest ways to accumulate coins, especially if you catch rare or mutated fish.

### Trading Fundamentals

| Concept | Description | Tips |
|---------|-------------|------|
| **Know your fish values** | Research the current market value before trading | Use community Discords and wikis |
| **Appraise before trading** | An appraised fish has confirmed value | Makes negotiations easier |
| **Mutation awareness** | Mutated fish are worth significantly more | Learn which mutations are most desired |
| **Timing matters** | Fish values can fluctuate with updates | Trade before nerfs, hold before buffs |

### What to Trade For

| You Have | Trade For | Why |
|----------|-----------|-----|
| Common mutated fish | Coins or better bait | Coins are universally useful |
| Rare fish without mutations | Mutated common fish | Mutation multipliers often beat base rarity |
| Excess bait | Coins or rare bait | Diversify your bait collection |
| Duplicate cosmetics | Needed items or coins | Reduce clutter, gain resources |

### Trading Tips

1. **Always appraise your fish before trading** — you need to know the exact value
2. **Don't undersell mutations** — a Shiny + Albino common fish can be worth more than a rare unmutated fish
3. **Trade during peak hours** — more players means better deals and faster trades
4. **Be patient** — the best trades come from waiting for the right buyer
5. **Avoid impulse trades** — sleep on any trade involving rare or mutated fish

Albino fish sell for 1.1x to regular merchants but **1.3x to the Albino Vendor**. Always find and sell Albino fish to the Albino Vendor for the extra 0.2x multiplier. Over a session of 20 Albino fish worth 100 C$ each, this saves 400 C$.

## FAQ

### What's the fastest way to earn coins in Fisch?

**Nurse Shark farming at Roslit Bay** is the fastest and most accessible method for most players. Using Minnows as bait and a Steady Rod or better, you can earn 50,000–80,000 C$ per hour consistently. For late-game players, the **Ethereal Prism Rod** at Atlantis is the absolute fastest method.

### Should I focus on rare fish or mutations?

**Mutations on common fish are often more profitable than unmutated rare fish.** A Shiny + Albino common fish (3.42x value) can easily outsell a rare fish without mutations. Focus on building luck and catching mutations first.

### Is the Ethereal Prism Rod worth 3,500,000 C$?

Yes, absolutely. Its Prismize passive (50% chance for 5.5x value) effectively **triples your earnings** compared to fishing without it. The rod pays for itself within a few dedicated farming sessions.

### How important is bait?

Very important. The right bait can **double or triple** your catch rate for specific fish types. However, don't buy expensive bait until you have a decent rod — good bait on a bad rod is wasted money.

### What's the best bait-to-rod combination?

For budget players: **Minnows + Steady Rod** at Roslit Bay.
For mid-game: **Truffle Worms + Resourceful Rod** (doubles bait effects).
For endgame: **Golden Tentacles + Masterline Rod** at Atlantis.

### How do I know if a fish is mutated?

Mutated fish have visual indicators — they glow, shimmer, or have unusual colors. When you reel in a fish, the catch notification will display the mutation name (e.g., "Shiny Bass").

### Can I make money without fighting other players?

Fisch is primarily a cooperative fishing game with no PvP. All money making is done through fishing, questing, and trading — no combat required.

### How much should I expect to earn per hour?

| Game Stage | Expected Earnings |
|-----------|-------------------|
| Early Game | 20,000–50,000 C$/hour |
| Mid Game | 50,000–150,000 C$/hour |
| Late Game | 100,000–300,000 C$/hour |
| Optimized Endgame | 200,000–500,000+ C$/hour |

### Should I save coins or spend them as I earn?

**Save for big rod upgrades.** Don't spend coins on cosmetics, small bait purchases, or incremental upgrades. Save for the big jumps: Carbon Rod (2,000 C$), Steady Rod (7,000 C$), Rod of the Depths (750,000 C$), Ethereal Prism Rod (3,500,000 C$).

### Is trading profitable?

Yes, if you understand fish values. Catching a mutated fish worth 5,000 C$ and selling it to another player for 8,000 C$ in coins is pure profit. However, make sure you're not getting scammed — always know the real value of what you're trading.
