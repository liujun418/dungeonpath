## 1. What is Fisch?

Fisch is a popular Roblox fishing simulation game where you catch fish, upgrade your gear, and explore different fishing locations. It's free to play and combines relaxing fishing mechanics with progression-based goals.

**Why play Fisch?**
- Chill gameplay perfect for casual players
- Deep progression system with dozens of fish to discover
- Regular updates with new content
- Social: trade and compete with other players

## 2. Getting Started

### Step 1: Join the Game

Search for **"Fisch"** on Roblox and join. You'll spawn on the starting dock with a basic rod.

### Step 2: Your First Catch

1. Click/Tap the water near the dock
2. Wait for a bite (you'll see a visual indicator)
3. Click/Tap again to reel in
4. You'll earn coins from your catch

### Step 3: Upgrade Your Rod

Your starter rod is basic. Visit the **Rod Shop** near the dock to upgrade. Better rods let you:
- Catch rarer fish
- Reel in faster
- Fish in deeper waters

## 3. Beginner Tips

| Tip | Why It Matters |
|-----|---------------|
| Start at Moosewood | Easiest location for beginners |
| Save coins for rod upgrades | Don't spend on cosmetics early |
| Check the weather | Some fish only appear in certain conditions |
| Talk to NPCs | They give quests that reward extra coins |
