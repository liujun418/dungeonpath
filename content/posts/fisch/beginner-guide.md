## What Is Fisch?

**Fisch** is one of the most popular fishing simulation games on Roblox, blending relaxing gameplay with a deep progression system that hooks (pun intended) players for hours. Whether you're a casual player who enjoys the zen of casting a line or a completionist chasing every rare mutation, Fisch offers something for everyone.

In Fisch, you:
- **Cast lines** at dozens of unique fishing locations across multiple islands
- **Catch fish** ranging from common minnows to legendary sharks with rare mutations
- **Earn coins (C$)** to upgrade rods, buy bait, and unlock new areas
- **Complete quests** from NPCs for XP, exclusive gear, and bonus rewards
- **Collect mutations** — special attributes like *Albino*, *Sparkling*, and *Shiny* that dramatically increase fish value
- **Compete and trade** with other players in a thriving community

This guide covers everything a brand-new player needs to go from their very first cast to catching rare, high-value fish.

## Getting Started: Your First 30 Minutes

### Step 1: Spawn at Moosewood

When you first join Fisch, you'll spawn at **Moosewood** — the tutorial dock area and your home base. This is where you'll learn the basics, buy your first rod upgrades, and interact with essential NPCs.

### Step 2: Complete Pierre's Tutorial

Head to the beach and find **Pierre**, the NPC with a book. He'll walk you through the fishing mechanics step by step:

1. **Cast your line** — click or tap on the water near the dock
2. **Wait for a bite** — watch for the visual bite indicator (your bobber dips and a notification appears)
3. **Reel in** — click or tap again when you see the bite signal
4. **Collect your catch** — the fish goes into your inventory and earns you coins

Completing Pierre's tutorial rewards you with XP and familiarizes you with the core loop. **Don't skip this** — it teaches mechanics that aren't explained anywhere else.

### Step 3: Talk to Phineas

Near the docks, find **Phineas** and accept his bait quest. He'll ask you to catch a specific fish, and completing this quest rewards you with **Bait Crates** — one of the best sources of bait for new players. This sets you up with bait early, which significantly improves your catch rate.

### Step 4: Sell Your First Fish and Save

Visit the **Fish Merchant** at the docks to sell your catches. Here's the most important beginner tip: **don't spend coins on cosmetics**. Every coin should go toward your next rod upgrade. Your first savings goal is the **Carbon Rod**.

### Step 5: Use Active Codes

Before you invest too much time, redeem any active codes for free rewards. Go to **Settings → Code Icon** and enter:

## Understanding the Core Mechanics

### How Fishing Works

Fisch's fishing system has three phases:

| Phase | What Happens | What You Do |
|-------|-------------|-------------|
| **Cast** | Your line enters the water | Click/tap the water to cast |
| **Wait** | Fish approach your bait; time depends on luck, bait, and location | Watch for the bite indicator |
| **Reel** | A fish bites! | Click/tap at the right moment to reel it in |

The timing between cast and bite depends on several stats:
- **Lure Speed** — how quickly fish are attracted to your bait
- **Luck** — chance to catch rare fish and trigger mutations
- **Control** — how easy it is to reel fish in (reduces escape chance)
- **Resilience** — ability to handle heavier fish without the line breaking
- **Max KG** — the maximum weight of fish you can catch

### The Appraisal System

Before selling any fish, visit an **Appraiser NPC** (found near most docks). Appraising a fish:
- Gives you **XP** toward your fishing level
- Has a chance to **increase the fish's value** (up to +25%)
- Reveals the fish's exact stats and mutation details

**Always appraise before selling.** It takes one click and can significantly boost your earnings.

### Mutations: The Key to Big Money

Mutations are special attributes that can appear on any fish, dramatically changing both appearance and value. Here's the complete mutation multiplier table:

| Mutation | Value Multiplier | How to Get | Rarity |
|----------|-----------------|------------|--------|
| **Albino** | 1.1x (1.3x to Albino Vendor) | Random chance | Common |
| **Glossy / Silver** | 1.6x | Random chance | Uncommon |
| **Shiny** | 1.85x | Random chance | Rare |
| **Sparkling** | 1.85x | Random chance / Events | Rare |
| **Lunar** | 2.5x | Night fishing, specific locations | Rare |
| **New Year's** | 2.6x | Seasonal event | Limited |
| **Sunken** | 4x | Deep water fishing | Very Rare |
| **Harmonized** | 4.2x | Specific locations / events | Very Rare |
| **Subspace** | 5x | Special methods, late-game | Very Rare |
| **Prismize** | 5.5x | Enchantments / special events | Ultra Rare |
| **Fallen** | 6x | Specific locations | Ultra Rare |
| **Mourned** | 7.5x | Rare event drops | Ultra Rare |
| **Mastered** | 8x | Mastery-based | Ultra Rare |

> **Pro Tip:** A **Shiny + Albino + Sparkling** fish stacks all three multipliers, turning a common 10 C$ fish into a 34 C$ catch (1.85 × 1.1 × 1.85). Aim for high-value base fish with multiple mutations for maximum profit.

### Enchantments

At the **Keeper's Altar**, you can apply enchantments to your rods using **Enchant Relics**. Enchantments add passive bonuses like increased luck, lure speed, or mutation chances. These become important in the mid-to-late game but aren't essential for beginners.

The **Nurse Shark** (Uncommon rarity) is the single best fish for beginners to farm for money. Here's why:

- **Location:** Roslit Bay (specific spot marked by experienced players)
- **Bait:** Use **Minnows** for best results
- **Value:** Sells for a solid amount even without mutations
- **With mutations:** A Shiny Nurse Shark or Albino Nurse Shark sells for significantly more
- **Catch rate:** Common enough that you'll catch multiple per session

## Bait Guide for Beginners

Bait significantly affects what you catch and how quickly. Understanding bait is one of the fastest ways to improve your game.

### Types of Bait

| Bait Type | How to Get | Best For | Notes |
|-----------|-----------|----------|-------|
| **Worms** | Bait Crates, Phineas quest | General fishing, beginners | Good universal luck bonus |
| **Minnows** | Bait Crates, shops | Nurse Sharks, fast catches | High preferred luck, low resistance |
| **Flakes** | Bait Crates | Common fish | Cheap and reliable |
| **Seaweed** | Bait Crates, shops | Herbivore fish | Cost-effective early option |
| **Night Shrimp** | Bait Crates, night fishing | Nocturnal fish | Good for night sessions |
| **Truffle Worms** | Rare drops, shops | High-value fish | Expensive but worth it for rare targets |
| **Luminous Larva** | Specific locations | Deep water fish | Best for Desolate Deep |

### How to Get Bait

1. **Phineas's Quest** — your first reliable source
2. **Bait Crates** — buy from merchants at most docks
3. **NPC Quests** — various NPCs reward bait for completing tasks
4. **Angler Quests** — higher-tier bait rewards
5. **Event drops** — seasonal events sometimes give exclusive bait

> **Beginner Tip:** Start with **Worms** and **Minnows**. They're cheap, effective, and work for most early-game fish. Don't waste coins on expensive bait until you have a good rod.

## Making Money Fast: Beginner Strategies

Here are the most effective ways to earn coins as a new player. For detailed coin-earning strategies, see our [Fisch Money Farming Guide](/posts/fisch/money-farming-guide/).

### Strategy 1: Nurse Shark Farming (Best for Beginners)

1. Travel to **Roslit Bay** by boat (west of Moosewood)
2. Equip **Minnows** as bait
3. Fish at the Nurse Shark spot
4. Sell to the Fish Merchant
5. Repeat until you can afford your next rod upgrade

### Strategy 2: Mutation Hunting

Focus on catching fish with mutations:
- **Albino fish** → sell to the Albino Vendor for 1.3x instead of regular 1.1x
- **Shiny + Sparkling** combos stack to ~3.4x value
- Even common fish with mutations can sell for more than rare fish without

### Strategy 3: Quest Completion

Complete all available quests before farming:
- Pierre's tutorial and follow-up quests
- Phineas's bait quests
- Any Angler Quests available at your level

Quests often reward more coins-per-minute than raw fishing.

### Strategy 4: Appraise Everything

Never sell a fish without appraising it first. The XP helps you level up (unlocking new areas), and the value increase can be up to 25%.

## Weather and Totems

Fisch has a dynamic weather system that affects which fish are available. Understanding weather is key to catching location-specific fish.

### Weather Types

| Weather | Effect | Best Fish |
|---------|--------|-----------|
| **Clear** | Standard fishing | All common fish |
| **Rain** | Increased rare fish spawn rate | Weather-specific rares |
| **Storm** | Higher risk, higher reward | Rare and storm-exclusive fish |
| **Fog** | Reduced visibility, unique spawns | Fog-exclusive fish |

### Weather Totems

You can control the weather using **Totems**, which are obtained from quests, events, or shops:

| Totem | Effect |
|-------|--------|
| **Rainbow Totem** | Creates ideal fishing weather |
| **Rain Totem** | Forces rain weather |
| **Eclipse Totem** | Triggers eclipse event |
| **Sundial Totem** | Forces clear weather |
| **Tempest Totem** | Triggers storm weather |

> **Beginner Tip:** Don't worry about totems early on. Focus on farming coins and upgrading your rod first. Weather manipulation becomes important in the mid-to-late game. For advanced strategies, see our [Fisch Advanced Fishing Techniques](/posts/fisch/advanced-fishing-techniques/) guide.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Fisch free to play?

Yes, Fisch is completely free on Roblox. There are optional in-game purchases, but everything is earnable through gameplay.

### What's the best first upgrade?

Save your coins for the **Carbon Rod** (~2,000 C$). It's the single biggest quality-of-life upgrade for new players and will make fishing significantly faster and more rewarding.

### What's the best bait for beginners?

**Worms** and **Minnows** are the best starting baits. Get your first batch from Phineas's quest, then buy more from bait shops.

### How do I find rare fish?

Rare fish depend on three things: **your rod's luck stat**, **your location**, and sometimes **weather conditions**. Upgrade your rod, explore new areas, and pay attention to the weather. Check our [Fisch Best Rods Tier List](/posts/fisch/best-rods-tier-list/) and [Fisch Fish Location Map](/posts/fisch/fish-location-map/) for detailed guides.

### What are mutations and why do they matter?

Mutations are special attributes (like *Shiny*, *Albino*, *Sparkling*) that randomly appear on fish. They change the fish's appearance and **multiply its sell value**. A Shiny fish sells for 1.85x normal price, and mutations can stack. See the mutations table above for the full list.

### How do I travel between islands?

Use the **boat** at Moosewood docks to travel to other islands. Roslit Bay is directly west of Moosewood and should be your first destination after the tutorial.

### Are there codes for free rewards?

Yes! Fisch regularly releases codes for free items, coins, and cosmetics. Check the codes table at the top of this guide for current active codes. Codes expire quickly, so redeem them as soon as you find them.

### Can I play Fisch with friends?

Yes! Fisch is a multiplayer game. You can join the same server as friends and fish together, trade fish, and compete for rare catches.
