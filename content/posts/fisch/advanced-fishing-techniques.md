## Weather Manipulation

### Understanding the Weather System

Fisch features a dynamic weather system that directly affects which fish appear at each location:

| Weather Type | Effect | Best For |
|-------------|--------|----------|
| **Clear** | Standard fishing conditions | Common and uncommon fish |
| **Rain** | Increased rare fish spawn rate | Rare fish hunting |
| **Storm** | Highest rare spawn rate, but dangerous | Legendary fish attempts |
| **Fog** | Unique fog-exclusive fish | Completing collection |

### Weather Totems

Totems let you control the weather. Here's the complete guide:

| Totem | Effect | How to Get | Best Use |
|-------|--------|-----------|----------|
| **Rain Totem** | Forces rain weather | Keeper's Altar, events | Rare fish hunting |
| **Sundial Totem** | Forces clear weather | Quest rewards | Consistent fishing conditions |
| **Tempest Totem** | Forces storm weather | Endgame content | Legendary fish attempts |
| **Rainbow Totem** | Creates ideal fishing weather | Events | Best overall weather |
| **Eclipse Totem** | Triggers eclipse event | Rare drops | Eclipse-exclusive fish |

### Weather Manipulation Strategy

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Check current weather at your target location |
| 2 | If weather doesn't match your goal, use the appropriate totem |
| 3 | Totems have cooldowns — plan your usage |
| 4 | During optimal weather, fish continuously for maximum yield |

## Pro Tips

### Tip 1: Learn the Bite Timing

Rare fish have different bite patterns than common fish:
- **Longer wait time** — rare fish take 2-3x longer to bite
- **Different indicator** — watch for a different visual cue
- **Multiple nibbles** — rare fish often nibble before fully biting

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I increase my luck stat?

Upgrade to rods with higher luck stats, use bait with luck bonuses, and fish during weather that boosts luck (rain/storm).

### What's the rarest mutation?

**Aether** (12x multiplier) is the rarest mutation, followed by Ocean's Ruin (10.2x) and Gravitas (9.81x).

### Can I force a specific mutation?

No. Mutations are random, but you can increase the chance with high-luck rods, weather manipulation, and bait bonuses.

### What's the best time to fish for rare fish?

Storm weather provides the highest rare spawn rate. Use a Tempest Totem to force storm conditions.
