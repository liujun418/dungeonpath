## Pressure Badges Overview

Pressure features one of the most extensive achievement systems on Roblox, with over 50 unique badges spanning everything from your first death to near-impossible solo challenges. Badges range from trivial (join the game) to extreme (reach room 500 in Endless Mode or clear The Ridge solo without hiding).

This guide organizes every badge by category and provides exact unlock conditions. For general survival fundamentals, start with our [Pressure Beginner Guide](/posts/pressure/beginner-guide/).

## Badge Hunting Strategy

### Recommended Unlock Order

1. **Start with death badges** — you'll earn most naturally through gameplay. Speed-run the special ones (Death Any%, *splat*, You Deserve This).
2. **Progress through entity survival badges** — focus on one entity category at a time. Angler variants first (easiest), then Pandemonium, then Wall Dwellers.
3. **Complete shop badges** — farm gold in standard runs, buy everything from Sebastian.
4. **Attempt escape badges** — start with standard Pardoned, then work up to team and solo challenges.
5. **Tackle event badges** — Raveyard and Worth The Wait badges during their active windows.
6. **Endless Mode last** — Premium Payout (room 500) should be your final goal after mastering all other content.

### Multiplayer-Only Badges

Several badges require coordinated teams: **Lonely**, **Power Of Friendship**, **Group Of Sweats**, **This Badge Sucks**, **Will 2 Live**, **Not On MY Watch!**, **:3**, **Effective Motivator**, **Like Nothing Happened**. Find a reliable group before attempting these.

## FAQ

### Do I need to complete Raveyard badges during Halloween?
Raveyard badges may only be obtainable during seasonal event windows. If the Raveyard map is accessible year-round, badges should remain earnable. Check the in-game badge menu for current availability.

### What's the hardest badge in Pressure?
**We Didn't Playtest To Make Sure This Was Possible** (The Ridge solo, no lockers, no revives) and **Premium Payout** (room 500 Endless Mode) are tied for hardest. Both require near-perfect execution across hours of gameplay.

### Can I earn badges in private servers?
Most badges can be earned in private servers, though some multiplayer badges may require a minimum player count. Check specific badge requirements — badges requiring 4+ or 8+ players naturally need a group regardless of server type.
