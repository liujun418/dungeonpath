## Ships & Sailing Basics

Ships are your primary method of traveling between islands and accessing ocean content in Sailor Piece. While the ship system is relatively straightforward compared to the game's combat and race mechanics, understanding navigation is essential for progression.

### Getting Your First Ship

1. Go to any major island and find the **Ship Sailor NPC** near the harbor/pier
2. Purchase a ship using in-game currency (Yen)
3. Open your **Ship Inventory** tab in the shop menu to spawn your purchased ship
4. Ships despawn after ~10 minutes of inactivity — re-spawn from the NPC if needed

### Ship Upgrades

Ship upgrades are purchased with Yen. Redeeming active codes is the fastest way to get Yen for ship upgrades without grinding. Codes like `SORRYFORBUGS`, `UPDATE2`, `DUNGEONS`, and `2MVISITS` have historically provided free currency rewards.

The ship's primary purpose is transportation and enabling Sea Beast spawns. Combat statistics are not ship-dependent — your character build (race, melee, sword, fruit) handles all combat.

## Sea 2 Unlock Quest

Sea 2 is the gateway to endgame content. Unlock it by completing the Sea Traveler's quest on World Island.

### Requirements

| Item | Quantity | How to Get |
|------|----------|------------|
| Ancient Fragments | 2 | 20 possible spawn locations across Sea 1 islands — search thoroughly |
| Map Pieces | 7 | Dropped by specific bosses across Sea 1 |

Once both are collected, return to the Sea Traveler to unlock Sea 2 and access Blue Planet.

## Sea Beasts — Complete Hunting Guide

Sea Beasts are the ultimate endgame ocean encounters in Sea 2. There are two types:

| Boss | HP | Spawn Zone |
|------|-----|------------|
| Sea Serpent | ~625 Billion | Open Sea |
| Kraken | ~1.25 Trillion | Deep Sea |

### Spawn Requirements

| Requirement | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| Sea Unlocked | Sea 2 |
| Minimum Bounty | 500,000 |
| Position | 200-600 studs from any island — **"Open Sea" banner must appear** |
| Method | Sail into open water and wait |

### Spawn Rate by Bounty

| Combined Group Bounty | Approximate Spawn Interval |
|----------------------|---------------------------|
| ~10 Million | Every 20-40 minutes |
| ~20 Million | Every 5-15 minutes |
| 50 Million (cap) | Near back-to-back |

> Farm in **groups of 3-12 players** to stack bounty. Solo farming is extremely slow. The 50M bounty cap provides the fastest possible spawn rate.

## Sea Beast Drops

| Item | Drop Rate | Notes |
|------|-----------|-------|
| Bloodline Stone | 100% | Guaranteed each kill |
| Clan Reroll | 100% | Guaranteed each kill |
| Kraken Armor | ~3.5% | Rare defensive item |
| Blossom Outfit | ~6.8% | Cosmetic outfit |
| Dragon Goddess Sword | ~2% (Serpent) / ~0.9% (Kraken) | Mythical S+ tier sword |
| Dragon Queen Title | ~0.85% (150-kill pity system) | **+97.5% damage boost** |

### Drop Optimization Strategy

Use a **Luck build** while fighting (Luckborn/Kitsune race, Luck-focused accessories). Switch to your highest DPS melee or sword spec to burn HP, then **switch back to Luck gear for the killing blow** to maximize rare drop chances. In a group, the player with the highest Luck should land the last hit.

## Complete Sea Beast Farming Strategy

1. **Form a group** of 3-12 players with high combined bounty
2. **Sail to Open Sea** (200-600 studs from any island)
3. **Wait for spawn** — highest-bounty player should lead the group
4. **Fight with Luck gear** initially, switch to DPS for HP burn
5. **Switch back to Luck** for the killing blow
6. **Server hop** if spawns slow down — some servers have better RNG than others

## Archangel Race Unlock (Sea 2)

The Archangel race is an evolved Angel race unlocked in Sea 2:

1. Collect **7 Halos** — 4 Hidden Halos (specific locations) + 3 Boss Halos (drops from bosses)
2. Visit the **Celestial Guide** on Blue Planet
3. Trade the 7 Halos for Archangel race unlock
