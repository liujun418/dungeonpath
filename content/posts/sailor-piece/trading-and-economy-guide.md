## Why the Economy Matters in Sailor Piece

In **Sailor Piece**, Beli (money) and Gems are the lifeblood of your progression. The best Devil Fruits cost hundreds of thousands of Beli or hundreds of Gems. Top-tier fighting styles, swords, and rerolls all require significant investment. Players who understand the economy progress **2-3 times faster** than those who grind blindly.

This guide covers everything from earning your first Beli to becoming a trading powerhouse with millions in the bank. Whether you want to buy the Dragon fruit, optimize your clan, or trade your way to exclusive cosmetics, this guide has you covered.

## Money Farming Methods

Earning Beli efficiently is the foundation of economic success. Different methods excel at different stages of progression.

### Early Game Money Farming (Level 0-3,000)

| Method | Beli per Hour | How It Works | Efficiency |
|--------|--------------|--------------|------------|
| **Island Quests** | 50,000-100,000 | Complete all quests on each island before moving on | Best early-game method |
| **Enemy Grinding** | 30,000-60,000 | Defeat regular enemies in your level range | Steady income |
| **Code Redemption** | 50,000-250,000 (one-time) | Redeem all active codes | Best immediate boost |
| **Chest Opening** | 10,000-30,000 | Find and open chests on islands | Supplemental income |

**Early Game Strategy:** Focus entirely on quests. They provide 3-5x more XP and Beli per hour than grinding regular enemies. Redeem all codes immediately for a massive cash injection.

**Mid-Game Strategy:** Boss farming becomes the primary income source. Use Boss Keys to avoid travel time between fights. Target Sukuna and Gojo first (fastest clears), then work up to Jinwoo and Alucard.

### Late Game Money Farming (Level 8,000-12,500)

**Late Game Strategy:** The full boss rush route (see Boss Strategies Guide) earns 40-80 Million Beli per complete cycle. Add luck boosts from codes for increased drop rates. If you have Sea 2 access, Sea 2 bosses provide even higher returns.

## Trading Strategies

### The Golden Rules of Trading

| Rule | Explanation |
|------|-------------|
| **Know current market values** | Prices change with updates. Check community resources before trading |
| **Never rush a trade** | Scammers create urgency. Take your time to verify values |
| **Trade up, never trade down** | Only accept trades where you receive equal or greater value |
| **Verify before confirming** | Double-check every item in the trade window before accepting |
| **Keep a trade log** | Track what you gave and received to evaluate your trading performance |

### How to Evaluate Trade Value

Use this framework to assess any trade offer:

| Factor | Weight | How to Assess |
|--------|--------|---------------|
| **Fruit Rarity** | Highest | Mythical > Legendary > Epic > Rare > Uncommon > Common |
| **Fruit Utility** | High | Does the fruit fit the current meta? Is it in demand? |
| **Item Scarcity** | High | How difficult is it to obtain? Boss drops with 1% rates are worth more |
| **Current Demand** | Medium | Is the fruit popular in the current meta? Check community discussions |
| **Future Potential** | Medium | Will the fruit be buffed/nerfed in the next update? Follow developer notes |

### What Is Overvalued in the Current Market

| Item | Why It Is Overvalued | Better Alternative |
|------|---------------------|-------------------|
| **Common fruits** | Traders sometimes overpay for collection completion | Skip common fruits -- they offer minimal combat value |
| **Old/nerfed fruits** | Nostalgia pricing. Check if the fruit was nerfed in recent updates | Trade for fruits in the current meta tier list |
| **Unawakened Mythical fruits** | The rarity drives prices up, but awakened lower-tier fruits may outperform them in combat | Consider an awakened Legendary fruit vs. unawakened Mythical |

### What Is Undervalued in the Current Market

| Item | Why It Is Undervalued | Opportunity |
|------|----------------------|-------------|
| **Boss Keys** | Many players do not realize the farming potential | Collect Boss Keys and trade them for premium fruits |
| **Map Pieces** | Needed for Sea 2, but casual players may not know this | Accumulate and trade when Sea 2 demand spikes |
| **Mid-tier fruits with awakening potential** | Players focus on Mythical, ignoring that awakened mid-tier fruits are strong | Buy awakened mid-tier fruits at a discount |
| **Cosmetic titles** | Purely cosmetic, but rare titles (0.2-1% drop rate) appreciate over time | Collect rare titles from boss drops |

## Trading Safety: How to Avoid Scams

The trading system is the most common place for players to lose valuable items. Follow these rules to protect yourself.

### Common Scam Tactics

| Scam Type | How It Works | How to Avoid |
|-----------|-------------|--------------|
| **Fake Fruit Trade** | Trader offers a fruit that looks rare but is actually common | Always read the full item name and rarity in the trade window |
| **Quick-Switch Scam** | Trader adds a valuable item, then swaps it for a cheap item at the last second | Review the final trade offer carefully before confirming. Take your time. |
| **Trust Trade Scam** | "Trade me your fruit first, I will trade you back double" | Never trade first with strangers. Use the built-in trade system which requires mutual confirmation |
| **Middleman Scam** | "Use this trusted middleman player" -- the middleman steals both items | Only use officially recognized middlemen from the community Discord. Verify their identity |
| **Distraction Scam** | Trader creates chaos (summons boss, causes lag) while swapping trade items | Cancel the trade if anything suspicious happens. Restart in a clean session |
| **"Overpay" Bait** | "I will overpay for your fruit!" but the overpay items are worthless | Evaluate every item independently. Do not get excited by quantity -- focus on quality |

### Safe Trading Checklist

Before accepting any trade, verify:

| Check | Question to Ask |
|-------|----------------|
| Item Names | Are the items exactly what was advertised? |
| Item Rarity | Is the rarity what I expect? (Common, Uncommon, Rare, Epic, Legendary, Mythical) |
| Item Count | Is the quantity correct? |
| Trade Terms | Does this trade benefit me? (trade up, never down) |
| Pressure Tactics | Is the other player rushing me? If so, cancel and reconsider |
| Second Account | Is the player using a known scammer alt account? Check community ban lists |

### Reporting Scammers

If you are scammed:
1. **Screenshot the trade** if possible
2. **Report to the official Discord** -- Shadowrise Devs monitor the Discord for scam reports
3. **Share the scammer's username** on community forums to warn others
4. **Learn from the experience** -- understand what went wrong to avoid it in the future

## FAQ

**Q: What is the fastest way to earn Beli?**
A: **Boss farming** is the fastest consistent method. At mid-game, farm Sukuna and Gojo for 200,000-500,000 Beli per hour. At late game, the full boss rush route earns 500,000-1,500,000+ Beli per cycle (47-70 minutes). Sea 2 bosses can earn 1,000,000-3,000,000+ Beli per hour.

**Q: Should I spend Beli or Gems on fruit spins?**
A: **Save Gems for Premium Gem Spins.** They have significantly better rates for rare and Mythical fruits. Use Beli for fighting styles, swords, and basic needs first. Only use Beli for fruit spins if you have excess money after essential purchases.

**Q: What fruit should I buy first?**
A: For early players, **Snow** (Epic, ~45,000 Beli) is the best value. Mid-game players should aim for **Quake** or **Light** (Legendary, 100,000-120,000 Beli). Endgame players should save for **Dragon** or **Fiend** (Mythical, 350,000-400,000+ Beli).

**Q: How do I know if a trade is fair?**
A: Compare the trade against current market prices. Use the fruit rarity tiers (Mythical > Legendary > Epic > Rare > Uncommon > Common) as a baseline. Factor in awakening status, meta relevance, and scarcity. If you are getting less value than you are giving, decline the trade.

**Q: Can I lose items in a trade?**
A: Only if you accept an unfair trade. The trading system requires both players to confirm, so you cannot be forced into a trade. However, scammers use psychological tactics (urgency, fake items, quick-switching) to trick you into accepting bad deals. Always verify before confirming.

**Q: What is the best clan for making money?**
A: **Devil clan** (+42% DMG, +60% HP, 30% bonus drop rate) is the best overall. The damage boost makes boss farming faster, and the 30% bonus drop rate increases the value of every boss kill.

**Q: Is Fishman race good for money farming?**
A: Fishman has +Income and +EXP gain, making it excellent for grinding enemies and quests for Beli. However, for boss farming, **Galevorn** (+75% DMG) is better because faster boss clears = more boss kills per hour = more total Beli.

**Q: How do I get gems without spending Robux?**
A: The best free methods are: opening chests on every island (5-50 gems per chest), clearing dungeons (100+ gems per clear), defeating bosses (50-200 gems per boss), and redeeming codes (100-400 gems per code). High-level NPC grinding also drops gems.

**Q: Should I trade fruits or keep them for myself?**
A: If the fruit is useful for your build and level, keep it. If you already have a better fruit or the trade offers significantly more value, trade it. Never trade a fruit you are actively using unless the replacement is clearly better.

**Q: What items should I never trade away?**
A: **Ancient Fragments** and **Map Pieces** -- you need 20 fragments and 7 map pieces to unlock Sea 2. **Boss Keys** for bosses you are actively farming. **Reroll items** if you have not yet achieved your target race and clan.
