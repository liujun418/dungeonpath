## How Races Work

Your race is the foundation of your build in Sailor Piece, providing permanent stat bonuses that affect every aspect of combat. Races are assigned randomly through Race Rerolls, which you farm from bosses, chests, codes, and the Merchant.

Unlike some other Roblox games, you don't upgrade individual races through versions. Instead, progression comes from rolling into higher-tier races and pairing them with complementary melee specs, swords, fruits, clans, and bloodlines.

## Recommended Hybrid Builds

### Build 1: Sword Hybrid (Best Overall)

The most versatile and consistent build across PvE and PvP.

```
Race: Swordblessed / Luckborn / Sunborn
Sword: Dragon Goddess / Atomic / Ice Queen
Fruit: Light (mobility + AoE)
Clan: Devil / Frostbane
Bloodline: Astral / Demonic / Doombringer
Haki: Observation + Armament + Conqueror (all three)
```

Why it works: Sword damage bonuses from the race stack with the S+ sword's base stats. Light fruit provides escape and repositioning. All three Haki types give dodge, damage, and passive aura.

### Build 2: Melee Tank

Maximum survivability and close-range damage.

```
Race: Warlord / Galevorn / Servant
Melee Spec: Cosmic Being / Moon Slayer / The World
Clan: Senzu / Pride
Bloodline: Primordial / Emperion
Haki: All three (focus Armament for damage boost)
```

Why it works: Warlord's +85% HP and +10% damage reduction make you nearly unkillable in extended fights. Cosmic Being's fast cooldowns mean constant ability uptime.

### Build 3: Farming / Luck Build

Optimized for rare drops from bosses and Sea Beasts.

```
Race: Luckborn / Kitsune / Archangel
Weapon: Light Fruit + any high AoE sword
Clan: Devil (item duplication passive)
Bloodline: Primordial (extra drop chance)
Strategy: Farm bosses on repeat for rare drops
```

Why it works: Luckborn's +35% Luck combined with Devil clan's item duplication and Primordial bloodline's drop chance creates the highest rare drop probability in the game.

### Build 4: Boss-Melter (Pure DPS)

Maximum single-target damage for Sea Beasts and World Bosses.

```
Race: Swordblessed / Warlord / Reaper
Weapon: Dragon Goddess / True Aizen
Melee: Moon Slayer / Strongest Shinobi
Clan: Senzu (damage stacking after 5 abilities)
Traits: Secret-tier damage multipliers
```

Why it works: Senzu clan's damage stacking combined with Swordblessed's +20% sword damage and S+ tier weapons deletes boss HP bars. Each ability used adds a damage stack.

## Essential Progression Systems

| System | Purpose | Priority |
|--------|---------|----------|
| Observation Haki | Dodge enemy attacks (press G) | High — unlock at Desert Island (250K coins + 300 Gems) |
| Armament Haki | Damage buff and defense penetration | High — unlock at Snow Island |
| Conqueror Haki | Passive damage boost + aura | Endgame |
| Clans | Reroll for S-tier buffs (Devil, Senzu, Pride) | Mid-game |
| Bloodlines | Sea 2 system — Primordial, Demonic, Astral are best | Late-game |
| Runes | Mid-late game DPS boost items | Mid-game |
| Artifacts | Equippable gear — damage, crit, defense | Mid-game |
| Traits | Passive stat multipliers — aim for Secret-tier | Late-game |
| Relics | Craftable buff items | Throughout |

## Quick Start Progression

1. Reroll out of **Human** immediately
2. Unlock **Observation Haki** at Desert Island
3. Unlock **Armament Haki** at Snow Island
4. Farm bosses (not mobs) for Race Rerolls
5. Save **multiple rerolls** before using them
6. Match your race to your build: Sword → Swordblessed/Sunborn, Melee → Warlord/Galevorn, Farming → Luckborn/Kitsune
