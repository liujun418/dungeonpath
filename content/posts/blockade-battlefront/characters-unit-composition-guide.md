Your character choice in ST: Blockade Battlefront determines your HP pool, weapon compatibility, mobility options, and special abilities. A bad character pick wastes Cen and makes surviving past wave 30 nearly impossible. A good one multiplies everything else you invest in.

This guide covers every playable character ranked, the Mythic and Legendary skin economy, stat breakdowns, unlock requirements, and optimal 4-player team compositions.

## Character Tier List Overview

Characters in Blockade Battlefront fall into four power tiers based on base stats, skin availability, weapon compatibility, and late-game scaling:

### S-Tier: Endgame Carry Potential

| Character | Base HP | Weapon Access | Best Skin | Why S-Tier |
|-----------|---------|---------------|-----------|------------|
| **Dark Speakerman** | Medium | All standard | MYTHICAL | 3x damage, 2x HP, 10 HP heal/hit with Mythic skin. Highest DPS ceiling in the game. |
| **Energize TV Man** | High | Heavy weapons | Titan Replica | 2,025 HP, Z-skill doubles all skill damage. Extremely durable. |
| **Uptake Titan** | Very High | Titan weapons | 100M (Uptake) | 20,000 HP, scaling damage per kill, +1500 HP per other Titan on server. |

### A-Tier: Strong and Reliable

| Character | Base HP | Weapon Access | Best Skin | Why A-Tier |
|-----------|---------|---------------|-----------|------------|
| **Large Cam** | High | Heavy weapons (Bazooka) | Villain Arc | F-skill range 2-3x, damage 3x (6k→18k). Required for Bazooka use. |
| **Large TV Man** | High | Heavy weapons | EXO (Alien) | +300 HP (1,500 total), gains explosion skill. Great AoE. |
| **Jetpack Plunger Man** | Medium | Standard + mobility | Hahoraga | 40 HP/sec regen. Highest mobility character, excellent for kiting. |
| **Tackleman** | Very High | Titan weapons | 100M (Tackleman) | 15,000 HP, time stop aura, 250 HP regen/5s. Extremely stable. |

### B-Tier: Situational Value

| Character | Base HP | Weapon Access | Best Skin | Notes |
|-----------|---------|---------------|-----------|-------|
| **Speaker Cam** | Medium | Support weapons | — | Buffs nearby allies' damage. Valuable in coordinated teams. |
| **Cameraman** | Low-Medium | Standard | Lucky | 50-90% chance to nullify damage with Lucky skin. High RNG survival. |
| **Zeppel** | Low | Standard | Mini Titan | HP jumps from 800→5,000 with Mini Titan skin. Massive transformation. |

| **Upgraded Camera Strider** | Medium | Standard | — | Decent mid-game filler. Good mastery scaling for dedicated players. |

## The Skin Economy: Mythic vs Legendary vs Epic

Skins are the true power system in Blockade Battlefront. A character's base stats are just the starting point — the right skin can 3x your damage output and double your survivability.

### Mythic Skins (God-Tier)

| Skin | Character | Key Stats | Acquisition |
|------|-----------|-----------|-------------|
| **MYTHICAL** | Dark Speakerman | 3x attack, 2x HP, 10 HP heal/enemy hit, dash ability | RNG crate / fragments |
| **Titan Replica** | Energize TV Man | 2,025 HP, Z-skill doubles skill damage | RNG crate / fragments |
| **Villain Arc** | Large Cam (Mastery 80) | F-skill 3x damage (6k→18k), range 2-3x | Mastery 80 reward |
| **100M (Uptake)** | Uptake Titan | 20,000 HP, 1.2x attack, 500 HP/10s, damage scaling/kill, +1500 HP/Titan on server | Code `100MVisit2` / 3,999 Robux / 10,000 Strange Fragments |
| **100M (Tackleman)** | Tackleman | 15,000 HP, 1.2x attack, time stop aura, rainbow F-skill, 250 HP regen/5s | Same as above |

### Legendary Skins (Strong Value)

| Skin | Character | Key Stats | Notes |
|------|-----------|-----------|-------|
| **EXO (Alien)** | Large TV Man | +300 HP (1,500 total), explosion skill | Great AoE addition |
| **Brawler** | Dark Speakerman | Restores 10 stamina per skill hit | Excellent sustain |
| **Lucky** | Cameraman | 50-90% damage nullification | Highest RNG survival in game |
| **Mini Titan** | Zeppel | HP 800→5,000 | Massive stat transformation |
| **Hahoraga (Adaptive)** | Jetpack Plunger Man | 40 HP/sec regeneration | Best sustain skin |

### Skin to Avoid

**Traitor** (Dr. TV Man Mythic) — widely considered the worst Mythic skin. Weak abilities, no jetpack, and poor stat scaling relative to its rarity tier. If you pull it from an RNG crate, don't invest resources into it.

## How to Unlock Characters and Skins

### Method 1: Helicopter Store (Every 5 Waves)

The primary way to unlock base characters. The helicopter arrives every 5 waves with a 30-second shopping window. Characters available:

| Wave | Characters Unlocked |
|------|-------------------|
| 5 | Default Cameraman, Basic Strider |
| 10 | Large Cam, Zeppel |
| 15 | Speaker Cam, Upgraded Camera Strider |
| 20 | Dark Speakerman, Large TV Man |
| 25+ | Jetpack Plunger Man, Energize TV Man |
| 30+ | Chief Clockman, Speaker Strider |
| 40+ | Titan-class characters begin appearing |

### Method 2: Skin Crates and RNG Drops

- Enemy kills have a small chance to drop skin crates
- Boss kills (every 5th wave) have higher drop rates
- Faint Memory Mode: 33% chance to drop Strange Fragments from completion
- 10,000 Strange Fragments can be exchanged for a guaranteed Mythic skin of your choice

### Method 3: Redeem Codes

- `100MVisit2` — Random 100M skin (Uptake, Tackleman, or others)
- `CamArmada` — 2,000 Gems (used for skin crate purchases)
- New codes released for milestones and events — check the official Discord regularly

### Method 4: Mastery Rewards

Each character has a Mastery track (1-100). Key milestones:
- Mastery 30: Small stat boosts
- Mastery 50: Moderate stat boosts + cosmetic
- Mastery 80: **Villain Arc Mythic skin** (Large Cam only)
- Mastery 100: Titles and bragging rights

## Optimal Team Compositions

Blockade Battlefront supports up to 4 players per server. A coordinated team with complementary roles survives 2-3x longer than four solo players with identical loadouts.

### The Standard Comp (Waves 1-40)

| Role | Character | Weapon | Titan | Responsibility |
|------|-----------|--------|-------|----------------|
| **Tank** | Large Cam | Minigun | Titan Cameraman | Front-line, absorb damage, hold aggro |
| **AoE** | Large TV Man | Bazooka | Speaker Titan | Clear swarm waves, AoE damage |
| **Flex** | Dark Speakerman | G36K → Minigun | Jetpack Double Plunger | Mobile damage, flank routes |
| **Support** | Speaker Cam | Minigun | Titan Cameraman | Buff team damage, cover flanks |

### The Late-Game Comp (Waves 40-70)

| Role | Character | Weapon | Titan | Responsibility |
|------|-----------|--------|-------|----------------|
| **Main Tank** | Energize TV Man (Titan Replica) | Minigun | Utam | Boss focus-fire, main damage soak |
| **AoE/Clear** | Large Cam (Villain Arc) | Bazooka | Jetpack Double Plunger | Wave clear, 18k F-skill nukes |
| **Mobile DPS** | Dark Speakerman (MYTHICAL) | Minigun + Bazooka | Titan Cameraman | Rotate lanes, burst priority targets |
| **Flex/Backup** | Jetpack Plunger Man (Hahoraga) | G36K | Speaker Titan | Sustain, revive teammates, kite enemies |

### The God Comp (Waves 70+ / Wave 90)

| Role | Character | Weapon | Titan |
|------|-----------|--------|-------|
| **Primary** | Uptake Titan (100M) | Titan weapons | Utam |
| **Secondary** | Tackleman (100M) | Titan weapons | Jetpack Double Plunger |
| **Tertiary** | Dark Speakerman (MYTHICAL) | Bazooka | Titan Cameraman |
| **Quaternary** | Energize TV Man (Titan Replica) | Minigun | Speaker Titan |

At this level, coordination with voice comms becomes essential — call out boss spawns, announce when Titans are about to die, and focus-fire priority targets together.

## Character Progression Roadmap

### Phase 1: Learning (Waves 1-15)
- **Use**: Default Cameraman
- **Goal**: Learn keybinds, wave patterns, helicopter timing
- **Don't**: Spend Cen on character unlocks yet — save for weapons first

### Phase 2: Foundation (Waves 15-25)
- **Unlock**: Large Cam (required for Bazooka at wave 25)
- **Weapon**: G36K → Minigun at wave 15
- **Goal**: Consistent survival to wave 25 without deaths

### Phase 3: Power Spike (Waves 25-40)
- **Main**: Large Cam with best available skin
- **Weapon**: Minigun + Bazooka (swap based on wave type)
- **Goal**: Start farming Cen efficiently, look for skin drops

### Phase 4: Specialization (Waves 40-70)
- **Pick a main**: Dark Speakerman, Energize TV Man, or Jetpack Plunger Man
- **Target a Mythic skin**: Save Strange Fragments or use codes
- **Goal**: Acquire and upgrade your first Mythic-tier skin

### Phase 5: Endgame (Waves 70+)
- **Main**: Any 100M skin character or MYTHICAL Dark Speakerman
- **Titans**: Full roster of 3 Titans rotating
- **Goal**: Clear wave 90 for the Burning Flame item
