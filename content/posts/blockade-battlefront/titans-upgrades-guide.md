Once you understand the basic wave loop in ST: Blockade Battlefront, the difference between dying at wave 20 and surviving past wave 50 comes down to two things: **what you buy** and **when you buy it**.

This guide covers every Titan ranked, the weapon progression path, Cen farming optimization, and the upgrade priority that takes you from mid-game survivor to endgame carry.

---

## Titan Tier List

Titans are the most powerful units in the game, with HP and damage that dwarf standard characters. You deploy them at the start of waves to tank, deal damage, and control lanes.

### S-Tier: Must-Buy

| Titan | Cost | Best For | Notes |
|-------|------|----------|-------|
| **Titan Cameraman** | Moderate | All content, waves 10-50 | Best HP-to-cost ratio in the game. Your first Titan, full stop. |
| **Jetpack Double Plunger** | High | Mobile damage, waves 25+ | Combines mobility with high burst damage. Harder to hit, covers more lanes. |
| **Utam** | Very High | Boss waves, waves 40+ | Highest single-target damage. Requires Titan access via Speaker or Titan Requests. |

### A-Tier: Strong Situational Picks

| Titan | Cost | Best For | Notes |
|-------|------|----------|-------|
| Large Cam | High | AoE crowd control | Great for swarm waves (31-40), but expensive for what it offers early |
| Speaker Titan | Moderate | Support, buffs | Buffs nearby units' damage. Pairs well with Titan Cameraman. |

### B-Tier: Niche Use

| Titan | Cost | Best For | Notes |
|-------|------|----------|-------|
| TV Titan | Moderate | Area denial | Locks down one lane effectively, but useless on multi-lane maps |
| Camera Strider | Low | Early filler | Cheap but squishy. Dies fast past wave 25. |

### C-Tier: Skip

| Titan | Notes |
|-------|-------|
| Basic Strider | Outclassed by Titan Cameraman in every metric |
| Wall Titan | Too slow, too expensive, dies to focus fire |

## Weapon Progression Path

### The Optimal Buy Order

| Wave | Purchase | Cen Cost | Rationale |
|------|----------|----------|-----------|
| 5 | **G36K** | Low | Reliable, accurate, carries you through wave 15 |
| 10 | **Titan Cameraman** | Moderate | Game-changing power spike |
| 15 | **Minigun** | Moderate | Higher DPS, large magazine, better for sustained fights |
| 20 | Save / Ammo reserves | — | Save Cen for next major purchase |
| 25 | **Bazooka** | High | AoE damage essential for wave 31-40 swarms |
| 30 | Second Titan | Moderate-High | Jetpack Double Plunger or Speaker Titan |
| 35+ | Ammo + Titan refresh | Variable | Keep Titans alive, maintain ammo stock |

### Weapons to Avoid Early

| Weapon | Why to Skip |
|--------|------------|
| Sniper variants | Too slow for wave pacing, low DPS |
| Pistol upgrades | Weak damage, waste of Cen |
| Melee weapons | You take too much damage in melee range |
| Any "cool-looking" expensive weapon | Flashy ≠ effective. Stick to the path. |

## Cen Farming: The Complete Strategy

Cen is the currency for everything — Titans, weapons, ammo, character unlocks. Optimizing your Cen income is the single biggest factor in progression speed.

### Active Farming: Waves 25-50 Sweet Spot

Waves 25 through 50 produce more Cen per minute than any other range:

| Wave Range | Cen/Minute | Difficulty | Recommendation |
|------------|-----------|------------|----------------|
| 1-10 | Very Low | Very Easy | Learning phase, minimal farming |
| 11-24 | Low-Medium | Easy-Medium | Decent, but not optimal |
| **25-50** | **Highest** | **Medium-High** | **Farm here** |
| 51-70 | High | High-Very High | Good if your server can handle it |
| 70+ | Very High | Extreme | Only in fully coordinated servers |

### Server Selection Strategy

A strong server is worth more than any individual upgrade:

1. Join a server and play through wave 15
2. Observe: are players using the helicopter? Are Titans being deployed? Is the team coordinated?
3. If yes → stay and push for high waves
4. If no → leave after wave 15 and find a new server

A coordinated team of 4+ players with Titans will earn 3-5× more Cen per hour than a disorganized server.

### Passive AFK Farming

For passive Cen income while away from keyboard:

1. Position your character near **the Statue** in the lobby
2. Set up an auto-clicker (clicking every 1-2 seconds)
3. The Statue generates small but steady Cen over time
4. Leave running overnight or during work/school hours

This is slow (~10-20% of active farming rate) but adds up over days.

### Quest Cen Bonuses

Daily and weekly quests provide lump-sum Cen payouts. Prioritize quests that align with your farming:
- "Survive X waves" quests — stack with your active farming
- "Deploy X Titans" quests — deploy cheaper Titans if needed for quest credit
- "Eliminate X enemies" quests — naturally completed during farming

## Upgrade Priority for Late Game (Waves 40+)

Once you have your core loadout (Minigun + Bazooka + Titan Cameraman + one more Titan), your upgrade priority shifts:

| Priority | Upgrade | Why |
|----------|---------|-----|
| **1** | Titan health/repair | A dead Titan provides zero value. Keep them alive. |
| **2** | Ammo capacity | Running out of ammo during wave 47 is a death sentence |
| **3** | Titan damage upgrades | More damage = faster wave clears = less damage taken |
| **4** | Character health/armor | You're squishy. Reducing incoming damage helps. |
| **5** | Speed/mobility | Positioning matters more late-game |

## Character Unlock Guide

Additional characters unlock as you accumulate Cen. Each has different stat spreads and skill sets:

| Character | Unlock Cost | Playstyle |
|-----------|------------|-----------|
| Default Cam | Free | Balanced stats, basic skills |
| Large Cam | Moderate | Higher HP, can use heavy weapons (Bazooka) |
| Speaker Cam | High | Support-oriented, buffs nearby allies |
| Jetpack Cam | High | High mobility, aerial combat skills |

Unlock **Large Cam** before wave 25 — it's required for the Bazooka, which is essential for swarm waves. Jetpack Cam is a luxury unlock for experienced players who want aerial repositioning.

## Optimal Loadout Summary

### Waves 1-25 (Early-Mid Game)
- **Weapon**: G36K → Minigun at wave 15
- **Titan**: Titan Cameraman at wave 10
- **Character**: Default Cam → Large Cam before wave 25
- **Goal**: Consistent survival with no deaths

### Waves 26-50 (Late Game)
- **Weapons**: Minigun + Bazooka (swap based on wave type)
- **Titans**: Titan Cameraman + Jetpack Double Plunger
- **Character**: Large Cam
- **Goal**: Carry your weight in team damage, keep Titans alive

### Waves 50+ (Endgame)
- **Weapons**: Minigun + Bazooka (always)
- **Titans**: Titan Cameraman + Jetpack Double Plunger + Utam
- **Character**: Large Cam or Jetpack Cam (preference)
- **Goal**: Coordinated Titan rotation, boss focus-fire, survival
