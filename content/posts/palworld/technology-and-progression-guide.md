## How the Technology Tree Works

The Technology tree is the primary progression system in Palworld. As you gain levels, you earn Technology Points (TP) that unlock new blueprints for crafting.

### Technology Points

| Source | Points Gained | Notes |
|--------|--------------|-------|
| Leveling up | 1 TP per level (50 total) | Primary source -- always spend immediately |
| Technical Manual | 1 TP per manual | Found in dungeons and some bosses |
| Ancient Technology | Separate track | Uses Ancient Civilization Parts, not TP |

### How to Spend TP

Open the Technology menu (default key: Tab, then select Technology). Each unlock costs 1 or 2 TP. You cannot respec -- plan your unlocks carefully.

**Critical rule:** You get exactly 50 TP from leveling (50 levels). There are more technologies than 50 TP. Choose wisely.

**Optional technologies to skip:**
- Stone-tier tools (skip if you can go straight to metal)
- Low-tier armor if you can reach the next tier quickly
- Decorative items (chairs, tables, etc.)
- Early Sphere types once you unlock the next tier

## Ancient Technology Tree

Ancient Technology is a separate tree that uses **Ancient Civilization Parts** instead of Technology Points.

### How Ancient Parts Work

| Source | Parts Dropped |
|--------|--------------|
| Tower Bosses (first clear) | 5-10 per boss |
| Alpha Bosses | 2-16 per kill (scales with boss level) |
| Dungeon Bosses | 2-6 per run |
| Legendary Pals | 8-16 per kill |

### Ancient Technology Unlocks (Priority Order)

| Priority | Technology | Ancient Parts Required | Level Required | Why |
|----------|-----------|----------------------|---------------|-----|
| 1 | **Grappling Gun** | 5 | 12 | Best mobility tool, saves from falls, fast travel |
| 2 | **Pal Accelerator** | 10 | 17 | Increases base work speed |
| 3 | **Pal Summoning Staff** | 5 | 20 | Aim Pals at specific targets |
| 4 | **Monitor Stand** | 10 | 22 | View Pal stats in base |
| 5 | **Electric Egg Incubator** | 20 | 30 | No temperature requirements for hatching |
| 6 | **Frostallion Saddle** | 20 | 46 | Ride Frostallion |
| 7 | **Jetragon Saddle** | 20 | 50 | Ride Jetragon |
| 8 | **Paladius Saddle** | 20 | 48 | Ride Paladius |
| 9 | **Necromus Saddle** | 20 | 49 | Ride Necromus |
| 10 | **Homing Missile Launcher** | 50 | 44 | Highest damage weapon |

**Ancient Part farming priority:** Grappling Gun (first) -> Pal Accelerator -> Egg Incubator upgrade -> Legendary saddles.

## Statue of Power and Lifmunk Effigies

The **Statue of Power** (unlocked at level 16) allows you to upgrade your capture power and player stats using Lifmunk Effigies.

### How It Works

| Mechanic | Details |
|----------|---------|
| Statue unlock | Technology level 16 (costs 1 TP) |
| Build cost | 20 Stone, 10 Paldium, 10 Ingot |
| Upgrade resource | Lifmunk Effigies (found glowing green around the world) |
| Stats upgraded | Capture Power (primary), Player Defense, Player Max Work Speed |
| Max capture power | Level 10 capture power (requires ~50-60 total effigies) |

### Upgrade Breakdown

| Capture Level | Effigies Required (Cumulative) | Effect |
|--------------|-------------------------------|--------|
| 1 | 4 | Slight capture power increase |
| 2 | 8 (12 total) | Noticeable improvement |
| 3 | 12 (24 total) | Good capture rate on equal-level Pals |
| 4 | 16 (40 total) | Strong capture rate |
| 5 | 20 (60 total) | Very strong |
| 6 | 24 (84 total) | High efficiency with any sphere |
| 7 | 28 (112 total) | Excellent capture chance |
| 8 | 32 (144 total) | Near-max effectiveness |
| 9 | 36 (180 total) | Minimal difficulty catching anything |
| 10 | 40+ (220+ total) | Maximum capture power |

### Player Stat Upgrades

The Statue also allows stat point allocation from catching 10 of each Pal species:

| Bonus | Requirement | Effect |
|-------|-------------|--------|
| +1 Defense | Catch 10 of any Pal species | Permanent defense increase |
| +1 Attack | Catch 10 of any Pal species | Permanent attack increase |
| +2 Work Speed | Catch 10 of any Pal species | Permanent work speed increase |

**Strategy:** Catch 10 of EVERY species you encounter. The stat bonuses from the Statue of Power add up significantly -- catching all available Pals to 10 can give you +50+ to each stat permanently.

### Lifmunk Effigy Farming Tips

1. **Best time to farm:** Night -- effigies glow bright green and are visible from far away.
2. **Best method:** Fly slowly on a flying mount (Nitewing, Faleris, Frostallion) at low altitude.
3. **Key locations:** Cliff edges, behind waterfalls, inside caves, on rock pillars.
4. **Tracker:** There is no in-game tracker for effigies. Mark them on your map manually.
5. **Drop rate:** Each region has approximately 10-15 effigies. Total on the map: ~150+.

**Effigy route for quick 30 effigies (see [Pal Locations Guide](../palworld/pal-locations-guide.md) for detailed area maps):**
1. Starting beach area (5 effigies)
2. Grassy Behemoth Hills cliffs (4 effigies)
3. Fort Ruins area (3 effigies)
4. Small Settlement to Rayne Tower (4 effigies)
5. Desolate Church area (3 effigies)
6. Eastern islands (6 effigies)
7. Sealed Realm entrance (5 effigies)

## Progression Roadmap: Level 1 to 50

### Phase 1: Stone Age (Levels 1-9, ~2-4 hours)

**Goal:** Establish first base, automate berry production, catch 10 of every Grasslands Pal.

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Build Palbox and basic shelter
- [ ] Build Berry Plantation
- [ ] Catch Lamball, Cattiva, Chikipi, Foxsparks, Tanzee
- [ ] Build Bed and Campfire
- [ ] Reach level 9 (catch 10 of each Pal for XP)
- [ ] Build Furnace
- [ ] Equip Bow and Torch
- [ ] Unlock Parachute

### Phase 2: Iron Age (Levels 10-19, ~4-8 hours)

**Goal:** Unlock second base, set up ore mining, start breeding.

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Build second base near ore deposits
- [ ] Unlock and build Ranch, Hot Spring
- [ ] Build Statue of Power -- invest first effigies
- [ ] Complete Rayne Tower (Zoe & Grizzbolt)
- [ ] Craft Crossbow and Pelt Armor
- [ ] Build Refined Furnace
- [ ] Catch or breed a Nitewing for flying
- [ ] Build Breeding Pen (level 19) -- see the [Breeding Guide](../palworld/breeding-guide.md) for combos and passive inheritance
- [ ] Start producing cakes (Ranch setup)

### Phase 3: Industrial Revolution (Levels 20-34, ~8-15 hours)

**Goal:** Breed Anubis, unlock guns, build Breeding Pen, tackle Lily & Lyleen.

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Breed Anubis (Rushoar + Nitewing method)
- [ ] Build Pal Essence Condenser
- [ ] Upgrade Palbox to level 3 (15 Pals)
- [ ] Unlock Wheat Plantation, start bread production
- [ ] Defeat Lily & Lyleen (level 25+)
- [ ] Craft Handgun
- [ ] Build Weapon Workbench
- [ ] Unlock third base
- [ ] Build base in desert or coal-rich area
- [ ] Catch Faleris for fast flying (level 35 saddle)

### Phase 4: Endgame Prep (Levels 35-44, ~15-25 hours)

**Goal:** Electric Furnace, Salad, Metal Armor, tackle Axel & Orserk and Marcus & Faleris.

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Build Electric Furnace
- [ ] Unlock and craft Salad for worker food
- [ ] Craft Metal Armor (upgrade as you go)
- [ ] Defeat Axel & Orserk (level 35-40)
- [ ] Defeat Marcus & Faleris (level 40-44)
- [ ] Craft Assault Rifle
- [ ] Start farming Ancient Parts (Alpha bosses)
- [ ] Catch/breed Jormuntide and Jormuntide Ignis
- [ ] Upgrade Palbox to level 4 (17 Pals)

### Phase 5: Endgame (Levels 45-50, ~25-40 hours)

**Goal:** Defeat Victor & Shadowbeak, capture all four Legendary Pals, craft endgame gear.

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Defeat Victor & Shadowbeak at the Pal Genetic Research Tower
- [ ] Capture Frostallion (prepare Fire Pals and heat-resistant... cold-resistant armor)
- [ ] Capture Jetragon (prepare ranged weapons, cold... heat-resistant armor for volcano)
- [ ] Capture Paladius + Necromus duo (bring Dark + Dragon Pals)
- [ ] Craft Pal Metal Armor (best in game)
- [ ] Craft Ultimate/Legendary Spheres
- [ ] Craft Rocket Launcher
- [ ] Build Assembly Line II
- [ ] Upgrade Palbox to level 5 (20 Pals)
- [ ] Max out Statue of Power (all effigies)
- [ ] Upgrade favorite Pals to 4 stars via Pal Essence Condenser

## Technology Point Optimization

You get exactly 49-50 TP from leveling (level 50). Here is the optimized spend for maximum efficiency:

**Essential unlocks (37 TP):**
- Base critical: Palbox, Bed, Berry Plantation, Ranch, Hot Spring, Statue of Power, Breeding Pen (7 TP)
- Crafting: Workbench (free), Cooking Pot, Mill, Electric Furnace, Refined Furnace, Weapon Workbench (5 TP)
- Production: Assembly Line I, Assembly Line II, Production Assembly Line, Sphere Factory (4 TP)
- Capture: Mega Sphere, Giga Sphere, Hyper Sphere, Ultra Sphere, Legendary Sphere (5 TP)
- Combat: Bow, Crossbow, Handgun, Assault Rifle, Rocket Launcher (5 TP)
- Armor: Cloth Outfit, Pelt Armor, Metal Armor, Pal Metal Armor (4 TP)
- Pal care: Pal Essence Condenser, Low Grade Medical Supplies, High Grade Medical Supplies (3 TP)
- Utility: Grappling Gun (Ancient, not TP), Parachute, Large Chest, Cooler Box (3 TP)

**Optional (remaining 12+ TP):**
- Salad/Pizza food technologies (2 TP)
- Wheat Plantation, Bee Hive (2 TP)
- High Quality Hot Spring, Premium Pal Bed (2 TP)
- Metal structures (1 TP)
- Additional weapon types (Makeshift Handgun, Musket) (2 TP)
- Mounted defenses, cosmetics, lower-tier items you skipped
