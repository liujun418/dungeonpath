## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do trails and sword skins affect gameplay?

No. All cosmetics in Blade Ball are purely visual. They do not change hitboxes, ability cooldowns, blocking mechanics, or any other gameplay element. Your performance depends entirely on your skill, not your cosmetics.

### What is the best trail in Blade Ball?

The Phoenix Trail and Void Trail are considered the best due to their rarity and visual uniqueness. However, the "best" trail is subjective and depends on your personal aesthetic preference.

### How do I get the SPARKLERR Blade?

The SPARKLERR Blade was released through the 5BVISITS code. Enter this code in the game's code redemption menu if the code is still active. Codes expire periodically, so check the Blade Ball Wiki or community Discord for current code status.

### Can I trade cosmetics with other players?

Blade Ball does not currently have a trading system. Cosmetics are bound to your account once unlocked. There is no way to give or trade items to other players.

### Are event cosmetics coming back?

Seasonal event cosmetics (Fire Blade, Ice Blade) typically return when their respective events recur. Milestone cosmetics (SPARKLERR Blade, Bubble Wand Sword) are unlikely to return because they were tied to specific player count milestones.

### What is the most expensive cosmetic in Blade Ball?

The Void Blade and Void Trail are among the most expensive, requiring tournament placement or very high-level achievements. They cannot be purchased with coins directly.

### How many trails are there in total?

There are approximately 12+ known trails, including the default trail, purchasable trails, event-exclusive trails, and code-unlocked trails. New trails are added during events and updates.

### How many sword skins are there in total?

There are approximately 15+ known sword skins, ranging from the default blade to event-exclusive and code-exclusive designs. New sword skins are added during events and milestone celebrations.

### Should I spend coins on cosmetics or abilities?

Prioritize abilities over cosmetics. Abilities directly affect your gameplay performance, while cosmetics are purely visual. Once you have a strong ability loadout (Infinity, Shadow Step, etc.), invest remaining coins in cosmetics. See our [Abilities Tier List](/posts/blade-ball/abilities-tier-list/) for purchase recommendations.

### How do I equip cosmetics?

Go to the customization menu in the lobby. Select the trail or sword skin you want to equip. Your selection will be saved and applied to all future matches until you change it.

### Are there any free cosmetics?

Yes. Code redemptions can unlock free cosmetics like the Bubble Wand Sword and SPARKLERR Blade. Additionally, the default trail and default blade are available to all players from the start.

### Will there be more maps in the future?

Based on the historical pattern of annual map updates, it is likely that a new map will be released in 2027. The developers may also introduce themed map variants for special events.
