## Why Collect Cosmetics in Blade Ball?

Cosmetics in **Blade Ball** serve one primary purpose: **making your character stand out**. They do not provide any gameplay advantage, but they are a symbol of your dedication, skill, and participation in the game's events.

When you see a player on the leaderboard with a rare trail and a legendary sword skin, you know they have invested significant time and effort into the game. Cosmetics are the visual language of achievement in Blade Ball.

This comprehensive guide covers every cosmetic category in Blade Ball, how to unlock them, their rarity rankings, and tips for building an impressive cosmetic collection.

## Wheel Spin Rewards and Odds

The wheel spin (also called the lucky spin or prize wheel) is a primary way to obtain rare and legendary cosmetics.

### How the Wheel Spin Works

1. Earn or purchase **spin tokens**
2. Open the **Wheel Spin** menu in the lobby
3. Click **Spin** to activate the wheel
4. The wheel lands on a random cosmetic item
5. If you receive a duplicate, you get a small coin refund instead

### Wheel Spin Odds Table

| Rarity | Approximate Odds | Example Rewards |
|--------|-----------------|-----------------|
| **Common** | 50-60% | Spark Trail, Dust Trail, Default variants |
| **Uncommon** | 20-25% | Fire Trail, Ice Trail, Silver Edge |
| **Rare** | 10-15% | Neon Blade, Dragon Trail, Void Edge |
| **Epic** | 4-7% | Phoenix Blade, Cosmic Trail, Galaxy Edge |
| **Legendary** | 1-2% | Excalibur, Divine Trail, Darkness Reaver |
| **Ultra-Rare** | 0.5-1% | Genesis Trail (if available), limited exclusives |

### How to Get Spin Tokens

| Method | Tokens Earned | Notes |
|--------|--------------|-------|
| **Win a match** | 1 token | Primary free method |
| **Daily login streak** | 1-3 tokens | Increases with consecutive days |
| **Achievement completion** | 2-5 tokens | Complete milestones like "50 wins" |
| **Purchase with Robux** | Varies | Direct purchase from the shop |
| **Event participation** | 3-10 tokens | Special event rewards |
| **Code redemption** | 1-2 tokens | Occasionally included in promotional codes — check our [Codes Guide](/posts/blade-ball/codes-guide/) for active codes |

> **Pro Tip:** Save your spin tokens for **special event wheels** that feature exclusive cosmetics. Regular wheels are available anytime, but event wheels rotate and may not return.

### How to Redeem Cosmetic Codes

1. Launch **Blade Ball** on Roblox
2. Look for the **Codes** or **Twitter/X** icon in the lobby (usually on the left or right side of the screen)
3. Click the icon to open the code entry window
4. Type or paste the code exactly as shown
5. Click **Redeem** or **Submit**
6. If the code is valid and active, your cosmetic will be added to your inventory
7. Check your **Inventory** to equip the new cosmetic

> **Important:** Codes have expiration dates and usage limits. Redeem them as soon as possible. A code that worked for one player may not work for another if it has reached its redemption cap. ## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I trade cosmetics with other players?

No, Blade Ball does not currently have a player-to-player trading system. All cosmetics are bound to your account.

### Do cosmetics affect gameplay?

No. All cosmetics are purely visual and do not provide any stat advantages, damage boosts, or speed increases. They are strictly for appearance.

### How do I equip a cosmetic after unlocking it?

Open the **Inventory** or **Cosmetics** menu from the lobby, select the cosmetic you want to use, and click **Equip**. Only one sword skin, one trail, and one ability effect can be active at a time.

### What happens if I get a duplicate from the wheel spin?

Duplicates are converted to a small **coin refund** instead of adding a second copy to your inventory. The refund amount depends on the rarity of the item.

### Are event cosmetics ever re-released?

Some event cosmetics return in future events, but others are truly limited-time and never come back. **Genesis Trail** and similar season-exclusive items have not been re-released.

### Can I use Robux to buy cosmetics directly?

Some cosmetics can be purchased with Robux through the game's shop, but most are earned with in-game coins or through events and the wheel spin.

### How many sword skins are in Blade Ball?

There are **20+ sword skins** across all rarity tiers, with more added through events and updates. The exact number grows with each game update.

### What is the rarest cosmetic in Blade Ball?

The **Genesis Trail** is widely considered the rarest cosmetic. It was only available during Season 1 and has not been re-released.

### Can I preview cosmetics before buying them?

Yes, most cosmetics have a **preview** button in the shop that lets you see the cosmetic on your character before purchasing.

### How do I get the Phoenix Blade and Phoenix Trail set?

The **Phoenix Blade** can be purchased for 3,500 coins or obtained through the wheel spin. The **Phoenix Trail** can be purchased for 2,500 coins or obtained through the wheel spin. They are sold separately.
