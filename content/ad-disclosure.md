## Your Choices

You have several options regarding the ads you see on our Site:

1. **Opt out of personalized ads**: Visit [Google Ads Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads) to manage your ad preferences
2. **Opt out of third-party vendor cookies**: Visit [www.aboutads.info](https://www.aboutads.info/choices/) to opt out of third-party vendor use of cookies for interest-based advertising
3. **Use browser controls**: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies, though this may affect site functionality
4. **Use an ad blocker**: You may choose to use ad-blocking software, though this impacts our ability to support the site
