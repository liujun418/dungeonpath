Advertising on DungeonPath#

DungeonPath.com displays advertisements to help support the cost of creating and maintaining our free guides and content.

Google AdSense#

We use Google AdSense, a third-party advertising service provided by Google LLC, to display advertisements on our website.

How It Works#

  • Google AdSense uses cookies and web technologies to serve ads based on your visits to our website and other websites on the internet
  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user’s prior visits to our website and/or other websites
  • The data collected is used solely for the purpose of delivering relevant advertisements to you

Data Collection#

When you view or click on ads on our Site, the following may occur:

  • Your IP address may be logged
  • Information about your browser and device may be collected
  • Browsing behavior may be tracked to improve ad relevance

For more details on what data is collected and how it is used, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

Your Choices#

You have several options regarding the ads you see on our Site:

  1. Opt out of personalized ads: Visit Google Ads Settings to manage your ad preferences
  2. Opt out of third-party vendor cookies: Visit www.aboutads.info to opt out of third-party vendor use of cookies for interest-based advertising
  3. Use browser controls: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies, though this may affect site functionality
  4. Use an ad blocker: You may choose to use ad-blocking software, though this impacts our ability to support the site

Affiliate Links#

Some links on DungeonPath may be affiliate links. This means that if you click on the link and make a purchase, we may receive a commission at no additional cost to you. We only recommend products and services that we believe may be of value to our readers.

Transparency#

We are committed to being transparent about our advertising practices. The advertisements displayed on our Site:

  • Are served by third-party ad networks
  • May be based on your browsing history and interests
  • Do not influence the content or opinions expressed in our guides
  • Are clearly distinguishable from our editorial content
