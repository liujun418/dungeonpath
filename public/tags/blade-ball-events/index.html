Blade Ball Battle Pass & Events Guide: All Seasons, Limited Items & LTMs (2026)

Battle Pass System Overview Blade Ball runs a seasonal battle pass system with new passes launching roughly every 30-40 days. Each pass features a free track and a premium track with exclusive abilities, sword skins, explosion effects, and limited cosmetics.
