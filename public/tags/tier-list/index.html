Dash Mobility Free The starting ability is already one of the best. Extremely fast repositioning, short cooldown, and works in every game mode. Don’t underestimate it. When activated, if the ball hits an opponent, it can eliminate them instantly. Game-changing in clutch 1v1 situations. Lightning Dash Mobility ~2,500 coins Faster version of Dash with improved range and a visual trail. Slightly better than regular Dash but costs coins. Force Field Defense ~1,200 coins Strong defensive shield that blocks the ball for a short duration. More affordable alternative to Infinity with slightly less coverage. A Tier: Strong and Reliable Ability Type Cost Why It’s A Super Jump Mobility ~1,000 coins Launches you high into the air, completely avoiding the ball. Good for escaping tight situations but leaves you vulnerable on landing. Platform Utility ~1,000 coins Creates a platform you can jump on to avoid the ball. Solid utility but requires positioning skill. Raging Deflect Offense/Defense ~2,000 coins Powerful counter-attack ability that reflects the ball with extra speed. Great for aggressive players. Gale’s Edge Offense Event New ability introduced in 2026. Strong offensive capability with good range. Still being fully evaluated by the community. B Tier: Situational but Useful Ability Type Cost Notes Teleport Mobility ~800 coins Teleports you to a random location. Useful when cornered but unpredictable. Blink Mobility ~600 coins Short-range teleport. Similar to Shadow Step but with less range and no invincibility frames. Shield Defense ~500 coins Basic block ability. Outclassed by Infinity and Force Field but affordable for beginners. Speed Boost Mobility ~700 coins Temporary speed increase. Helpful for repositioning but doesn’t directly help with blocking. Time Slow Utility ~1,500 coins Slows time briefly, giving you more reaction time. Niche but useful for learning. C Tier: Outclassed Ability Type Cost Why It’s C Fire Step Mobility Event Flashy but mechanically similar to Blink. Visual upgrade, not mechanical. Ice Shield Defense Event Weaker than standard Shield. Limited availability makes it hard to recommend. Tornado Utility ~1,200 coins Creates a tornado that can deflect the ball. Unreliable and slow activation. D Tier: Avoid Ability Type Cost Why It’s D Basic Block Defense Free Your default ability if you have nothing else equipped. Completely outclassed by every other option. TDS Tower Tier List Overview Tower Defense Simulator has dozens of towers, and the 2026 balance update shifted the meta significantly. This tier list ranks every tower based on overall performance across Normal, Hardcore, and Fallen modes. Quick Reference: Tier Definitions Tier Meaning S+ Game-defining. Essential for any serious strategy S Top-tier. Core of most winning loadouts A Strong. Viable in all modes with good placement B Situational. Good in specific maps or compositions C Niche. Only useful in easy modes or early game D Avoid. Outperformed by almost every other option S+ Tier: Game-Defining Towers Tower Type Cost Why It’s S+ Accelerator Damage High Highest DPS in the game. No tower comes close to its raw damage output. Essential for every late-game composition. Placement limit makes it even more valuable. Commander Buffer ~2,500 The best buffer in TDS. A max-level Commander increases nearby tower damage and fire rate dramatically. Every serious loadout runs at least one. Golden Pyromancer Damage/AoE Event Top golden tower with infinite burn times, exceptional range, and massive crowd control. Even after burn immunity became more common, its raw DPS keeps it relevant. S Tier: Core Meta Towers Tower Type Cost Why It’s S Electroshocker Damage/Stun ~2,000 Often preferred over Golden Pyro now that many enemies have burn immunity. Stuns groups, deals consistent damage, and has excellent area denial. Golden Scout Damage Event Best early-to-mid game tower post-2026 update. Fast fire rate, scales well with upgrades, and transitions smoothly into late game. Golden Cowboy Damage Event Consistent high DPS throughout the entire game. No range issues, reliable damage output, and excellent value. Rocketeer Damage High Massive AoE damage. The placement limit of 5 is its only real drawback. When you can place them, they melt boss waves. DJ Buffer Medium Second-best buffer after Commander. Increases fire rate for nearby towers. Often paired with Commander for maximum buff stacking. A Tier: Strong and Reliable Tower Type Cost Why It’s A Pyromancer Damage/AoE ~1,500 Excellent crowd control with burn damage. Less effective against burn-immune enemies, but still a staple for most compositions. Mortar Damage/AoE ~2,000 Great cluster damage for grouped enemies. Recently nerfed in the 2026 balance update but still strong with good early game presence. Freezer Utility ~1,800 Slows enemies significantly. Essential for Hardcore mode where enemy speed can overwhelm your defenses. Less needed in Normal mode. Golden Crook Boss Damage Event Strong damage with good range. Solid choice for any composition. Golden Minigunner Damage Event High fire rate, good DPS. Slightly outclassed by Golden Cowboy but still very viable. Golden Soldier Damage Event Reliable damage dealer with decent range and fire rate. Ace Pilot Damage Shop Excellent early game DPS post-2026 buff. Good range for a starter tower. B Tier: Situational but Useful Tower Type Cost Notes Sniper Damage Medium Highest single-shot damage, but slow fire rate. Good against bosses, poor against groups. War Machine Damage/AoE High Strong late-game tower with good AoE. Expensive to upgrade fully. Brawler Damage Shop Strong close-range damage, affordable. Good early game but falls off late. Assassin Damage Shop High single-target damage. Great against bosses but limited AoE. Electrician Buffer Medium Alternative buffer tower. Less impactful than Commander/DJ but still useful. Supply Drop Economy Medium Generates coins over time. Good if you need extra economy but outperformed by Farm in most situations. Hunter Damage Medium Good damage against single targets. Decent for mid-game. C Tier: Early Game Only Tower Type Cost Notes Scout Damage Free Your starting tower. Replace ASAP with Golden Scout or Ace Pilot. Farm Economy Low Generates passive income. Useful but doesn’t contribute to damage. Gunner Damage Low Decent early damage, completely outclassed mid-game. Pursuer Damage Medium Average stats across the board. Nothing special. Demoman Damage/AoE Medium Explosive damage but unreliable targeting. Ranger Damage Medium Decent range, mediocre damage. D Tier: Avoid Tower Type Cost Why It’s D Melee Damage Free Shortest range in the game. Enemies often bypass before it can attack. Paintballer Damage Low Outclassed by every other damage tower. Turret Damage Low Very slow fire rate, low damage. Early game only.
