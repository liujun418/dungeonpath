No range issues, reliable damage output, and excellent value. Rocketeer Damage High Massive AoE damage. The placement limit of 5 is its only real drawback. When you can place them, they melt boss waves. DJ Buffer Medium Second-best buffer after Commander. Increases fire rate for nearby towers. Often paired with Commander for maximum buff stacking.
