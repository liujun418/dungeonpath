Last updated: May 10, 2026. This tier list reflects the current Monster Legends meta after the Spring 2026 balance patch. Rankings are based on competitive PvP data, Team War performance, and Adventure Map utility.

How We Rank Monsters#

Each monster is evaluated across four categories and given an overall tier:

  • PvP Multiplayer — Performance in the ranked ladder against human opponents
  • Team Wars — Value in coordinated team battles and defense setups
  • Adventure Map — Efficiency at clearing PvE content
  • Dungeons — Performance in time-limited dungeon events

Tiers are defined as:

TierRatingDescription
SMeta-DefiningDominates their role; no real substitute. Must-have if obtainable.
AExcellentTop-tier performer; consistently strong in their niche.
BGoodSolid choice; effective but outclassed by higher tiers.
CNicheUsable in specific situations; not a generalist pick.
DOutclassedPower-crept by newer monsters; invest only if you have nothing better.
