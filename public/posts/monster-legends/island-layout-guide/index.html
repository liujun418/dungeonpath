Understanding Islands and Habitats#

Your islands are the foundation of your Monster Legends economy. Every habitat you place generates passive gold, and every monster you house contributes to your collection, breeding pool, and combat roster. A well-organized base produces 2-3x more gold than a haphazard one.

Space Efficiency Tips#

  • Habitats are 3x3 or 4x4. Plan your grid to fit them without gaps.
  • Don’t place temples on element islands. Temples on the main island free up space on dedicated islands for more habitats.
  • Group buildings by use frequency. The buildings you tap most (Breeding, Hatchery, Farms) should be closest to where your screen opens.
  • Leave paths between habitat blocks. Makes collecting gold easier without mis-tapping.

Daily Gold = (Sum of all habitat gold rates × Gold trait multipliers × Temple bonuses) × Collection frequency efficiency

Collection frequency efficiency:
- Collect every 2 hours: ~90% efficiency (near-perfect)
- Collect 3x per day: ~75% efficiency
- Collect 1x per day: ~50% efficiency (wasting half your potential gold)

Late-Game Base Organization#

When you have 8+ islands and 50+ habitats, organization becomes critical:

  1. Elemental islands (one per element): Fill each entirely with habitats of that element + its temple
  2. Overflow islands: For elements where you have more monsters than habitat slots
  3. Vanity island: Use one island for decoration and event buildings (they don’t need to be efficient)
  4. Operating island (Main): Breeding Mountain, Hatchery, Farms only — move all habitats to dedicated islands
