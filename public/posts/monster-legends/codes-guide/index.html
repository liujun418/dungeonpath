How to Redeem Codes in Monster Legends#

  1. Open Monster Legends on your device
  2. Tap the Settings gear icon (top-right corner)
  3. Look for the “Redeem Code” or “Promo Code” button
  4. Enter the code exactly as shown (codes are case-sensitive)
  5. Tap Confirm — your reward will appear in your inbox
  6. Claim rewards from your inbox within 24 hours or they expire

Code redemption troubleshooting:
