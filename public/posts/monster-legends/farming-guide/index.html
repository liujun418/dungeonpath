Why Farming Matters#

In Monster Legends, you can obtain most monsters for free through breeding and events. What limits your progress isn’t monster acquisition—it’s resources. You need millions of gold and food to level, rank up, and evolve your team. Players who farm efficiently progress 3-5x faster than those who don’t.

This guide covers the most efficient gold and food farming methods, organized from earliest available to endgame.

Part 1: Gold Farming#

Gold is required for: building upgrades, habitat purchases, ranking up monsters, temple upgrades, and island expansions. You’ll need hundreds of millions over your playthrough.

Part 2: Food Farming#

Food is the real bottleneck in Monster Legends. Leveling a single Mythic from 1 to 100 costs millions of food. Unlike gold, food has no passive generation—it must be actively farmed.

Food Farming Strategy by Play Style#

Casual (plays 1-2x per day):

  • Run the highest food node you can clear 3-5 times
  • Complete all daily missions
  • Use dungeon keys on Food Dungeon exclusively
  • Expected: 100,000 – 300,000 food per day

Active (plays 3-5x per day):

  • Farm food nodes whenever stamina is full
  • Prioritize food rewards in every event
  • Food Dungeon every time keys are available
  • Expected: 400,000 – 800,000 food per day

Hardcore (max efficiency):

  • Auto-farm best food node overnight with autoclicker or multi-run feature
  • Never let stamina cap
  • Buy extra dungeon keys with spare gems (only after all permanent upgrades)
  • Expected: 1,000,000 – 2,000,000+ food per day

Daily Checklist for Maximum Resources#

  • Collect all habitat gold (morning, midday, evening)
  • Clear all Adventure Map stamina on best food node
  • Complete all 5 daily missions
  • Run Food Dungeon if keys are available
  • Check Team War status and contribute attacks
  • Claim free shop rewards (daily freebie)
  • Feed your main team (don’t hoard food — applying levels earns back the investment)

Weekly Checklist#

  • Participate in active breeding/maze/race event
  • Complete Team War attacks (if war is active)
  • Upgrade temples using accumulated gold
  • Expand habitats if nearing gold cap too often
  • Review monster roster — sell duplicate Commons
