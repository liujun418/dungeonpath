How to Redeem Codes#

  1. Open Blade Ball in Roblox
  2. Look for the Twitter/X icon or Codes button on the side of the screen (usually on the right)
  3. Click it to open the code entry window
  4. Type or paste the code exactly as shown (codes are case-sensitive)
  5. Click Redeem to claim your reward

What Rewards Can You Get?#

Wheel Spins#

Wheel spins let you spin the in-game wheel for random rewards including:

  • Sword skins
  • Trails
  • Coins
  • Abilities (rare)
  • Emotes

Tips for Maximizing Code Rewards#

  1. Redeem immediately — codes can expire at any time
  2. Follow official social media — new codes are announced there first
  3. Don’t share codes publicly until you’ve redeemed them (they can hit use limits)
  4. Save wheel spins for when new items are added to the wheel

Frequently Asked Questions#

How often are new codes released?#

New codes are typically released during game updates, milestone celebrations (visits, likes), holidays, and special events. Expect 1-3 new codes per month.

Can I use codes multiple times?#

No. Each code can only be redeemed once per account.

Do codes work on all platforms?#

Yes, codes work on all platforms where Roblox is available (PC, mobile, Xbox, etc.).

What’s the best code reward?#

Wheel spins are the most valuable because they can give you rare items, including exclusive sword skins and trails.
