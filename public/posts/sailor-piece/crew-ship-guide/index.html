Overview of Crew and Ship Systems#

In Sailor Piece, your crew and ship are the backbone of your adventure. Your crewmates provide combat abilities, passive buffs, and special skills during sea encounters. Your ship determines your travel speed, cargo capacity, combat capability, and survivability in dangerous waters.

Managing both effectively is the difference between thriving and sinking in the Grand Line.
