What Are Devil Fruits in Sailor Piece?#

Devil Fruits are the cornerstone of power progression in Sailor Piece, the massively popular open-world sea exploration RPG on Roblox developed by Shadowrise Devs. These mystical fruits grant unique abilities that define your playstyle, from devastating elemental attacks to reality-bending powers.

With 50+ Devil Fruits currently in the game, choosing the right one is one of the most impactful decisions you will make. The fruit you pick determines your damage output, mobility, crowd control capabilities, and how you approach every boss fight and PvP encounter.

For detailed boss strategies including drop tables and recommended loadouts for every boss, see the Sailor Piece Boss Strategies Guide.

This guide covers every aspect of Devil Fruits in Sailor Piece as of May 2026: complete fruit lists organized by type and rarity, how to obtain each fruit, trading values, awakening mechanics, and a definitive tier list to help you make the best choice for your build.

Devil Fruit Types: Paramecia, Zoan, and Logia#

All Devil Fruits in Sailor Piece fall into three broad categories, each with distinct advantages and playstyles.

Paramecia Fruits#

Paramecia fruits grant supernatural abilities that alter your body, manipulate the environment, or produce substances. They are the most diverse category and make up the majority of fruits in the game.

FruitRarityAbility Type

InvisibleCommonStealth and invisibility
VenomEpicPoison damage over time

StringEpicThread-based attacks and traps
RubberUncommonElastic body and bounce attacks

Paramecia fruits excel at versatility. They offer a mix of damage, crowd control, and utility that makes them viable across all game modes. The top-tier Paramecia fruits like Quake and Dough can dominate both PvE and PvP.

Zoan Fruits#

Zoan fruits allow you to transform into animals or mythical creatures, granting enhanced physical stats, new attack patterns, and transformation-based abilities.

FruitRarityTransformation
DragonMythicalFull dragon form with fire breath
LeopardLegendaryFast feline form with claw attacks

PhoenixEpicFlying form with regeneration

ChimeraLegendaryMulti-form hybrid creature

Zoan fruits are best for Melee mains who want to amplify their physical damage output. The transformation adds bonus stats on top of your Melee and Defense investment, making Zoan users incredibly tanky and damaging in close combat.

Logia Fruits#

Logia fruits grant the power to create, control, and transform into natural elements. They are among the most powerful fruits in the game.

FruitRarityElement

MagmaB-RankMolten lava attacks

Logia fruits traditionally provide elemental immunity to physical attacks (though Armament Haki can counter this). They excel at area denial and sustained damage output.

What Rarity Really Means#

Higher rarity fruits have:

  • More ability slots (up to 5 moves for Mythical vs. 2 for Common)
  • Higher damage scaling with Power stat investment
  • Better AoE coverage for group combat and mob farming
  • Unique mechanics like flight, transformation, or elemental immunity
  • Higher trading value when you want to sell or swap

However, a well-played lower-tier fruit will outperform a poorly-used high-tier fruit. Focus on mastering your chosen fruit before worrying about upgrading.

Trading Tips to Avoid Getting Scammed#

  1. Always check current market values before trading — prices shift with updates
  2. Use the Trading Hub on Sailor Island where there are witnesses
  3. Never trade first with unknown players
  4. Be wary of “overpay” claims — if it sounds too good, it probably is
  5. Record your trades as evidence in case of disputes
  6. Know what you are trading for — do not accept unfamiliar items without researching their value
  7. Mythical fruits are NEVER worth trading for multiple lower-tier fruits — the rarity alone makes them more valuable long-term

FAQ#

Q: What is the best Devil Fruit for beginners? A: For beginners, any fruit from the Uncommon to Rare tier works fine early on. Bomb or Snow are excellent starting fruits because they have simple abilities and good AoE for grinding. Do not waste resources trying to spin for a Mythical fruit until you have leveled up and understand the game.

Q: Can I change my Devil Fruit after eating one? A: Yes. You can consume a different Devil Fruit, but you will lose the abilities of your previous fruit. There is no refund mechanism, so be certain before switching. Some players keep a backup fruit in their inventory for testing purposes.

Q: How do I increase my fruit mastery? A: Fruit mastery increases by using your fruit abilities in combat. Each ability use grants mastery XP. Boss fights and PvP provide more mastery XP than regular mob grinding. Aim to use all your abilities regularly to level them evenly.

Q: Are Mythical fruits worth the cost? A: Absolutely. Mythical fruits like Dragon, Fiend, and Kitsune are game-changers. They have more abilities, higher damage scaling, and unique mechanics that lower-tier fruits cannot match. If you can afford one through trading or guaranteed spins, prioritize it.

Q: What fruit should I use for PvP? A: The top PvP fruits are Kitsune, Fiend, Dragon, Quake, and Dough. Kitsune and Fiend are particularly dominant due to their versatility and high burst damage. However, skilled players can win with lower-tier fruits by mastering Observation Haki dodge timing and ability combos.

Q: How many fruits can I have at once? A: You can only eat and use one Devil Fruit at a time. However, you can collect and store multiple fruits in your inventory for trading or future use. The inventory limit is typically 5-10 fruits depending on your bag upgrades.

Q: Do Devil Fruits lose value over time? A: Fruit values fluctuate with game updates. When new fruits are added or existing fruits are buffed/nerfed, the trading market adjusts. Mythical fruits tend to hold their value best. Check the latest trading channels and community Discord for current prices before major trades.

Q: Can I awaken any fruit? A: In theory, any fruit can be awakened. However, awakening requires significant resources (Awakening Stones, Power Shards, Bloodline Stones), so it is only worthwhile for Epic-tier and above fruits. Awakening a Common or Uncommon fruit is a waste of rare materials.

Q: Where do fruits spawn on islands? A: Devil Fruits spawn under trees on most islands. They are small glowing objects that are easy to miss. Check the base of every tree on each island you visit. Fruits respawn approximately every 30-60 minutes per server.

Q: Is it better to buy fruits with Beli or Gems? A: Gems generally give better fruit odds. Premium spins (50 Gems) have significantly better Rare+ chances than Basic spins (15,000 Beli). If you have gems, use them for spins. Save Beli for fighting styles, swords, and other purchases.
