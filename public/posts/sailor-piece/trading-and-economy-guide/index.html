Why the Economy Matters in Sailor Piece#

In Sailor Piece, Beli (money) and Gems are the lifeblood of your progression. The best Devil Fruits cost hundreds of thousands of Beli or hundreds of Gems. Top-tier fighting styles, swords, and rerolls all require significant investment. Players who understand the economy progress 2-3 times faster than those who grind blindly.

For a complete ranking of every fruit with prices, awakening guides, and tier lists, see the Sailor Piece Devil Fruits Guide.

This guide covers everything from earning your first Beli to becoming a trading powerhouse with millions in the bank. Whether you want to buy the Dragon fruit, optimize your clan, or trade your way to exclusive cosmetics, this guide has you covered.

Trading Safety: How to Avoid Scams#

The trading system is the most common place for players to lose valuable items. Follow these rules to protect yourself.

Common Scam Tactics#

Scam TypeHow It WorksHow to Avoid
Fake Fruit TradeTrader offers a fruit that looks rare but is actually commonAlways read the full item name and rarity in the trade window
Quick-Switch ScamTrader adds a valuable item, then swaps it for a cheap item at the last secondReview the final trade offer carefully before confirming. Take your time.
Trust Trade Scam“Trade me your fruit first, I will trade you back double”Never trade first with strangers. Use the built-in trade system which requires mutual confirmation
Middleman Scam“Use this trusted middleman player” – the middleman steals both itemsOnly use officially recognized middlemen from the community Discord. Verify their identity
Distraction ScamTrader creates chaos (summons boss, causes lag) while swapping trade itemsCancel the trade if anything suspicious happens. Restart in a clean session
“Overpay” Bait“I will overpay for your fruit!” but the overpay items are worthlessEvaluate every item independently. Do not get excited by quantity – focus on quality

Safe Trading Checklist#

Before accepting any trade, verify:

CheckQuestion to Ask
Item NamesAre the items exactly what was advertised?
Item RarityIs the rarity what I expect? (Common, Uncommon, Rare, Epic, Legendary, Mythical)
Item CountIs the quantity correct?
Trade TermsDoes this trade benefit me? (trade up, never down)
Pressure TacticsIs the other player rushing me? If so, cancel and reconsider
Second AccountIs the player using a known scammer alt account? Check community ban lists

Reporting Scammers#

If you are scammed:

  1. Screenshot the trade if possible
  2. Report to the official Discord – Shadowrise Devs monitor the Discord for scam reports
  3. Share the scammer’s username on community forums to warn others
  4. Learn from the experience – understand what went wrong to avoid it in the future

FAQ#

Q: What is the fastest way to earn Beli? A: Boss farming is the fastest consistent method. At mid-game, farm Sukuna and Gojo for 200,000-500,000 Beli per hour. At late game, the full boss rush route earns 500,000-1,500,000+ Beli per cycle (47-70 minutes). Sea 2 bosses can earn 1,000,000-3,000,000+ Beli per hour.

Q: Should I spend Beli or Gems on fruit spins? A: Save Gems for Premium Gem Spins. They have significantly better rates for rare and Mythical fruits. Use Beli for fighting styles, swords, and basic needs first. Only use Beli for fruit spins if you have excess money after essential purchases.

Q: What fruit should I buy first? A: For early players, Snow (Epic, ~45,000 Beli) is the best value. Mid-game players should aim for Quake or Light (Legendary, 100,000-120,000 Beli). Endgame players should save for Dragon or Fiend (Mythical, 350,000-400,000+ Beli).

Q: How do I know if a trade is fair? A: Compare the trade against current market prices. Use the fruit rarity tiers (Mythical > Legendary > Epic > Rare > Uncommon > Common) as a baseline. Factor in awakening status, meta relevance, and scarcity. If you are getting less value than you are giving, decline the trade.

Q: Can I lose items in a trade? A: Only if you accept an unfair trade. The trading system requires both players to confirm, so you cannot be forced into a trade. However, scammers use psychological tactics (urgency, fake items, quick-switching) to trick you into accepting bad deals. Always verify before confirming.

Q: What is the best clan for making money? A: Devil clan (+42% DMG, +60% HP, 30% bonus drop rate) is the best overall. The damage boost makes boss farming faster, and the 30% bonus drop rate increases the value of every boss kill.

Q: Is Fishman race good for money farming? A: Fishman has +Income and +EXP gain, making it excellent for grinding enemies and quests for Beli. However, for boss farming, Galevorn (+75% DMG) is better because faster boss clears = more boss kills per hour = more total Beli.

Q: How do I get gems without spending Robux? A: The best free methods are: opening chests on every island (5-50 gems per chest), clearing dungeons (100+ gems per clear), defeating bosses (50-200 gems per boss), and redeeming codes (100-400 gems per code). High-level NPC grinding also drops gems.

Q: Should I trade fruits or keep them for myself? A: If the fruit is useful for your build and level, keep it. If you already have a better fruit or the trade offers significantly more value, trade it. Never trade a fruit you are actively using unless the replacement is clearly better.

Q: What items should I never trade away? A: Ancient Fragments and Map Pieces – you need 20 fragments and 7 map pieces to unlock Sea 2. Boss Keys for bosses you are actively farming. Reroll items if you have not yet achieved your target race and clan.
