What Is Sailor Piece?#

Sailor Piece is an open-world sea exploration RPG on Roblox developed by Shadowrise Devs. Released in November 2025, it has quickly become one of the most popular games on Roblox — regularly pulling in ~265K concurrent players.

Inspired by One Piece and other anime series like Jujutsu Kaisen and That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime, Sailor Piece puts you in a vast ocean world where you explore islands, fight powerful bosses, collect Devil Fruits, master Haki, and grow your character from level 0 to 16,000+.

Why play Sailor Piece?

  • Massive open world across two seas with 20+ islands
  • 50+ Devil Fruits with unique abilities and movesets
  • Deep progression system with stats, Haki, races, clans, and fighting styles
  • Challenging boss fights with millions to billions of HP
  • Trading system and social features
  • Frequent major updates (every 1-2 weeks)

Getting Started: Your First Session#

Spawn and First Steps#

When you first join Sailor Piece:

  1. You spawn on Starter Island (Level 0+)
  2. Talk to the Quest NPC — your first quest is to defeat 5 Thieves
  3. Defeat the Thief Boss (Level 25) as your second quest
  4. Visit the Haki Trainer — unlock Armament Haki (press G) immediately. This is essential for combat efficiency.
  5. Redeem active codes — go to Settings (top-right corner) and enter codes for free rewards

The Core Progression Loop#

Sailor Piece’s gameplay follows this loop:

  1. Accept quests from NPCs on your current island
  2. Grind enemies in the area for XP and Beli (money)
  3. Level up and allocate stat points into your chosen build
  4. Defeat the island boss to progress
  5. Travel to the next island for stronger enemies and better rewards
  6. Unlock new abilities (Haki, fighting styles, Devil Fruits)
  7. Repeat until you reach the level cap and unlock Sea 2

The #1 Mistake: Spreading Stats Evenly#

Do NOT put points into all 5 stats. This is the most common beginner mistake and will leave you mediocre at everything. Pick ONE damage path and commit.

Fruit Awakening#

Fruits can be awakened for stronger movesets and higher damage. This requires specific materials found in later seas (Sea 2+). Don’t worry about awakening in your early game — focus on getting a decent fruit first.

Fruit Main Tips#

  • If you’re a Fruit main, invest ONLY in Power and Defense
  • Never put points into Melee, Sword, or Gun if you’re using a fruit
  • Use a fruit that matches your current level — don’t save a top-tier fruit for later
  • Awakening dramatically increases fruit power — prioritize it once you reach Sea 2

Haki System — Core Combat Progression#

Haki is the most important combat system in Sailor Piece. There are three types:

For a complete guide to all three Haki types, fighting styles, and advanced combat techniques, see the Sailor Piece Haki and Combat Guide.

1. Armament Haki (Press G)#

What it does: Increases physical damage output and enables you to hit Logia-type enemies (elemental enemies that are normally immune to physical attacks).

How to unlock: Talk to the Haki Trainer on Starter Island.

Why it’s essential: Without Armament Haki, you cannot damage Logia-type enemies, which appear throughout the game. Always keep it active during combat.

2. Observation Haki (Press H)#

What it does: Grants auto-dodge/evasion chance. When active, you’ll automatically dodge some incoming attacks.

How to unlock: Progress through islands and talk to the Haki Trainer.

Why it’s important: Auto-dodge is crucial for surviving boss fights and PvP. Combine with manual Q-dodging for maximum evasion.

3. Conqueror’s Haki (Press J)#

What it does: Endgame Haki that provides a massive permanent damage multiplier.

Requirements to unlock:

  • Defeat 500 enemies with Armament Haki active
  • Dodge 500 hits with Observation Haki active
  • Defeat 200 bosses

Why it’s the ultimate goal: Conqueror’s Haki is the strongest Haki type. The requirements are grind-heavy but worth the effort.

How to Defeat Bosses Efficiently#

  1. Use mobility — Press Q to dash/dodge large AoE attacks
  2. Time your attacks — Strike during boss animation windups
  3. Use Z spec attacks — Strong burst damage combos
  4. Learn attack patterns — Focus on dodging first, damage second
  5. Use the right combo — Xenodok A + Demon Gate Spirit Stage 7 is cited as an effective boss-kill combo

FAQ#

Q: Is Sailor Piece free to play? A: Yes, Sailor Piece is completely free on Roblox. Premium currency (Gems) can be earned through gameplay or purchased with Robux.

Q: What’s the best Devil Fruit for beginners? A: For beginners, any fruit from the Rare or Epic tier (like Snow or Dark) is a good start. Don’t obsess over getting a Mythical fruit immediately — focus on leveling first. Once you have resources, aim for Dragon or Quake as your endgame fruit.

Q: What’s the best race? A: Galevorn is the best for combat (+80% HP, +75% DMG). Fishman is best for grinding/leveling (+Income, +EXP gain). Reroll at the Race Reroller NPC on Sailor Island.

Q: How do I unlock Sea 2? A: You need to reach Level 12,500+, complete Ascension V, collect all 20 Ancient Fragments (scattered across Sea 1), collect all 7 Map Pieces (boss drops), and talk to the Sea Traveler NPC on World Island.

Q: What are Haki and why do I need it? A: Haki is the core combat system. Armament Haki (G) increases damage and lets you hit Logia enemies. Observation Haki (H) gives auto-dodge. Conqueror’s Haki (J) provides a massive damage multiplier but requires grinding to unlock.

Q: How do I get gems fast? A: The fastest ways are: opening chests on every island, grinding high-level NPCs, clearing dungeons, redeeming codes (some give 100-400 gems), and defeating bosses for gem drops.

Q: What fighting style should I learn? A: Start with whatever you can afford. As you progress, aim for Sharkman Karate or Water Kung Fu for good damage and mobility. Endgame styles like Strongest in History have the highest damage scaling.

Q: How often does Sailor Piece get updates? A: Very frequently — major updates every 1-2 weeks. Recent updates include Sea 2 (April 2026) and the Anti-Magic update (April 2026). Check the official Discord or wiki for patch notes.

Q: Can I play Sailor Piece on mobile? A: Yes, Sailor Piece supports PC, Mac, iPhone, Android, Xbox One, PS4, and PS5 with cross-platform play.
