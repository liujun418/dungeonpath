What Is Dress To Impress?#

Dress To Impress (DTI) is one of the most viral Roblox games of 2024-2026, with hundreds of millions of visits and a massive community on TikTok and YouTube. It’s a fashion competition game where players style their avatars around random themes, walk the runway, and get voted on by other players.

In DTI, you:

  • Compete in themed fashion rounds — themes range from “Gothic” to “Date Night” to “Spongebob”
  • Mix and match hundreds of clothing items, hairstyles, accessories, and makeup options
  • Walk the runway and pose for other players
  • Vote on other players’ outfits (1-5 stars) — the highest average score wins
  • Unlock exclusive items through codes, events, and hidden collectibles
  • Practice in Freeplay mode to experiment with outfit combinations

This guide covers everything a brand-new player needs to go from their first round to consistently winning themed contests.

Getting Started: Your First Game#

Step 1: Join the Game#

Search for “Dress To Impress” on Roblox and join any available server. You’ll start in the main lobby area.

Step 2: Understand the Game Loop#

Each round follows this cycle:

PhaseDurationWhat You Do
Theme Reveal~5 secondsA random theme is announced — read it carefully
Dressing Phase~90 secondsSelect clothing, hair, accessories, and makeup to match the theme
Runway Walk~30 secondsWalk the runway in your outfit and strike poses
Voting Phase~30 secondsVote on every other player’s outfit (1-5 stars)
Results~10 secondsTop 3 outfits are displayed, winner announced

Step 3: The Dressing Room#

During the dressing phase, you have access to:

TopsShirts, blouses, jackets, dresses
BottomsPants, skirts, shorts
ShoesHeels, boots, sneakers, flats
AccessoriesJewelry, bags, hats, glasses
MakeupLipstick, eyeshadow, blush
PosesRunway poses for the catwalk

Step 4: Redeem Codes for Free Items#

In the dressing room, click the handbag icon with “CODE” in the bottom-left corner to enter active codes for free outfits and items.

How to Win: Dressing Strategies#

Strategy 1: Stay On Theme#

The single most important factor in winning is matching the theme clearly. Players vote higher for outfits that immediately communicate the theme.

ThemeWhat WorksWhat Doesn’t
GothicDark colors, layered accessories, dramatic silhouettesPastels, bright colors, casual wear
Date NightElegant dresses, heels, tasteful accessoriesOverly casual, sporty outfits
Deck of CardsRed/black/white color scheme, card-inspired accessoriesUnrelated colors and styles
Dark AcademiaBlazers, plaid, muted tones, bookish accessoriesNeon colors, sporty looks
CottagecoreFlowy dresses, floral patterns, natural accessoriesDark colors, urban styles

Strategy 2: Master Color Coordination#

A cohesive color palette is the difference between a 2-star and 5-star outfit.

Color SchemeHow to UseExample Themes
MonochromaticAll one color familyGothic, Dark Academia
ComplementaryOpposite colors on the wheelDeck of Cards, Dark or Light
PastelSoft, muted tonesCottagecore, Spring
BoldVivid, high-contrast colorsHarajuku, Heavy Metal

Strategy 3: Don’t Ignore Accessories#

Accessories are what separate good outfits from winning ones:

  • Jewelry — necklaces, earrings, bracelets add polish
  • Bags — should match the formality level of the outfit
  • Hats/Headpieces — can define a theme (e.g., Gothic crown for Gothic theme)
  • Glasses — add character to certain aesthetics

Strategy 4: Practice Your Pose#

Your runway pose matters. Players who strike confident, well-timed poses get more votes.

  • Walk to the end of the runway
  • Pause and pose for 2-3 seconds
  • Turn and walk back
  • Don’t rush — let other players see your outfit

Hidden Collectibles: Seashells & Treasure Chests#

For a complete guide on collectible locations, see our Seashells and Collectibles Guide.

Seashell Locations#

There are 3 seashells hidden in the main lobby. Collect all 3 and bring them to a secret room after a round ends to unlock the Seashell Crown — a rare and exclusive headpiece.

Seashell #Location Hint
1Near the entrance/lobby spawn area — look on the ground
2Behind or near the dressing room area
3Near the runway entrance or back of the lobby

How to Get the Seashell Crown:

  1. Collect all 3 seashells during the round
  2. Wait for the round to end
  3. Find the secret room (appears after round ends)
  4. Bring all seashells to the secret room
  5. Claim your Seashell Crown reward

Treasure Chests#

During special events like Scarlet’s Treasure Hunt, treasure chests appear in the lobby:

  • Each chest contains seashells (e.g., 50-200 seashells per chest)
  • Collect all chests for bonus rewards
  • Seashells can be used to purchase exclusive seashell-themed packs during summer updates

Game Modes#

Regular Mode#

The standard competitive mode. Players are matched into rounds with random themes. Vote on other players, earn the highest average score to win.

Freeplay Mode#

Freeplay lets you experiment with outfits without the pressure of competition.

How to Access Freeplay:

  1. Click the gear icon in the bottom-left corner
  2. Select “Free Play”
  3. Join the Freeplay server

What You Can Do in Freeplay:

  • Try unlimited outfit combinations
  • Practice runway poses
  • Test color palettes
  • Plan outfits for specific themes

Pro Tips for Winning#

Tip 1: Study Winning Outfits#

Pay attention to the top 3 outfits each round. What colors did they use? What accessories? Learn from the best players in your server.

Tip 2: Use Community Resources#

The DTI community on TikTok and YouTube is incredibly active. Search for “DTI outfit ideas” or “Dress To Impress [theme] tutorial” for inspiration.

Tip 3: Don’t Overcomplicate#

Sometimes the simplest, most theme-accurate outfit beats an over-styled one. A clean Gothic outfit with 3 well-chosen pieces will outvote a messy 10-piece outfit.

Tip 4: Vote Strategically#

You vote on every player. Be honest but understand that your votes affect who wins. High-quality voters (who vote consistently with the majority) may get matched in better lobbies. For a full breakdown of the voting system, see our Runway and Voting Guide.

Tip 5: Theme Tier List#

Not all themes are equal. According to the 2026 community consensus, Gothic is the most reliable theme for earning 5-star votes because the color palette and style are well-defined. Themes like “Dark or Light” are harder because they’re ambiguous.

Frequently Asked Questions#

Is Dress To Impress free?#

Yes, DTI is completely free on Roblox. Some exclusive items require codes or event participation, but the core game is free.

How many themes are there?#

As of May 2026, there are 515+ themes in the game, with new ones added regularly through updates.

How do I get free outfits?#

Redeem active codes using the handbag icon in the dressing room. Follow the official DTI social media for new code announcements.

What’s the best theme to win?#

Gothic is widely considered the most reliable theme. The dark color palette, layered accessories, and dramatic silhouettes are well-established and easy to execute well.

Can I play with friends?#

Yes, join the same server as your friends. You’ll compete against each other in the same rounds.

What’s Freeplay mode?#

Freeplay is a practice mode where you can experiment with outfits without the competitive timer or voting. Access it through the gear icon → Free Play.

How do I get the Seashell Crown?#

Find all 3 hidden seashells in the lobby, then bring them to the secret room that appears after a round ends.
