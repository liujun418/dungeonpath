What Is Legendary Island?#

Legendary Island is one of the most challenging islands in My Singing Monsters, featuring exclusive legendary-tier monsters that cannot be found on any other island. These monsters contribute unique instrumental and vocal tracks to the island’s song, and collecting them all unlocks the full musical composition.

The island was introduced as part of MSM’s ongoing content expansion and represents the pinnacle of breeding complexity. Legendary monsters require rare breeding combinations, significant resource investment, and patience.

Breeding Strategy for Legendary Island#

The Entbrat Strategy#

Entbrat is the cornerstone of legendary monster breeding. It carries all four natural elements (Plant, Cold, Air, Water), making it the universal breeding partner for ethereal monsters.

StepActionNotes
1Breed EntbratT-Rox + Potbelly = Entbrat (4-element monster)
2Level Entbrat to 15Higher level = slightly better breeding odds
3Pair with target parentE.g., Entbrat + T-Rox for Ghazt
4Use breeding structureThe Enhanced Breeding Structure increases odds by 5–10%
5RepeatExpect 50–100+ attempts for ethereal monsters

Step-by-Step Walkthrough#

Phase 1: Unlock Legendary Island (Days 1–3)#

  1. Reach Player Level 15 — Focus on completing objectives and breeding on your existing islands
  2. Build 3 prerequisite islands — Ensure you have at least Plant Island, Cold Island, and Air Island populated
  3. Accumulate 500–1,000 Diamonds — Save from daily rewards, achievements, and level-up bonuses
  4. Purchase Legendary Island — Tap the island menu and select Legendary Island

Phase 2: Build Core Infrastructure (Days 3–7)#

  1. Place the Enhanced Breeding Structure (150 Diamonds) — This is your first purchase
  2. Build 2–3 food structures — You will need significant food for legendary breeding
  3. Place decorations — Maximize happiness from day one
  4. Breed your first Entbrat — If you do not already have one at level 15

Phase 3: Begin Legendary Breeding (Days 7–30)#

  1. Start with Ghazt — It is the most commonly obtained legendary and serves as proof of concept
  2. Breed 50+ attempts — Expect failure; the 1–3% rate means you will fail many times
  3. Once Ghazt succeeds, target the next ethereal — Move through Grumpyre, Reebro, Jeeode, Humbug
  4. Level each legendary to 15 — Maximizes coin production and happiness bonus

Phase 4: Complete the Collection (Days 30–90)#

  1. Target Karbunkle and Whisp — These require 3-element combinations and have slightly better rates
  2. Work on Epic variants — These are the rarest and may take months of breeding
  3. Complete the song — Place all monsters and enjoy the full Legendary Island composition
  4. Claim all milestone rewards — Ensure you collect all diamond and item rewards at each milestone
