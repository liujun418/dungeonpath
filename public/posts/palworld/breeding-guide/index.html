How Breeding Works in Palworld#

Breeding allows you to produce eggs that hatch into specific Pals based on the parents’ species. It is one of the most important mechanics in the game, used to acquire rare Pals, transfer passive skills, and produce Pals with superior stats.

Requirements#

To start breeding, you need:

ItemHow to GetLevel Unlock

Breeding Pen Setup#

  1. Unlock the Breeding Pen blueprint at Technology level 19 (costs 2 Technology Points).
  2. Build it with: 100x Wood, 20x Stone, 50x Fiber, 10x Paldium Fragment.
  3. Place the pen on flat ground with a clear area around it (Pals need pathfinding room).
  4. Assign one male and one female Pal to the pen (throw them inside the pen area).
  5. Place Cake in the wooden box that appears inside the pen.
  6. Wait. The Pals will interact, consume one Cake, and produce an egg.
  7. Place the egg in an Egg Incubator (level 10 technology) to hatch.

The entire process takes approximately 2-5 minutes of real time from cake consumption to egg production, and then the egg incubation time depends on the Pal rarity:

Temperature Control#

Eggs need specific temperatures to incubate. You can achieve this with:

Hot temperatureBuild in desert/volcanic biome OR place Cooler nearby
Extreme cold/hotUse multiple heaters/coolers or build in extreme biomes

Cake Production Chain#

Cake is the only food that enables breeding. Each breeding attempt consumes one Cake. Crafting a single Cake requires:

IngredientSourceQuantity
FlourWheat Plantation -> Mill5
EggRanch (Chikipi) or wild Chikipi drops8
MilkRanch (Mozzarina) or wild Mozzarina drops7
Red BerryBerry Plantation or wild bushes2
HoneyRanch (Beegarde) or wild Beegarde drops2

Setting Up Cake Production#

  1. Build a Wheat Plantation (level 30) – produces wheat that gets milled into flour.
  2. Assign a Planting Pal and Watering Pal to the plantation.
  3. Build a Mill (level 15) – converts wheat to flour.
  4. Build a Ranch (level 12) and assign Chikipi (eggs) and Mozzarina (milk).
  5. Build a Bee Hive (level 28) or assign Beegarde to a ranch for honey.
  6. Use a Cooking Pot (level 17) to craft the Cake.

Pro tip: Jormuntide (Watering 4) or Jormuntide Ignis (Kindling 4) dramatically speeds up your cake production chain.

Breeding Combination Mechanics#

Palworld uses a specific formula to determine offspring. The offspring Pal is determined by the parents’ Paldeck numbers. The formula is:

(Parent A internal ID + Parent B internal ID) / 2 = Offspring internal ID

The result is rounded, and the Pal with the closest internal ID to that average becomes the offspring. If the result lands exactly between two Pals, the game chooses the one with the higher internal ID.

This means you can reliably predict offspring by knowing the parents. Here are the most important combinations.

How to Breed Specific Top Pals#

Breeding Anubis (S-Tier Handiwork)#

Anubis is arguably the most important Pal to breed early. With Handiwork level 4, it transforms your base’s production speed.

Easiest Anubis recipe:

StepParentsResultNotes
1Any male Relaxaurus + Any female DirehowlJormuntideCatching both is easy
2Male Jormuntide + Female Nitewing (or vice versa)AnubisThis produces Anubis directly

Alternative recipe (no breeding needed for parents):

Parent AParent BResultWhere to Catch Parents
RushoarNitewingAnubisRushoar: Forest, Nitewing: open sky anywhere
PenkingBushiAnubisPenking: Fort Ruins Alpha, Bushi: Volcano

Desired passive skills to breed onto Anubis:

  • Remarkable Craftsmanship (+50% work speed, +30% work speed from artisan too - actually Artisan is +50%, Work Slave is +30%)
  • Artisan (+50% Work Speed)
  • Work Slave (+30% Work Speed, -30% Attack)
  • Serious (+20% Work Speed)
  • Lucky (+15% Work Speed, +15% Attack)

Ideal Anubis passive set: Artisan + Work Slave + Serious + Lucky (or Remarkable Craftsmanship if available).

Passive Skill Inheritance#

Passive skills are inherited from parents to offspring. Understanding inheritance is key to breeding Pals with perfect skills.

Inheritance Rules#

RuleDescription
Random poolOffspring gets skills from a pool created from both parents’ skills
4-skill limitEach Pal can have at most 4 passive skills
Inheritance rateApproximately 60-70% chance to inherit each individual passive skill from parents
New skillsOffspring can very rarely roll random new skills
Blank PalsUsing Pals with no passives increases the chance of blank offspring

Breeding for Perfect Passives: Step-by-Step#

Goal: Create an Anubis with Artisan + Work Slave + Serious + Lucky

  1. Step 1 – Acquire base Pals: Catch multiple Anubis (via breeding or catching). Look for ones with at least one of your desired passives.

  2. Step 2 – Consolidate passives: Breed Anubis with other Anubis to combine passives. Example: Anubis (Artisan only) x Anubis (Work Slave only) = offspring with a chance of having both.

  3. Step 3 – Add more passives: Continue breeding offspring with skill consolidation. Use the Pal Essence Condenser to improve Pal stats while breeding.

  4. Step 4 – Two-skill consolidation: Once you have an Anubis with Artisan+Work Slave and another with Serious+Lucky, breed them together.

  5. Step 5 – Patience: The odds of all four passives landing on one offspring are roughly 10-15% per egg. Prepare to hatch 10-20 eggs.

Fusion Breeding (Pal Essence Condenser)#

The Pal Essence Condenser (level 22 technology) allows you to upgrade a Pal’s partner skill to higher star levels. This is separate from breeding but directly complements it.

Star LevelPals RequiredStat BonusPartner Skill Boost
1 Star4 of same species+5% ATK/DEFIncreases effectiveness
2 Stars16 total (12 more)+10% ATK/DEFSignificant boost
3 Stars32 total (16 more)+15% ATK/DEFMajor boost
4 Stars64 total (32 more)+25% ATK/DEFMaximum boost

Breeding produces large quantities of the same Pal, which can then be condensed. This is the primary reason to mass-breed common Pals like Lamball or Cattiva – condensing them into a 4-star version creates a significantly more powerful Pal.

Advanced Breeding Strategies#

Strategy 1: The Anubis Factory#

  1. Breed two Anubis parents (any passives).
  2. Set up 3-4 Breeding Pens with Anubis pairs.
  3. Automate cake production with a dedicated base section (wheat farm + mill + ranch + beegarde).
  4. Mass-produce Anubis eggs.
  5. Use excess Anubis for Pal Essence Condenser upgrades.
  6. Keep breeding until perfect passives appear.

Strategy 2: Passive Skill Libraries#

Build a collection of Pals with individual high-value passives. Keep them organized in your Palbox. When you want to breed a specific Pal, pair your target breeder with the Pal that carries the needed passive, then breed the offspring back together until passives consolidate.

Strategy 3: Legendary Passive Breeding#

Legendary Pals (Frostallion, Jetragon, Paladius, Necromus) come with the Legend passive inherently. You can breed this passive onto other Pals by:

  1. Catching a male Frostallion (has Legend passive).
  2. Breeding it with a female of another species.
  3. Some offspring will inherit the Legend passive.
  4. Continue breeding offspring to transfer Legend to other species.

This is how you create combat Pals with Legend + Ferocious + Musclehead + Celestial Emperor for maximum damage.

Strategy 4: Base Worker Mass Production#

For a fully automated base with 15 Pals, you ideally want:

  • 1x Anubis (Handiwork 4, perfectionist passives)
  • 1x Jormuntide (Watering 4)
  • 1x Jormuntide Ignis (Kindling 4)
  • 1x Astegon (Mining 4)
  • 1x Wumpo (Transport 4)
  • 1x Lyleen (Planting + Medicine)
  • 2x Frostallion (Cooling 4 if needed for food preservation)

Breed each of these once with passives, then level them up naturally.
