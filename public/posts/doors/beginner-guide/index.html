What Is DOORS?#

DOORS is a multiplayer roguelike horror game on Roblox developed by LSPLASH in collaboration with Redibles. Since launching in 2022, it has become one of the most iconic horror games on the platform — inspiring countless YouTube videos, TikTok clips, and a passionate community.

Inspired by games like Spooky’s Jump Scare Mansion and Spelunky, DOORS puts you in a procedurally generated hotel (and later, mines) where you must navigate through 100+ numbered doors, survive hostile entities, solve puzzles, and reach the end alive.

Why play DOORS?

  • Tense, atmospheric horror with clever entity design
  • Procedurally generated runs — every playthrough is unique
  • Deep entity roster with unique warning signs and survival mechanics
  • Puzzle-solving (code-breaking, memory games, item hunts)
  • Multiplayer co-op — revive teammates and share resources
  • Multiple floors and game modes for endless replayability
  • 87+ achievements to collect

Getting Started: Your First Session#

How to Start#

  1. Launch DOORS from Roblox and enter the Lobby
  2. Join the LSPLASH group for a free badge (and formerly a free revive)
  3. Select The Hotel from the elevator — this is where you’ll start
  4. Play solo or invite friends — multiplayer makes survival easier
  5. Redeem codes immediately — open the Lobby Shop and enter codes for free Knobs

All Hotel Entities#

For a detailed bestiary covering every entity’s behavior, spawn conditions, and survival strategies, see our DOORS Entities Bestiary.

Rush#

WarningLights flicker/dim
SurvivalHide in nearest locker or behind a bed immediately
Danger★★

Rush is the first entity you’ll encounter and the most common. When lights flicker, don’t panic — find the closest hiding spot and get in. The key is speed, not perfection.

Ambush#

WarningLights flicker for LONGER than Rush
SurvivalHide in locker; it bounces back MULTIPLE times — stay hidden until all passes end
Danger★★★

The entity that kills most beginners. Unlike Rush, Ambush doesn’t just pass once — it returns several times. Players who peek between passes get caught. Stay hidden until you’re absolutely sure it’s gone.

Screech#

WarningWhispering sound in dark rooms
SurvivalQuickly look around (move your mouse/camera) to scare it away
Danger★

Simple encounter — if you hear whispering in a dark room, spin your camera rapidly. Don’t overthink it.

Seek#

WarningCutscene triggers at Door 50
SurvivalRun the chase; follow glowing arrows and hands for direction; jump over fallen furniture
Danger★★★

Seek triggers a long hallway chase sequence. The key is to follow the glowing guidance (arrows, floating hands) and jump over obstacles. Don’t try to fight back — just run.

Figure (Door 50 & Door 100)#

WarningHeavy breathing sound; you hear it approaching
SurvivalCrouch-walk to stay quiet; hide in closets when it’s close; hold breath (hold Space)
Danger★★★★

Figure is the most complex entity in The Hotel. There are two encounters:

Door 50 — The Library: You need to find books with shape symbols, translate them to numbers using a chart, and enter the code to unlock the door. Figure hunts you during this process.

Door 100 — The Electrical Room: Final boss. Circuit breaker memory puzzle — a pattern of switches is briefly shown, and you need to replicate it. Memorize and match the pattern.

Key tips for Figure:

  • Always crouch-walk when Figure is active — running makes noise
  • Hide in closets when you hear it approaching
  • Hold Space to hold your breath when Figure is very close

Dupe#

WarningMultiple doors in one room with different numbers
SurvivalOnly enter the door with the correct number matching the room count
Danger★★

Simple but deadly. Count which door you should be on, then only enter that door. Wrong doors = damage.

Halt#

WarningGlitching visuals in long hallway
SurvivalRun BACKWARD the way you came, then forward again
Danger★★

Counterintuitive mechanic — you need to go backward first. Many players die because they try to push forward.

Eyes#

WarningFloating eyeballs visible in rooms
SurvivalWalk around them; touching causes damage
Danger★

Snare#

WarningDistorted door texture (subtle visual cue)
SurvivalDon’t walk through doors with suspicious textures
Danger★★

Puzzle Solutions#

Library Safe Code (Door 50)#

  1. Enter The Library and collect scattered books
  2. Each book contains shape symbols that correspond to numbers
  3. Find the door with a code lock and reference chart
  4. Translate shapes → numbers using the chart
  5. Enter the number combination to unlock

Pro tip: Camp before the library for 3-4 minutes so Guiding Light spawns and guides you through the puzzle.

Circuit Breaker (Door 100)#

  1. A pattern of switches is briefly displayed in a box
  2. Empty square = leave switch OFF
  3. Filled square = turn switch ON (match the number shown)
  4. Memorize and replicate the pattern

In Super Hard Mode, the puzzle is more challenging but follows the same rules. Practice the memory technique — chunk the pattern into groups of 2-3.

Generator Rooms (The Mines)#

  1. Find Fuses scattered in nearby rooms
  2. Install them in the generator to power it and open the next door
  3. If you have a Crowbar (3% spawn), you can skip these entirely

Minecart Track Switching (The Mines)#

  1. Use minecarts to break through wooden barricades
  2. Adjust the rail lever/dial to switch tracks toward the correct path
  3. Speedrun technique: send the cart the right way without changing the dial on Door 101

The Nest Anchors (Door 200)#

  1. Activate 5-6 Anchors around the area
  2. Figure hunts by sound — crouch-walk everywhere
  3. Place Alarm Clocks away from your position to distract Figure
  4. Be strategic about movement order — plan your route before starting

FAQ#

Q: Is DOORS free to play? A: Yes, DOORS is completely free on Roblox.

Q: How many floors are there? A: There are multiple floors: The Hotel (Doors 1-100), The Mines (Doors 101-200), The Outdoors (subfloor), The Backdoor (secret ~25 rooms), The Rooms (subfloor, being revamped as The Archives), Rush Mode, and Endless Mode.

Q: What’s the hardest entity in DOORS? A: Figure at Door 200 (The Nest) is widely considered the hardest. It hunts by sound in a large area while you need to activate 5-6 Anchors under pressure. The Door 100 circuit breaker puzzle is also notoriously difficult for beginners.

Q: How do I unlock The Mines? A: You can access The Mines from the Lobby with the Rock Bottom badge, or after completing The Hotel.

Q: What are Knobs and how do I get them? A: Knobs are the permanent out-of-run currency. Earn them through codes (redeem in Lobby Shop), achievements, and gameplay. Use them for permanent upgrades and Lobby Shop items.

Q: Can I play DOORS with friends? A: Yes! DOORS supports multiplayer. Playing with friends makes it easier to survive, share items, and revive teammates.

Q: What’s Rush Mode? A: Rush Mode is a permanent game mode where all entities look and sound like Rush. Figure and Seek are significantly faster, and there’s a new shooting mechanic. It’s chaotic and fast-paced.

Q: What’s Endless Mode? A: Endless Mode features a single looping room that continues indefinitely with no set endpoint. It tests endurance and consistency.

Q: How do I get the best codes rewards? A: The code 777 gives 777 Knobs + Stardust + 1 Revival — it’s the most valuable. Redeem it ASAP as codes may expire at any time.
