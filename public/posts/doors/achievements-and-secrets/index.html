DOORS Secrets Overview#

DOORS is packed with hidden content that goes far beyond simply reaching the exit. The game features 87+ achievements/badges, secret rooms accessible only through specific actions, developer easter eggs, hidden lore, and challenge modes that test your mastery of the game.

This guide covers every known secret, achievement, and hidden element in DOORS. Whether you are a completionist trying to 100% the game or a curious explorer wanting to know what lies behind the hidden doors, this is your definitive reference.

SECRET ROOMS AND HOW TO ACCESS THEM#

The Library Secret (Door 50)#

The Library at Door 50 contains more than just the Figure encounter and code puzzle.

How to Find the Secret:

StepAction
1Enter The Library at Door 50
2Before starting the book hunt, explore the perimeter of the room
3Look for a hidden alcove or bookshelf that appears slightly different
4Interact with the suspicious bookshelf to reveal a hidden area
5Inside, you may find extra loot, lore items, or a rare entity encounter

What You Find:

  • Hidden containers with rare items
  • Possible lore fragments about the Hotel’s history
  • A chance encounter with rare entities

The Electrical Room Secrets (Door 100)#

The Electrical Room at Door 100 has hidden elements beyond the circuit breaker puzzle.

Secrets to Find:

SecretHow to AccessReward
Hidden PanelAfter solving the circuit breaker, search the walls for a hidden panelLore item or rare container
Developer NoteExamine the electrical panels closely; some contain developer messagesEaster egg text
Extra LootSearch behind the circuit breaker boxAdditional gold or items

Pro Tip: Take your time in the Electrical Room after solving the puzzle. Figure is gone once the door opens, so you can safely search for hidden items.

The Green House#

A secret room with a garden theme, hidden somewhere in The Hotel.

How to Find It:

StepAction
1Play through The Hotel normally
2Look for rooms that have unusual plant decorations or a green-tinted door
3The Green House may appear randomly between certain door ranges
4Enter when you spot it — it does not appear in every run

What You Find:

  • Themed room with plant decorations
  • Possible Green Herb (used for Skull Lock in the Infirmary)
  • Easter egg references to other LSPLASH games
  • Rare item containers

The Painting Room#

A room filled with paintings, some of which contain hidden secrets.

How to Find It:

StepAction
1Progress through The Hotel
2Look for a room with multiple paintings on the walls
3The room may appear between Doors 30-60
4Examine each painting carefully

Secrets in the Painting Room:

The Adit (The Mines Secret Area)#

The Adit is the entry point to The Mines and contains its own secrets.

How to Access:

StepAction
1Enter The Mines from the Lobby or after completing The Hotel
2The Adit (Door 101) is the first area
3Search thoroughly before starting the generator sequence
4Hidden containers and lore items are scattered around

What You Find:

  • Initial Figure encounter (blind, less aggressive)
  • Hidden Fuses for the generator
  • Lore about The Mines’ history
  • Rare item containers

FAQ#

Q: How many achievements are there in DOORS? A: There are 87+ achievements/badges in DOORS as of May 2026. New achievements are added with each major update.

Q: What is the hardest achievement to get? A: The Perfectionist achievement (complete The Hotel without taking any damage) is widely considered the hardest. It requires flawless execution of every entity encounter.

Q: How do I find the secret Backdoor? A: The Backdoor is a secret room that appears under specific conditions. The exact trigger is still being explored by the community, but it is believed to involve specific door sequences and camping behaviors.

Q: Are there any achievements that require multiplayer? A: Some achievements are easier with multiplayer (like reviving teammates), but most can be completed solo. The Solo Survivor achievement specifically requires playing alone.

Q: What is the Library secret? A: The Library at Door 50 contains a hidden alcove or room behind certain bookshelves. It may contain rare items, lore fragments, or hidden entity encounters.

Q: Can I get achievements in Rush Mode? A: Rush Mode has its own set of achievements, including Rush Mode Clear. Some general achievements also apply to Rush Mode runs.

Q: What achievements were added with The Outdoors? A: The Outdoors added achievements including Touched Grass, Down The Water Spout, Do’h!, Feed The Birds, Picking Flowers, Detour, and Rebirth.

Q: How do I get the “Pls Donate” achievement? A: Tip Jeff a total of 100g across multiple donations. This means donating 10g ten times (or equivalent) at Jeff’s Shop.

Q: Is there a 100% completion guide? A: This guide covers all known achievements and secrets. For the most up-to-date completion checklist, check the DOORS Wiki Achievements page.

Q: Will The Archives update add new achievements? A: Yes, The Archives update (expected May/June 2026) will add a significant number of new achievements tied to the revamped Rooms subfloor content.
