TDS Tower Tier List Overview#

Tower Defense Simulator has dozens of towers, and the 2026 balance update shifted the meta significantly. This tier list ranks every tower based on overall performance across Normal, Hardcore, and Fallen modes.

Quick Reference: Tier Definitions#

Tier Meaning
S+ Game-defining. Essential for any serious strategy
S Top-tier. Core of most winning loadouts
A Strong. Viable in all modes with good placement
B Situational. Good in specific maps or compositions
C Niche. Only useful in easy modes or early game
D Avoid. Outperformed by almost every other option

S+ Tier: Game-Defining Towers#

Tower Type Cost Why It’s S+
Accelerator Damage High Highest DPS in the game. No tower comes close to its raw damage output. Essential for every late-game composition. Placement limit makes it even more valuable.
Commander Buffer ~2,500 The best buffer in TDS. A max-level Commander increases nearby tower damage and fire rate dramatically. Every serious loadout runs at least one.
Golden Pyromancer Damage/AoE Event Top golden tower with infinite burn times, exceptional range, and massive crowd control. Even after burn immunity became more common, its raw DPS keeps it relevant.

S Tier: Core Meta Towers#

Tower Type Cost Why It’s S
Electroshocker Damage/Stun ~2,000 Often preferred over Golden Pyro now that many enemies have burn immunity. Stuns groups, deals consistent damage, and has excellent area denial.
Golden Scout Damage Event Best early-to-mid game tower post-2026 update. Fast fire rate, scales well with upgrades, and transitions smoothly into late game.
Golden Cowboy Damage Event Consistent high DPS throughout the entire game. No range issues, reliable damage output, and excellent value.
Rocketeer Damage High Massive AoE damage. The placement limit of 5 is its only real drawback. When you can place them, they melt boss waves.
DJ Buffer Medium Second-best buffer after Commander. Increases fire rate for nearby towers. Often paired with Commander for maximum buff stacking.

A Tier: Strong and Reliable#

Tower Type Cost Why It’s A
Pyromancer Damage/AoE ~1,500 Excellent crowd control with burn damage. Less effective against burn-immune enemies, but still a staple for most compositions.
Mortar Damage/AoE ~2,000 Great cluster damage for grouped enemies. Recently nerfed in the 2026 balance update but still strong with good early game presence.
Freezer Utility ~1,800 Slows enemies significantly. Essential for Hardcore mode where enemy speed can overwhelm your defenses. Less needed in Normal mode.
Golden Crook Boss Damage Event Strong damage with good range. Solid choice for any composition.
Golden Minigunner Damage Event High fire rate, good DPS. Slightly outclassed by Golden Cowboy but still very viable.
Golden Soldier Damage Event Reliable damage dealer with decent range and fire rate.
Ace Pilot Damage Shop Excellent early game DPS post-2026 buff. Good range for a starter tower.

B Tier: Situational but Useful#

Tower Type Cost Notes
Sniper Damage Medium Highest single-shot damage, but slow fire rate. Good against bosses, poor against groups.
War Machine Damage/AoE High Strong late-game tower with good AoE. Expensive to upgrade fully.
Brawler Damage Shop Strong close-range damage, affordable. Good early game but falls off late.
Assassin Damage Shop High single-target damage. Great against bosses but limited AoE.
Electrician Buffer Medium Alternative buffer tower. Less impactful than Commander/DJ but still useful.
Supply Drop Economy Medium Generates coins over time. Good if you need extra economy but outperformed by Farm in most situations.
Hunter Damage Medium Good damage against single targets. Decent for mid-game.

C Tier: Early Game Only#

Tower Type Cost Notes
Scout Damage Free Your starting tower. Replace ASAP with Golden Scout or Ace Pilot.
Farm Economy Low Generates passive income. Useful but doesn’t contribute to damage.
Gunner Damage Low Decent early damage, completely outclassed mid-game.
Pursuer Damage Medium Average stats across the board. Nothing special.
Demoman Damage/AoE Medium Explosive damage but unreliable targeting.
Ranger Damage Medium Decent range, mediocre damage.

D Tier: Avoid#

Tower Type Cost Why It’s D
Melee Damage Free Shortest range in the game. Enemies often bypass before it can attack.
Paintballer Damage Low Outclassed by every other damage tower.
Turret Damage Low Very slow fire rate, low damage. Early game only.

The 2026 balance update significantly shifted the tower meta:

Ace Pilot Buffed Moved from B to A tier. Better early game presence

Tips for Maximizing Tower Performance#

  1. Placement matters more than tier — A well-placed B-tier tower can outperform a poorly placed S-tier tower
  2. Buffer towers multiply everything — A Commander buffing two Accelerators is better than three Accelerators without a buffer
  3. Upgrade in stages — Don’t max out one tower before placing others. Balance your economy across all towers
  4. Know the enemy path — Study the map before placing towers. Inside corners always get more uptime
  5. Save for late game — Don’t spend all your coins early. Accelerator and Rocketeer need significant investment

Frequently Asked Questions#

What’s the best tower in TDS right now?#

Accelerator has the highest raw DPS and is essential for late-game compositions. Golden Pyromancer is the best golden tower overall.

What towers should I use as a beginner?#

Start with Golden Scout (or Scout if you don’t have it), add Pyromancer for AoE, and a Commander for buffering. This combo works on every Normal map.

Is Mortar still good after the nerf?#

Yes. Mortar dropped from S to A tier but still has excellent cluster damage and early game presence. It’s not the best AoE anymore, but it’s still very viable.

How do I get Golden towers?#

Golden towers are obtained through limited-time events, codes, and special promotions. Follow the official TDS Discord for event announcements.

Should I use Freezer in Normal mode?#

Freezer is optional in Normal mode but essential for Hardcore and Fallen. If you’re struggling with fast enemy waves, add one.
