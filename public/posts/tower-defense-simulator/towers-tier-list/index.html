TDS Tower Tier List Overview#

Tower Defense Simulator has dozens of towers, and the 2026 balance update shifted the meta significantly. This tier list ranks every tower based on overall performance across Normal, Hardcore, and Fallen modes.

For detailed map-by-map strategies with tower placement guides, see the TDS Best Maps Guide. For advanced tactics and tower combos, see the TDS Strategy Guide.

Tips for Maximizing Tower Performance#

  1. Placement matters more than tier — A well-placed B-tier tower can outperform a poorly placed S-tier tower
  2. Buffer towers multiply everything — A Commander buffing two Accelerators is better than three Accelerators without a buffer
  3. Upgrade in stages — Don’t max out one tower before placing others. Balance your economy across all towers
  4. Know the enemy path — Study the map before placing towers. Inside corners always get more uptime
  5. Save for late game — Don’t spend all your coins early. Accelerator and Rocketeer need significant investment

Frequently Asked Questions#

What’s the best tower in TDS right now?#

Accelerator has the highest raw DPS and is essential for late-game compositions. Golden Pyromancer is the best golden tower overall.

What towers should I use as a beginner?#

Start with Golden Scout (or Scout if you don’t have it), add Pyromancer for AoE, and a Commander for buffering. This combo works on every Normal map.

Is Mortar still good after the nerf?#

Yes. Mortar dropped from S to A tier but still has excellent cluster damage and early game presence. It’s not the best AoE anymore, but it’s still very viable.

How do I get Golden towers?#

Golden towers are obtained through limited-time events, codes, and special promotions. Follow the official TDS Discord for event announcements.

Should I use Freezer in Normal mode?#

Freezer is optional in Normal mode but essential for Hardcore and Fallen. If you’re struggling with fast enemy waves, add one.
