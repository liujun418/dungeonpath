1. What is Pressure?#

Pressure is a horror-survival game on Roblox set in the Hadal Blacksite, an underwater research facility. You navigate through dark corridors, avoid terrifying creatures, and try to survive as long as possible.

Why play Pressure?

  • Intense horror experience with genuinely scary moments
  • Deep lore and story hidden throughout the facility
  • Regular updates (latest: “Half A Slice Of Cake” - March 2026)
  • Great for fans of Doors looking for something new

2. Getting Started#

Step 1: Understand the Goal#

Your objective is simple: survive and progress through the facility. Each door you open leads to deeper, more dangerous areas.

Step 2: Learn the Controls#

  • WASD or Joystick: Move
  • Mouse/Touch: Look around and interact
  • Shift: Sprint (use wisely — stamina is limited)
  • E: Interact with objects

Step 3: The First Few Doors#

Don’t rush. The early doors are your tutorial:

  • Learn to listen for creature sounds
  • Hide in lockers when you hear danger approaching
  • Pick up key items when you see them

3. Survival Tips#

Tip Why It Matters
Listen carefully Most creatures give audio cues before appearing
Don’t sprint unnecessarily Stamina is limited and you need it for escapes
Memorize locker locations Your safe spots save your life
Play with friends Co-op is easier and more fun
Check for updates New content changes strategies

4. Monster Overview#

Monster Danger Level How to Survive
Ambush High Hide immediately when lights flicker
Rush Medium Get to a hiding spot ASAP
Snare Medium Watch for traps on the floor

Note: Pressure received a massive update in March 2026 (“Half A Slice Of Cake”) adding 200+ item skins, 260+ charms, and 50+ modifiers. This guide reflects the post-update game.

5. FAQ#

Q: Is Pressure free to play? A: Yes, free on Roblox.

Q: Is it too scary for younger players? A: Pressure is genuinely scary. It’s rated for older Roblox players. Parents should be aware.

Q: Can I play solo or should I team up? A: Both work. Solo is more intense, co-op is more forgiving.

Q: How is Pressure different from Doors? A: Similar concept, but Pressure has its own unique monsters, atmosphere, and progression system. Think of it as a spiritual successor with its own identity.
