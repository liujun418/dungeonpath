Overview of Hidden Content in Pressure#

Pressure is a deep-sea horror survival game set in the underwater facility P.I.A. (Pressure Induced Apparatus). While the main gameplay follows a linear floor-by-floor progression through increasingly dangerous areas, the game contains dozens of hidden rooms, secret entity encounters, and Easter eggs that most players never discover on their first run.

These hidden areas often contain rare items, lore fragments, or unique entity encounters that can significantly impact your survival chances. Some are intentionally placed by developers; others are community-discovered glitches that have become part of the game’s culture.

Tips for Finding Hidden Areas#

  • Pay attention to wall textures: Hidden doors and passages often have slightly different textures than surrounding walls. Look for discoloration, cracks, or panels that don’t quite match.
  • Listen for audio cues: Many hidden areas have faint ambient sounds – dripping water, humming machinery, or whispers. Use headphones for the best chance of detecting these.
  • Explore during calm periods: Entities are less likely to interrupt your exploration during non-chase periods. The best time to search for secrets is immediately after an entity encounter when there’s a brief safe window.
  • Check above and below: Many hidden areas are accessed by looking up (ceilings with loose grates) or down (cracked floor panels). Don’t just look at eye level.
  • Take notes: The codes and clues for hidden areas are scattered across multiple floors. Audio logs, computer terminals, and environmental clues all contribute to solving the puzzles.
  • Use community resources: The Pressure community on Discord and Reddit regularly shares new discoveries. Some secrets require multiple players to trigger.
