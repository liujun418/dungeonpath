Races Overview#

Blox Fruits features 7 playable races, each with four upgrade versions (V1 through V4). Your starting race is randomly assigned among Human, Rabbit, Shark, and Angel. Ghoul, Cyborg, and Draco must be unlocked through quests.

Each race offers unique abilities that dramatically impact your combat style, mobility, and survivability. V4 (Race Awakening) is the endgame upgrade system, unlocking powerful transformations and gear choices.

V4 — Temple of Time (Third Sea)#

Prerequisites:

  1. Race V3 unlocked
  2. Defeat rip_indra (Raid Boss)
  3. Defeat Dough King (get Mirror Fractal)
  4. Find Blue Gear on Mirage Island (nighttime only — use race ability while staring at the moon)

Awakening Process:

  1. Go to the Temple of Time and pull the lever
  2. Gather 2 other players with different races
  3. Activate all three race abilities together during a Full Moon
  4. Complete the race trial (defeat waves of enemies)
  5. Place your gear in the Ancient Clock

Cost: ~26,750 Fragments per full race awakening. Total for all 6 base races: 160,500 Fragments.

Controls: Press T for V3 ability, Y for V4 transformation.

Detailed Race Ability Breakdowns#

Human — The Damage Dealer#

VersionAbilities
V1No buffs
V2+Movement speed, +Instinct dodge regen, +Dash distance, +Flash Step range
V3 — Last ResortDamage increases the lower your HP drops. High-risk, high-reward for clutch PvP moments
V4 Gear: Limit BreakRage meter builds during combat, uncapped to 150% at Tier 2 — progressively boosts all damage
V4 Gear: PsychoFlash Step gains 3 charges, temporary invisibility and speed on use. Tier 2: faster regen, longer dashes

Best for: Aggressive players who want maximum damage output. Pairs well with combo-heavy fruits like Dough and Spirit.

Rabbit (Mink) — The Speed Demon#

VersionAbilities
V1+1.5x movement speed
V2+2x speed, +Dash distance, reduced energy cost for dashes
V3 — Agility+4x movement speed, massively reduced dash cooldown
V4 Gear: Whirlwind (Blue)Dashes leave behind damaging tornadoes that trap enemies
V4 Gear: Lightning Cloak (Red)Dashes become significantly longer with electricity. Tier 2: Super Dash by holding dash button

Best for: Hit-and-run tactics, kiting, escaping unfavorable fights. Weakest V4 in terms of raw combat power — relies on outmaneuvering opponents.

Shark — The Tank#

VersionAbilities
V1Reduced water damage as a fruit user
V2Zero water damage, faster swimming
V3 — Water BodyReduces all incoming damage by 65-70% temporarily
V4 Gear: Leviathan’s ArmourWater shield that regenerates when you deal damage, absorbs ~85% damage
V4 Gear: WhirlpoolHits apply water debuff — slows enemies and reduces their defense, stacks

Best for: PvP tanking. Nearly unkillable in water. S-Tier V4 for beginners learning PvP and veterans who want survivability. Lacks land mobility.

Angel — The Healer#

VersionAbilities
V1+Extra air jumps
V2+More air jumps, reduced air jump energy cost
V3 — Heavenly BloodHeals 14-20% HP, +15% defense, regenerates energy. Low cooldown
V4 Gear: Prince of the SkiesHold dash to glide, hold jump to fly freely. Tier 2: greatly enhanced flight
V4 Gear: King’s RuleLight aura that slows, deals damage over time, drains energy, and distorts enemy screen

Best for: Sky camping and healing. Excellent with Portal fruit. King’s Rule aura can stun-lock enemies in combos.

Ghoul — The All-Rounder#

VersionAbilities
V1+30% movement speed at night
V2Life Leech — heal 25% of all fighting style damage dealt
V3 — Heightened Senses+Damage, +Speed, +Defense, and 40% cooldown reduction for 8 seconds
V4 Gear: Domain ExpansionDark field inflicting slowness, health regen negation, and blindness. Night passives active during day
V4 Gear: Blood SiphonLife leech works on ALL attacks. ~10% on players, ~5% on NPCs. Tier 2: ~15% on players

Best for: Aggressive, sustained combat. Pairs perfectly with Sanguine Art. Arguably the best overall V4 for the cooldown reduction + life leech combination.

Cyborg — The Instinct Breaker#

VersionAbilities
V1No buffs
V2+Defense vs melee/sword/gun, converts 15% damage taken into energy
V3 — Energy CoreAoE field with tick damage, +30% defense, breaks enemy Instinct (Observation Haki)
V4 Gear: Energy ControlDamage chains to nearby enemies via electric orbs. Super Jump and Super Dash
V4 Gear: AftershockAttacks apply electrifying effect that disables enemy Instinct

Best for: PvP dominance. The electric DoT prevents enemies from charging moves. S-Tier in competitive PvP.

Draco — The Dragon (Newest)#

VersionAbilities
V1+5% Health, +5% Melee Damage, +5% Damage Reduction. Cosmetic: small horns
V2+Speed, +Dash, Fiery Flight (fly ~5 seconds), Flash Step causes burn/explosion damage
V3 — Primordial ReignBuffs you + allies by 15% damage/speed, debuffs enemies 15% speed, cancels enemy skills in range. 10s duration
V4 Gear: Cometic FieldHits attach explosive orbs — 3-4 stacks detonate for massive damage (~5K+), breaks Instinct. Allies also apply orbs. Damage scales with bounty
V4 Gear: Thorn MailReflects ~45% of incoming damage back at attackers. Reflected damage breaks Instinct

Best for: Late-game powerhouse. Team support + massive DPS. Can 3-shot high-bounty players. S-Tier alongside Shark.

Pro Tips#

  • Race version progress is saved per race — switching away and back preserves your V2/V3/V4
  • Ghoul and Draco can be re-obtained for free from Experimic/Dragon Wizard
  • Cyborg costs 2,500 Fragments to switch back via the secret NPC
  • V4 meter fills by dealing/taking damage from players and Sea Beasts (not regular NPCs)
  • Cursed Dual Katana puzzle room and Chocolate Land are the best spots for fast V4 training
  • Full V4 awakening (all upgrades) requires ~90 minutes of transformation time
