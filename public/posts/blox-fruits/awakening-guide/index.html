What Is Fruit Awakening in Blox Fruits?#

Fruit awakening is one of the most powerful progression systems in Blox Fruits. When you awaken a Devil Fruit, each of its moves (M1, Z, X, C, V, and sometimes F) gains enhanced properties: increased damage, larger hitboxes, additional effects, and sometimes entirely new mechanics. An awakened fruit can easily double your combat effectiveness.

However, awakening requires completing challenging raids, gathering rare materials, and spending significant resources. This guide covers everything you need to know: how to start raids, what materials each fruit needs, the best awakened builds, and strategies for completing raids efficiently.

If you are just starting out, our Leveling Guide will help you reach the required level for raids. For information on which fruits are worth awakening, check our Fruits Tier List.

How to Unlock Fruit Awakening#

Prerequisites#

Before you can awaken any fruit, you must meet these requirements:

RequirementDetails
LevelMinimum level 1500 (Third Sea access)
LocationCastle on the Sea in the Third Sea
Quest GiverMysterious Scientist NPC inside the Castle
Starting Cost5,000 Beli to begin your first raid

Step-by-Step: Starting Your First Awakening#

  1. Reach Level 1500 – Complete quests and grind in the Second Sea until you hit the level requirement
  2. Travel to the Third Sea – Use the boat mechanic from the Second Sea to cross to the Third Sea
  3. Find the Castle on the Sea – Located in the floating island area. Look for the large castle structure
  4. Talk to the Mysterious Scientist – Found inside the castle. This NPC sells raid quests
  5. Purchase a Raid Chip – Costs 5,000 Beli for the basic raid. Higher difficulty raids cost more
  6. Use the Raid Chip at the Door – A glowing door appears nearby. Interact with it to enter the raid instance
  7. Complete the Raid – Defeat all waves of enemies and the final boss within the time limit
  8. Collect Awakening Materials – Drop from the final boss. Materials vary by fruit type
  9. Return to the Mysterious Scientist – Trade materials to awaken specific fruit moves

Solo Raid Strategy#

Completing raids solo is challenging but very rewarding. Here is the optimal approach:

Recommended Fruit for Solo Raids: Buddha (awakened)

AspectStrategy
FruitBuddha – massive HP pool, melee range, and high damage make raids significantly easier
SwordRengoku or Dark Dagger – high damage output for boss phases
Fighting StyleGodhuman – best combo potential and damage in the game
AccessoriesPaladin Ring (defense) + Dragon Scale Tank (HP boost)

Wave-by-Wave Solo Strategy:

  1. Wave 1: Use Buddha’s Z and X moves to clear mobs quickly. Stay in transformed form.
  2. Wave 2: Focus one elite enemy at a time. Use Godhuman combos (C -> X -> Z) for burst damage.
  3. Wave 3: Kill the mini-boss first, then clean up reinforcements. Save your V move for the boss.
  4. Wave 4 (Final Boss): This is the hardest part. Stay at medium range, dodge the boss’s AoE attacks, and use your V move when the boss’s HP drops below 30% for a quick finish.

  • Coordinate V move timing – stacking multiple ultimate abilities on the final boss can skip entire phases
  • Use Portal’s teleport ability to quickly reposition players who get knocked down

Frequently Asked Questions#

Q: Can I awaken multiple fruits? A: Yes, but each fruit requires its own set of materials. Focus on one fruit at a time to avoid spreading your resources too thin.

Q: Do awakened moves work with the unawakened fruit? A: No. You must have the specific fruit equipped and the corresponding move awakened to use the enhanced version.

Q: What happens if I switch fruits after awakening? A: Your awakening progress is saved to that specific fruit. If you switch to a different fruit, you need to awaken it separately. If you switch back, all awakened moves are still available.

Q: Is it better to raid solo or with a team? A: For material farming, team raids are significantly faster (3-4x more materials per hour). For learning raid mechanics, start solo on Normal difficulty.

Q: Can Universal Fragments replace any material? A: Yes, Universal Fragments can substitute for any specific material. However, they have a very low drop rate (1-2 per Expert raid), so do not rely on them as your primary farming method.

Q: Which fruit should I awaken first? A: If you focus on PvP, awaken Leopard first. For PvE and raiding, awaken Buddha first. For a balanced approach, Dragon is the best starting point. See our Fruits Tier List for detailed rankings.
