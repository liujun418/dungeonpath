Current Active Codes#

Status: No Active Public Codes (May 2026)#

As of May 2026, there are no active public promo codes for Pet Simulator 99. BIG Games has shifted their reward strategy toward:

  • Event-based rewards — earn free pets, gems, and boosts by participating in limited-time events
  • Merchandise codes — one-time-use codes included with official BIG Games merchandise (plushies, collectibles) purchased from shop.biggames.io/codes
  • Milestone rewards — automatic bonuses given to all players when the game reaches certain visit counts

Note: This situation is not uncommon for Roblox games. Many games transition from frequent public codes to event-driven rewards as they mature.

How to Redeem Codes#

When codes are available, here is how to redeem them:

  1. Open Pet Simulator 99 in Roblox
  2. Click the Twitter/bird icon in the game lobby (usually on the left side of the screen)
  3. Enter the code exactly as shown in the text box (codes are case-sensitive)
  4. Press Redeem — rewards are delivered instantly to your inventory

Merchandise Codes#

BIG Games sells official merchandise at shop.biggames.io/codes that includes one-time-use codes. These codes typically reward:

  • Exclusive pets (often Huge or Titanic variants)
  • Large gem bundles
  • Special cosmetics or auras

Merchandise codes are the only working codes as of May 2026. They require purchasing physical products but offer significantly better rewards than traditional promo codes.
