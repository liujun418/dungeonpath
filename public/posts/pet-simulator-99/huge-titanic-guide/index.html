Huge Pets: The First Major Milestone#

Base Drop Rate#

The standard drop rate for a Huge pet from any egg is approximately 1 in 2,000,000 (0.00005%). This sounds impossibly low, but there are multiple ways to boost your odds.

Current Huge Rotation Pets#

Every 2 days, specific Huge pets get boosted hatch rates. The rotation always includes pets obtainable from the current best egg. Check the in-game banner for the current selection.

Best Methods for Your First Huge Pet#

Method 1: Huge Rotation (Free, Recommended)

  1. Check which Huge pets are in the current rotation
  2. Go to the area that drops eggs for those pets
  3. Hatch eggs with Huge Hunter and Active Huge Overload enchants
  4. Be patient — even with boosts, this can take many hatches

Method 2: Prison Keys (Reliable)

  1. Unlock Prison World via the Void World cannon
  2. Collect or trade for Prison Keys
  3. Break cells with Prison Keys for 10x Huge chance
  4. Each cell break gives you a shot at a Huge Prison pet

Method 3: Events (Best Value)

Events frequently offer the best free-to-play Huge pet opportunities. The current Void RNG Event (Update 78) features boosted rates for multiple Huge pets through the RNG egg mechanic.

Frequently Asked Questions#

What is the drop rate for Huge pets?#

Base rate is approximately 1 in 2,000,000 without boosts. With Huge Hunter enchants, Prison Keys, and event bonuses, this can improve significantly.

What is the difference between Huge, Titanic, and Gargantuan?#

Huge pets are the first exclusive tier with boosted stats. Titanic pets are 2x stronger and ridable. Gargantuan pets are 3x the size of Titanic and the ultimate power tier.

How do I get a Titanic pet?#

Through exclusive eggs, event eggs, Titanic Gifts (crafted in Combine-O-Matic for ~500K+ diamonds), RNG eggs, trading, or Robux packs. Events are the most reliable free method.

What is the Huge Rotation?#

A system that changes every 2 days, giving boosted hatch rates for specific Huge pets from the current best egg. Check the in-game banner to see what is currently featured.
